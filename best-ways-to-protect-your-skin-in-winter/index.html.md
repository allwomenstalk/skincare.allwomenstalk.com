# The Best Ways to Protect Your Skin during the Winter ...

What are the **best ways to protect your skin in winter**? It is important to protect your skin during the winter in order to prevent dry and rough skin. If you want to keep your skin smooth and nourishing this season, these are the **best ways to protect your skin in winter**.

## 1. Moisturize

Moisturizing your skin will help prevent dryness and is one of the **best ways to protect your skin in winter**. Remember to always moisturize immediately after you take a shower because it will help to keep your skin smooth all day long.

## 2. Protect

Don’t forget to wear gloves and a scarf before going outside in the cold because this will keep your skin warm. Also, sunscreen is vital for protecting your skin from the sun because it can further damage your skin.

## 3. Exfoliate

Exfoliating is great for removing dead skin cells and keeping your skin smooth. Find an exfoliating mask that you can use for your face, body, and lips to keep them nourished.

## 4. Humidifier

Humidifiers get moisture into the air which is great for your skin and prevents it from drying out. You can also add a few drops of your favorite essential oil to add a wonderful scent to your home.
