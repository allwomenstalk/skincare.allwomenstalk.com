# What Should You Know about Melting Cleansers ...

There’s always something new in the world of skincare and right now melting cleansers are gaining in popularity. As with any skincare product, it’s always a good idea to learn more about it before you give it a try. If you have specific skin issues, like eczema or dryness, you should discuss new products with your dermatologist to ensure that it’s the right choice for you. In the meantime, trying a melting cleanser is something that will change your life, so here’s what you need to know before you buy.

## 1. They Transform in Your Hands, Aka, Melting

Melting cleansers come in several formats, but the basic idea is the same. They start out sort of solid, but melt as you use them. The product is designed to give you a really good cleanse so that your skin is free of dirt, debris and bacteria that can lead to breakouts and other unpleasant skin issues. If you want to try something new, I totally encourage you to give a melting cleanser a try. It’s going to change the way you see facial cleansers, and that’s a good thing!

## 2. Some Are for Use in a Dry Face

Never washed your face dry? You aren’t the only one! Many melting cleansers work by applying them directly to a dry face. After gently massaging the product into your skin, you wet your face to finish the job. Sounds strange, doesn’t it? Don’t let that stop you though because melting cleansers get rave reviews from many testers.

## 3. The Cleansers Are Formulated to Remove Makeup

Melting cleansers are a great option if you wear makeup on a regular basis. Because they are designed to remove makeup, they are an especially good choice for removing all the products you wear. That includes eye shadow, liner, blush, bronzer, mascara and foundation. If you don’t get all that makeup off at the end of the day, it can leave you with breakouts. Melting cleansers are also great if you don’t wear makeup, so give one a try today!

## 4. Many Melting Cleansers Are Infused with Vitamins and Minerals

One of the great things about skincare products is that many of them pump your skin full of nutrients that promote healthy and supple skin. For example, some of them contain vitamin C, which is an antioxidant that can help fight skin cancer and the signs of aging, while also brightening and smoothing your skin. The bottom line is that melting cleansers can help you improve the health of your facial skin.

## 5. It’s Recommended to Use a Melting Cleanser Twice a Day

Many skincare experts suggest washing your face morning and night and most melting cleansers are ideal for twice daily use. Use the cleanser before applying your sunscreen, moisturizer and makeup in the morning. Then, use it again before bed to wash it all off before you go to sleep. It’s as easy as that!

## 6. The Products Are Great for Several Skin Types

Melting cleansers are especially recommended for dry and normal skin types. You might be able to use one if you have sensitive skin, but make sure you read the labels first so that you choose one that doesn’t have any fragrances or other harsh ingredients.

## 7. You Might Feel like the Cleanser Isn’t Washing off

One final note on facial cleansers. Some testers said that it doesn’t feel like the cleanser is rinsing, but that once you finish and pat your face dry, it won’t feel like that anymore. In fact, most reviewers say that a melting cleanser leaves them with hydrated and smooth skin.
