Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best skincare products for glowing skin?
Look for products with vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, and antioxidants to brighten and hydrate your skin.
How often should I use a moisturizer?
Apply moisturizer twice daily, morning and night, to keep your skin hydrated and smooth.
Are natural skincare products better for sensitive skin?
Natural products can be gentler, but always patch test to ensure they suit your skin type.
What ingredients should I avoid in skincare?
Avoid harsh sulfates, parabens, and synthetic fragrances if you have sensitive or acne-prone skin.
Can anti-aging products work for women in their 20s?
Yes, early use of products with retinol and antioxidants can help delay signs of aging.
Is sunscreen really necessary every day?
Absolutely—using SPF daily protects your skin from UV damage and premature aging.
How do I choose the right cleanser for my skin type?
Opt for gentle, non-stripping cleansers for dry or sensitive skin, and foaming ones for oily skin.
Can I use the same skincare products year-round?
You might need richer moisturizers in winter and lighter formulas in summer to suit seasonal changes.
How important is exfoliation in skincare?
Exfoliating 1-2 times a week helps remove dead skin cells for a smoother, brighter complexion.
Are expensive skincare products always better?
Not necessarily—effective skincare depends on ingredients, not price, so choose products that suit your skin needs.
