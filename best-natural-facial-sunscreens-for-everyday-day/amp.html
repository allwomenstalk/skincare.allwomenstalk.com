13 Best Natural Facial Sunscreens for Everyday Day ...

Pratima Neem Rose Face Sunscreen SPF 30 • BareMinerals SPF 30 Natural Sunscreen • Coola Mineral Face SPF 30 Matte Cucumber • Josie Maran Argan Daily Moisturizer SPF 40+ • Lavanila the Healthy Sunscreen SPF 40 Face Cream • More ...

By Lisa

There are countless sunscreens on the market, but finding one that’s suitable for our facial skin can be quite challenging. We don’t want to use something thick and goopy and run the risk of clogging our pores. But at the same time, we want to make sure we’re properly protecting the skin on our face. Well ladies, you can rest easy knowing we did our homework and found some amazing facial sunscreens that are strong yet sensitive. Take a peek below!

1. Pratima Neem Rose Face Sunscreen SPF 30

The first that I noticed when I saw this facial sunscreen was the chic packaging. If you’re like me, you’re used to sunscreen that’s packaged in obnoxious colors or come in the standard tube. Pratima’s sunscreen stands out in more ways than one as it’s an all-natural formula that blocks UVA, UVB and UVC rays while keeping skin clear.

Price: $24.00 at pratimaskincare.com

2. BareMinerals SPF 30 Natural Sunscreen

If your skin loathes liquid facial sunscreens, give this mineral powder sunscreen a go. It’s weightless, easy-to-apply and it even comes in a tinted formula so you need not worry about that annoying white cast that many sunscreens leave behind. Another plus about using a powder sunscreen is that reapplying it over makeup is a breeze!

Price: $28.00 at sephora.com

3. Coola Mineral Face SPF 30 Matte Cucumber

Stay cool as a cucumber while you’re outdoors with Coola’s matte finish sunscreen. Even though facial sunscreen is a matte formula, it actually works great on all skin types, even the most sensitive babes! This sunscreen is also formulated with lots of skin saving ingredients like Vitamin C, Evening Primrose Oil, Rosehip Oil and Omega-3 acids for radiant skin.

Price: $36.00 at coolasuncare.com

4. Josie Maran Argan Daily Moisturizer SPF 40+

Josie Maran got us all on the Argan Oil bandwagon and this is another Argan-infused product that’s sure to be a hit. This natural facial sunscreen is free of chemicals and physically deflects harmful UVA rays, which is one of the top contributors to premature aging. It also shields skin from UVB rays and the Argan Oil helps moisturize and repair the skin from the outdoors. This facial sunscreen is mild, lightweight and makes for an excellent makeup primer so you get a lot of bang for your buck!

Price: $14.00 at sephora.com

5. Lavanila the Healthy Sunscreen SPF 40 Face Cream

All-natural facial sunscreens don’t get much better than this. Lavanila’s Healthy Sunscreen is a moisturizing sunscreen that’s chemical and residue-free. This lightweight cream is enriched with antioxidants, amino acids and natural botanicals for the healthiest sunscreen you can get your hands and face on!

Price: $28.00 at sephora.com

6. Suntegrity 5 in 1 Natural Moisturizing Face Sunscreen, Broad Spectrum SPF 30

Now here’s a sunscreen that works overtime! This tube might appear small, but it treats, hydrates, protects, primes and covers the skin. Oh, and did I mention that it’s tinted? This facial sunscreen comes in five different shades and in addition to its superb protecting and perfecting qualities, it contains anti-inflammatory ingredients, making it perfect for skin with rosacea, acne or melasma.

Price: $45.00 at suntegrityskincare.com

7. CoTZ Face Sunscreen for Natural Skin Tones, SPF 40

Here’s another multi-tasking facial sunscreen that is sure to impress you. CoTZ Face sunscreen’s tinted cream blends seamlessly into a matte finish, provides broad spectrum protection, helps prevent uneven skin tones and is totally free of chemicals. This gentle, weightless cream allows us to have our fun in the sun without the worry of breakouts or burns.

Price: $17.00 at cotzskincare.com

8. Juice Beauty SPF 30 Oil Free Moisturizer

Beauties with oily or blemish-prone skin will adore Juice’s oil-free moisturizer with SPF 30. The weightless hydrator harnesses the power of fruit extracts, lemon balm and vitamins to promote healthy skin renewal, protect the skin and nourish it with organic ingredients. Heal and protect your skin with the best of what nature has to offer.

Price: $29.00 at juicebeauty.com

9. Kiss My Face Face Factor Sunscreen, SPF 30

Kiss your face with antioxidants, Hydresia oleosomes, green tea and licorice to fight free radicals, soothe and protect your skin from the sun. This gluten-free formula nourishes skin without sulphates, parabens or phthalates, so you’re naturally helping your skin fight sun damage without any unnecessary or harmful ingredients.

Price: $12.95 at kissmyface.com

10. John Masters Organics Natural Mineral SPF 30

This natural facial sunscreen combines green tea, jojoba oil and shea butter to hydrate, revitalize and replenish skin. Along with broad spectrum coverage, this mineral sunscreen protects all skin types from the sun and wind. Made with certified organic ingredients, this weightless sunscreen is perfect to layer under makeup and use every day.

Price: $32.00 at johnmasters.com

11. Jason Facial Sunscreen Broad Spectrum SPF 20

This weightless, oil-free facial sunscreen is perfect worn under makeup or alone. Formulated with green tea and grapeseed extracts, it protects skin while nourishing it with natural ingredients. This will be your go-to natural sunscreen to keep fines lines at bay without clogging pores or disturbing makeup.

Price: $12.39 at jason-personalcare.com

12. AmorePacific Natural Protector SPF 30/PA+++

You might balk at the price tag of this next sunscreen, but it really is pretty amazing! Think of this as a skin care product and not just another sunscreen. This sunscreen is actually formulated to keep skin shine-free and clear. It also absorbs excess oil, balances moisture and keeps skin healthy and youthful.

Price: $65.00 at sephora.com

13. Boscia Oil-Free Hydration SPF 15

For a facial sunscreen that protects as well as it perfects your complexion, pick up Boscia’s oil-free hydrator. This little wonder not only protects your skin from the sun, but it also shields skin from environmental stresses and pollution. Fine lines and pores are diminished while offering powerful hydration.

Price: $36.00 at sephora.com

There are lots of great options and these formulations are incredible. I have never been so impressed with the offerings of sunscreens that all go above and beyond their call of duty. Which facial sunscreen will you choose?
