## 1. Mask with Cucumber and Clay

There are only a few ingredients in this easy mask and you might even have them lying around your house already. This mask works on both oily and combination skin so you’re sure to love the results.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon cucumber juice
1 egg white
A few drops lemon juice
½ teaspoon clay

Directions:

Combine all of the ingredients to create a paste. Smooth the mixture over your face and let it sit for 10 to 20 minutes before rinsing.

Source: [bestnaturaltips.com](https://bestnaturaltips.com/20-homemade-face-masks-for-oily-skin/2)

## 2. Banana Face Mask

This mask works by sweeping away grease from your skin without drying it out. You probably have everything you need right in your kitchen.

Ingredients:

1 banana
1 tablespoon honey
Few drops lemon juice

Directions:

Mash the banana very well, then mix in the honey. Add a few drops of lemon juice and stir to combine. Apply the mixture to your face and wait 15 minutes before washing it off.

Source: [naturalbeautytips.co](https://naturalbeautytips.co/best-homemade-face-masks-for-oily-skin/)

## 3. Simple Egg White Mask

When you want something totally simple, this mask will do it for you. With only two ingredients, it’s really easy to throw together and you will love how to whisks away oil and leaves you with a perfect matte complexion.

Ingredients:

1 egg white
Juice from ½ lemon

Directions:

Beat the egg white until it gets frothy, then add the lemon juice. Massage the mixture into your skin and wait 30 minutes. Then rinse.

Source: _homemademasks.net_

## 4. Orange and Aloe Vera Mask

The citrus in oranges is great for getting rid of oily skin and the aloe vera calms the skin without leaving it looking greasy. You will love how much your skin looks when you put this mask on your face.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon orange juice, freshly squeezed
1 teaspoon aloe vera gel

Directions:

Stir together the ingredients and spread the mixture on your face. Let the mask soak in for 15 minutes, then rinse.

Source: _fashionising.com_

## 5. Try Strawberries on Your Face

You don’t see strawberries in facial masks very often, but they have great astringent properties, which makes them perfect for oily skin.

Ingredients:

6 strawberries
3 tablespoons honey
2 egg whites
1 tablespoon lemon juice

Directions:

Mash the strawberries into a pulp, then add the lemon juice, honey and egg white. Mix well. Spread the mixture over your face and wait 10 minutes, then rinse.

Source: _fashionising.com_

## 6. You’ll Love Apricot and Yogurt

This mask combines the perfect blend of ingredients that will help counteract oil and prevent it from making you look greasy. It’s easy to make and you’ll love the way it smells.

Ingredients:

1 apricot
1 tablespoon yogurt
½ teaspoon clay

Directions:

Peel and mash the apricot in your food processor. Add the yogurt and clay and mix them into a paste. Apply the mask to your skin and let it sit for 20 minutes. Rinse with warm water.

Source: [bestnaturaltips.com](https://bestnaturaltips.com/20-homemade-face-masks-for-oily-skin/3)

## 7. Gentle Oatmeal Mask

Oatmeal is great for your skin for many reasons. Not only does it soothe the skin, but it also acts as a sponge to soak up oil and remove it from your face.

Ingredients:

1 egg yolk
1 tablespoon honey
Oatmeal

Directions:

Combine the yolk and honey. Add enough oatmeal to create a paste. Spread it over your skin, wait 15 minutes, then rinse.

Source: [sheknows.com](https://www.sheknows.com/living/articles/822977/homemade-face-masks-for-oily-skin/)

## 8. Vegan Chocolate Face Mask

If you want something totally indulgent, you will love this mask. It smells wonderful and will easily soak up excess oil. Just don’t be tempted to eat it!

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon [unsweetened cocoa powder](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/best-brands-of-cocoa-that-every-baker-should-use/)
3 tablespoons clay
Few drops full-fat coconut milk

Directions:

Mix the ingredients well in a small bowl. Apply the mask and let it sit for 20 minutes. Rinse.
