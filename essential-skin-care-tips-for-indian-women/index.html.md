# 7 Essential Skin Care Tips for Indian Women ...

Here at All Women Stalk, we love to hear from our readers and one reader asked for skin care tips for Indian women, so here is your post! Thanks for reading everyone! When I started writing this post, I realized just how important it was because truthfully, there are no skin care tips for Indian women all gathered together in one place! If you are an Indian woman and are dying to know what skin care tips for Indian women you should be using, check out my tips below!

## 1. Moisturizer with SPF

Indian skin tones typically burn way easier than any other skin tones, so make sure that you are moisturizing with a moisturizer that has SPF in it. Truthfully, this could benefit every single skin tone out there! The higher the SPF, the better, as you want to make sure that you are protecting your skin! So, my first skin care tip for Indian women would be to invest in an SPF moisturizer!

## 2. Exfoliate

Because Indian skin is typically a little bit more oily than most, you have to exfoliate. You could use something like a sugar scrub to exfoliate or you could use a cleanser with exfoliating agents inside of it, it is all your choice! This skin care tip for Indian women will keep your skin looking beautiful and fab all the time!

## 3. Lemon Juice & Honey for Oily Skin

If you really want some natural skin care tips for Indian women, why not try a honey and lemon juice facial? This mask can actually draw out the oil in your skin and can truly make sure that your skin looks really, really pretty constantly. It'll also make sure that your skin is healthy and glowing!

## 4. Honey, Oatmeal & Egg Yolks for Dry Skin

If you have dry skin, another skin care tip for Indian women is a honey, oatmeal and egg yolk mask. Whisk it all together, place it on your face and pat it in. After you leave it on for about 15 to 20 minutes, you can wash it off with warm water and your skin should be more hydrated and beautiful looking!

## 5. Know Your Skin Type

When you are looking for skin care tips for Indian women, you want to make sure that you know your skin type. This means you should know if your skin is oily, if it is dry or if it is a combination of the two. That way, you'll know the best way to treat your skin and how to make it look beautiful!

## 6. Cleanse Twice a Day

A lot of Indian women feel the need to cleanse their skin twice. This can be good and bad. For me, I cleanse once, but I wash my makeup off with just warm water. If you do cleanse twice, make sure that you are applying a moisturizer twice, as you want to make sure that your skin stays super hydrated and still looks beautiful at all times!

## 7. Avoid Abrasive Cleansers

Finally girls, the last skin care tip for Indian women that we're going to talk about is avoiding super abrasive cleansers. You don't want something that has a ton of chemicals or something that is going to be super harsh and hard on your skin. Just get a cleanser that is very light, airy and will clean your skin, not make it blotchy and hard!

So readers, there you have it! All of my skin care tips for Indian women that really do work! Do you have any other skin care tips for Indian women that I may have missed? Give 'em up in the comments!
