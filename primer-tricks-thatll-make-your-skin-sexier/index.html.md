# 7 Primer Tricks 🃏 That'll Make Your Skin Sexier ...

Don't underestimate the importance of primer. According to _Allure_, it can turn your skin from drab to fab. That's why you should keep some in your bathroom cabinet, so you can try out these beauty tricks:

## 1. TRY a TINTED FORMULA

![TRY a TINTED FORMULA](https://img.allw.mn/content/d8/ap/n6p3iyhw565dca21f0f7a403586247_570x321.gif)

If your skin is dull, try using a light pink primer to add a glow to your skin. Meanwhile, lilac primers can brighten sallow skin, and green primers can neutralize redness.

## 2. LESS is MORE

![LESS is MORE](https://img.allw.mn/content/pp/l3/im2im4l3565dc9ee29b8a699526057_467x350.gif)

Only place a tiny drop of primer on your finger. Then massage it all over your face.

## 3. BOOST YOUR LASHES

![BOOST YOUR LASHES](https://img.allw.mn/content/du/zv/gn5gbfcn565dc9ee90f1c863982927_500x213.gif)

There's more than one type of primer. You can buy an eyelash primer that will condition lashes. All you have to do is apply it before your mascara.

## 4. GO EASY on ACNE

![GO EASY on ACNE](https://img.allw.mn/content/sc/y8/e1q9f5fp565dc9eee107e394341314_480x270.gif)

Instead of using products meant to reduce acne, you can buy a primer with anti-acne ingredients. After all, the less products you use on your skin, the better. You don't want to irritate it instead of help it.

## 5. PRIME YOUR LIPS

![face, lip, cheek, eyebrow, nose,](https://img.allw.mn/content/lw/b4/md9xzb2n58905991f21df142103558_500x200.gif)

If you put primer on your lips, it'll prevent your lipstick from feathering. You don't have to buy a special product for your lips. Your face primer will work just as well.

## 6. TREAT YOUR EYES

![TREAT YOUR EYES](https://img.allw.mn/content/xr/o2/v0jss9ib565dc9f5a263b464961137_570x321.gif)

Make sure you dab some primer around your eyes. Why? Because it will "prevent makeup from smudging or creasing by absorbing oils, soften crow's-feet, and counteract redness."

## 7. SMOOTH YOUR DÉCOLLETAGE

![SMOOTH YOUR DÉCOLLETAGE](https://img.allw.mn/content/ul/o5/qgyiug6k565dc9ef9f7c5276906342_262x180.gif)

You can put primer on more than just your face. Try rubbing a bit onto your chest or shoulders to make your skin look younger and softer.
