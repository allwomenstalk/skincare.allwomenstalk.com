# I tested every method for picking the best shower gels for women here’s what worked

If you're anything like me, you're always looking for **good shower gels** — there are always new and delicious ones to try! From formulas designed to wake you up to those which create a luxurious, sweet-smelling lather, these are some of the **best shower gels** to try this season. Finding **the best shower gel** for your needs can completely transform your [body care routine](https://skincare.allw.mn/body-care-basics) and leave you feeling pampered. Before we dive into the favorites, here are a few things I look for in a great wash:

- Uplifting or relaxing scents that last.
- Gentle formulas for sensitive skin.
- Great value for money.

## 1. Imperial Leather Foamburst Sweet Vanilla and Cherry Blossom

![Imperial Leather Foamburst Sweet Vanilla and Cherry Blossom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12145836_4811_599x570.jpg)

This super-yummy shower gel is magical! It comes out of the can as a clear gel and provides masses of sweet vanilla and cherry-scented foam for a luxurious shower. Its lather is so rich it can even double up as shaving foam, so it's two products in one! I think this is one of the **best shower gels** on the market right now.

## 2. Philosophy Melon Daiquiri

![Philosophy Melon Daiquiri](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12145858_2923_599x558.jpg)

This scrumptious shower gel can also be used as shampoo and bubble bath, leaving your hair and body with a fresh melon scent. It's my go-to scent when I need a mood boost during my [morning rituals](https://skincare.allw.mn/morning-rituals)! You can explore more scents at the official [Philosophy](https://www.philosophy.com) website.

## 3. Philosophy Senorita Margarita

![Philosophy Senorita Margarita](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12150549_4567_599x555.jpg)

For the freshness of a margarita with lime in your shower, Senorita Margarita is one of the **top shower gels** out there! It wakes you up with its zingy lime scent which lingers all day, leaving you feeling fresh and energetic. If you want the **best shower gels for women** that pack a citrus punch, this is it.

## 4. Palmolive Milk and Honey

![Palmolive Milk and Honey](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12150800_5896_599x594.jpg)

This simple yet luxurious shower gel is one of my favorites; I always have a bottle on the bathroom shelf. Its blend of milk and honey soothes and moisturizes skin and leaves you smelling sweet — kind of like a milkshake! It never irritates my skin either, which is something I appreciate in **good shower gels**.

## 5. Nivea Powerfruit Relax

![Nivea Powerfruit Relax](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12150830_8828_599x785.jpg)

Mmmm antioxidants, blueberry and acai berry means this shower gel smells great, and it cleanses your skin gently. It always leaves me feeling fresh and awake. You can check out their full range of products at [Nivea](https://www.nivea.com).

## 6. Radox Shower Cranberry and Blackcurrant Smoothie

![Radox Shower Cranberry and Blackcurrant Smoothie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12150913_4949_599x929.jpg)

What could be better than a smoothie for your shower? With real cranberry seeds for a burst of fruity goodness, juicy blackberries and calming chamomile, this really is like washing all over with a fruit smoothie. It smells amazing and I find it leaves my skin really soft too, which is an added bonus for my [daily skincare](https://skincare.allw.mn/daily-skincare) routine!

## 7. Origins Calm to Your Senses Body Cleanser

![Origins Calm to Your Senses Body Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12150938_6613_599x478.jpg)

This perhaps isn't a shower gel you would use every day because it is quite expensive, but I was lucky enough to get a little bottle as part of a gift set. It blends relaxing lavender with soothing, sweet vanilla and coconut oil. It smells absolutely amazing and leaves your skin so soft, plus it creates a rich, luxurious lather. This is one of my favorite treats for the shower, and a little goes a long way! Visit [Origins](https://www.origins.com) to see their full collection.

## 8. Radox Nourish Shower Cream

![Radox Nourish Shower Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12151056_1640_599x568.jpg)

This is a great, moisturizing shower cream if your skin is feeling dry and in need of some pampering. I love its zingy ginger scent that's spicy and uplifting, and the natural shea butter helps to soothe and protect dry skin so it will stay soft all season long.

## 9. Dove Go Fresh Revive Body Wash

![Dove Go Fresh Revive Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/12151123_2583_599x505.jpg)

I have the Dove Go Fresh deodorant with pomegranate and am addicted to the scent, so when I saw this body wash with pomegranate and lemon verbena, I had to try it out! It didn't let me down and left my skin feeling smooth and nourished, with no irritation. It's a fantastic **good shower gel** for those lazy mornings when you need waking up, as the scent is really fresh and pretty! You can find it at [Dove](https://www.dove.com).

So there you have it, 9 shower gels that are perfect for your morning shower (or a shower at any time of day really). You could even use some of these as bubble bath, particularly the Philosophy ones which are designed to be multi-tasking, so there is no need to buy separate products! I love to have a couple of shower gels to choose from so I can pick one to suit my mood. What is your all-time favorite shower gel?
