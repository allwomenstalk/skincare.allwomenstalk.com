## 1. Where Skin Science Meets Ritual: The Ultimate S Enriched Cleansing Foam

![](https://img.allw.mn/aad07935324d20242f70b9dac77cee_1024x1024.jpg)

Skincare, in the Sulwhasoo universe, is not merely routine - it is a **reverent ritual**. And with the debut of [The Ultimate S Enriched Cleansing Foam](https://us.sulwhasoo.com/collections/korean-cleanser/products/the-ultimate-s-enriched-cleansing-foam?variant=43908027744301), cleansing becomes its own act of transformation. This isn’t your everyday face wash. It’s a luxurious, sensorial experience that elevates the simple gesture of cleansing into a moment of artful care.

At its core is **Sulwhasoo’s pioneering HydroLeo Technology™**, an innovation inspired by the French mille-feuille pastry. Just as the dessert is composed of delicately stacked layers of cream and pastry, this cleanser is crafted with an elegant alternation of oil and water layers. The result? A **dual-action formula** that begins as a lush oil and blossoms into a cushiony, creamy foam. In one gesture, it sweeps away impurities, environmental debris, and even ultra-fine dust particles, while lavishing the skin with hydration and nourishment.

Infused with a **30% blend of skin-conditioning oils, including antioxidant-rich olive oil**, this cleanser is uniquely designed to protect the skin barrier while it purifies. It also features glycerin and ectoin, offering a moisture surge that’s palpable from the very first rinse. Supporting this symphony are revered ingredients from Sulwhasoo’s herbal apothecary - **ginseng berry, Re.D Flavonoid™, peptides, niacinamide, and vitamin E** - each chosen for their soothing, brightening, and anti-aging benefits.

## 2. Color, Comfort, and Care: Perfecting Lip Color in Shade Bloom

![](https://img.allw.mn/365a3e1faafe87651be4c3ce95f389_1024x1024.jpg)

Where the cleanser brings the skin into balance, [Sulwhasoo’s Perfecting Lip Color](https://us.sulwhasoo.com/products/perfecting-lip-color) adds the final brushstroke - a **sheer veil of color that’s as nourishing as it is beautiful**. The latest shade to join the lineup, Bloom, is a muted warm pink, designed to harmonize seamlessly with the natural color of your lips. It’s soft, it’s subtle, and it’s effortlessly wearable - equally suited for a summer brunch as for a sunset soirée.

More than a balm and more refined than a lipstick, the **Perfecting Lip Color formula** treads that rare space where treatment meets tint. Its feather-light texture glides on without stickiness, and its color builds with each swipe, allowing for sheer daytime polish or a deeper evening hue. It’s beauty that behaves exactly how you want it to - with grace.

What sets this formulation apart is its **nourishing core**. At the heart is ginseng seed oil, a proprietary Sulwhasoo extract derived from the brand’s most treasured botanical. Known for its powerful moisturizing and absorption capabilities, it delivers fast hydration that penetrates without residue. Blended with sunflower seed wax and glycerin, the formula also supports the lip barrier, **making it ideal for lips prone to dryness or flaking**.

**The brand’s commitment to craftsmanship is evident in every detail** - from the multi-sensorial textures to the precision of its botanical formulations.
