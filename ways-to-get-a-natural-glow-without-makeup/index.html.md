# Super Simple Ways to Get a Natural Glow for Girls Who Hate Makeup ...

As girls, we're always looking for **ways to get a natural glow.** The other night I was getting ready with a couple of girlfriends to go out and they were shocked to hear I had never used foundation before and were eager to teach me how. When they applied it to my face, my skin looked utterly FLAWLESS, but I was surprised at how the look did not seem right on me, probably because I am so used to not having makeup on. I didn’t like the thick feeling of it, but since my roommates are both so good at doing their makeup and are both beautiful people, I couldn’t stand next to them without looking like a bum. SO, I found a great alternative that is a light makeup and can replace foundation. I now have a routine that gives me a perfect natural glow. So here are my favorite **ways to get a natural glow.**

## 1. Find the Right Product

![beauty, eyebrow, chin, cheek, close up,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/b4/dz4sk57g59ba725388789249723701_600x750.jpg)

The best of the many **ways to get a natural glow** is to find the right product for your skin. Use a beauty blender to apply it to your face and it will give you a perfect glow. Choose something that feels lightweight, which helps make your skin look naturally soft.

## 2. Add Highlighter and Contour Your Face

![hair, eyebrow, human hair color, blond, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fb/h5/f0l39tub59ba71ccbe4b9285252226_600x658.jpg)

Now, the only other thing I add is highlighter and a contour product. I don’t even use any sort of bronzer or powder. The highlighter and contour products stand out great against the my foundation.

## 3. Then Add Your Eye Routine and Lips

![human hair color, beauty, eyebrow, blond, supermodel,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z9/ay/p8g665gc59ba7203c0b57746356661_600x750.jpg)

This rounds out the look while still remaining super easy and simple. Go for a look that you love in terms of eyeshadow and mascara. Top things off with a great lip color and you're good to go.
