# 7 Drugstore Lotions That Really Work and save You Money ...

I admit that not all drugstore products are good ones, but when it comes to drugstore lotions, you can get what you want without having to spend a ton of money. Lotion is a must for any skin condition. It keeps your entire body soft, supple and healthy. If you think you have to get a second job to finance your lotion habits, you don’t. Check out these highly rated drugstore lotions that even celebrities and dermatologists recommend. I promise you won’t be disappointed.

## 1. EOS Hand Lotion

![EOS Hand Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ec/v1/bzxnquq1_300x288.jpg)

You might think lip balm when you hear EOS, but the truth is that they are one of the best labels to look for when it comes to drugstore lotions. This wonderful moisturizer contains aloe, antioxidants and shea butter, a combination that promises to leave your hands soft, healthy and infused with ingredients that keep them looking radiant and youthful. The subtle scent is just right too.

## 2. St. Ives Advanced Body Moisturizer

![St. Ives Advanced Body Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ik/m9/vdjw1kp8_300x291.jpg)

St. Ives is a household name and has been for many years. The company has a range of skincare products to meet anyone’s needs. You’re going to love this moisturizer that’s absolutely loaded with oatmeal and shea butter, two ingredients that are well known for moisturizing the skin all over your body. The fragrance of this lotion can’t be beat either.

## 3. Vaseline Intensive Care Total Moisture Clean Feeling Lotion

![Vaseline Intensive Care Total Moisture Clean Feeling Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zy/oe/o10xdc8y_600x540.jpg)

If you prefer a lotion that won’t leave your skin feeling slimy, check out this great product from Vaseline. With such a great reputation, you know this lotion has to be good. A combination of nourishing and softening ingredients comes together to give you a great smelling and super effective lotion you can use all over your body.

## 4. Udderly Smooth Hand and Body Lotion

![Udderly Smooth Hand and Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xf/la/g4o3irzk_300x300.jpg)

Not only is the packaging of this lotion fun to look at, but the product itself is awesome too. Vitamin E and aloe vera combine in one lotion to give you skin you’ll love to touch. This creamy formula leaves you with soft, supple skin you’ll want to keep forever.

## 5. Mountain Ocean Skin Trip Moisturizer

![Mountain Ocean Skin Trip Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/up/0907qp76_450x450.jpg)

I love this lotion because it really works, but it also smells like a beach vacation. The coconut scent is lovely, but it won’t overwhelm you either. At the same time, the lotion boasts coconut oil, aloe and lanolin, all of which are perfect for keeping your skin moisturized and healthy. This lotion absorbs quickly and won’t leave you feeling slimy.

## 6. Johnson’s Baby Lotion

![Johnson’s Baby Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/j7/hyg67enl_389x389.jpg)

It’s not just for babies! The gentle ingredients and subtle scent of this lotion make it perfect for anyone. It goes on smooth and lasts forever because you don’t need a ton for ample coverage. This lotion is affordable and super easy to find so you’ll never be left high and dry. Use some on your little one, then slather it on yourself. You’ll both benefit.

## 7. Suave Cocoa Butter Skin Therapy Lotion

![Suave Cocoa Butter Skin Therapy Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c9/fz/hqbvioqq_450x450.jpg)

I saved one of the best for last! This lotion is one of my absolute favorites. It has a bit of a cocoa scent so you’ll smell delicious in addition to being soft and moisturized. This is one of the least expensive lotions you’ll ever find and it works so good you’ll never be able to believe how great it leaves your skin looking and feeling.

Do you use any of these great drugstore lotions? If not, which one do you want to try first? Remember to apply lotion after your shower each day to lock in moisture and keep your skin youthful and healthy for your entire life.
