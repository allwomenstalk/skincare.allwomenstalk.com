# 7 Simple Tricks to Get Perfect Skin in 7 Days ...

It probably won’t surprise you to learn that out of all of the health and beauty related issues people search solutions for online, achieving great skin is amongst the most popular. With issues like dryness, oiliness, redness, flakiness and overall acne being prominent for people of all ages and races, the desire to seek tips and tricks to improve one’s skin and appearance is one of the most prominent searches online. It’s fair to say that more serious conditions like acne take months and months to get under control, but here are some basic **tips for how to get perfect skin in 7 days**.

## 1. Clean Eating

The state of your skin can often be a reflection of the food that you put in your body. Seek out foods that are high in antioxidants, things like berries, pecan nuts and even dark chocolate. Having a healthy diet doesn’t have to mean restricting yourself and avoiding all of the fun stuff!

## 2. Don’t Pop

It’s tempting to pop zits when you see them, but that’s an easy way to leave permanent damage on your skin. Plus, popping a zit only serves to spread the oil over your face. Instead, you an eradicating serum that you put on top of the affected areas to dry and draw them out. No popping, no scabs, no freaking out!

## 3. Face Masks

Face masks are a fun and simple way to improve your skin in short time. Pick one that has detoxifying properties, slap it on, and sit down to a couple of your favourite Netflix show episodes before taking it off. It’s probably the easiest and most enjoyable skin care regime possible!

## 4. Moisturiser

You need to be using the right kind of moisturiser for your skin type to avoid skin breakouts. Also, you should try to avoid putting too much on that thin skin underneath your eyes, and overall you should be applying it very lightly. Don’t lather it on because it will only lead to oily skin.

## 5. Hydration

Keep your body hydrated by drinking lots of water every day, but also use a special hydrating moisturiser on your face that promises special care and attention to your skin. Doing both results in a double whammy of moisturising!

## 6. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is basically a nectar from the gods when it comes to skincare! It’s amazing when you put it on your face, it can help to clear away spots and make your skin feel smooth and silky.

## 7. Cleansing

Did you know that on average when you cleanse your face, you are only clearing about 70% of it? There are products that you can buy that will treat your skin to a much more intense and full cleansing, almost like a spa in the palm of your hand. They do it all in one for you, exfoliation, cleansing and massaging!
