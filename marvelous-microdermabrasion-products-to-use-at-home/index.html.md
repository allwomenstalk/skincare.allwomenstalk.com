# 7 Marvelous Microdermabrasion Products to Use at Home ...

At-home microdermabrasion products are a total beauty godsend! There are lots of benefits to getting microdermabrasion, but getting regular treatments at a spa can get quite pricey. Microdermabrasion is known for helping brighten up dull skin and improve the appearance of dark spots and fine lines. Don’t get me wrong, I know some treatments are better left to professionals and some treatments just don’t work as well when you get an at-home kit. Fortunately, I found 7 microdermabrasion products that can give you impressive results without the high price tag.

## 1. Olay Regenerist Microdermabrasion & Peel System

![Olay Regenerist Microdermabrasion & Peel System](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18214712_2676_409x388.jpg)

Let’s get right into this list of microdermabrasion products with one of the best budget-friendly beauty brands around, Olay. Olay’s Regenerist Microdermabrasion & Peel System can help you achieve a better skin tone as well as minimize the appearance of fine line and wrinkles with regular use. The beauty of this kit is that you can get the benefits of a peel and microdermabrasion so you’ll have better skin texture and tone while activating surface cell renewal. You can get it for $23.94 at [walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 2. DDF Revolve 400X Limited Edition Bundle Set

![DDF Revolve 400X Limited Edition Bundle Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18214713_4253_599x468.jpg)

Another highly rated at-home microdermabrasion set to consider is DDF’s micro-polishing system and it boasts some pretty impressive results! According to DDF, their micro-polishing system can deliver 8x better facial exfoliation than a scrub, instantly reduce pore appearance and it’s more effective than a professional microdermabrasion. This skin polisher isn’t harsh or abrasive and results are said to be noticeable in a short time. Get yours for $98 at [beautybar.com](https://goo.gl/RALoqz).

## 3. Mary Kay TimeWise Microdermabrasion Set

_ Mary Kay has gotten in on the microderm madness with their very own Timewise Microdermabrasion Set. This two-step skin care system features Refine, the first step to polish and revitalize your skin and Replenish, the second step to nourish and replenish the fresh, new skin. Timewise is clinically tested for irritation and allergies and is said to leave you with noticeably softer, smoother, youthful-looking skin with just a few uses. Find it for $50 at marykay.com_.

## 4. Dr. Brandt Microdermabrasion

![Dr. Brandt Microdermabrasion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18214712_7361_329x480.jpg)

Dr. Brandt’s microdermabrasion is more like a scrub than a microdermabrasion product, but can be just as effective. It’s got pharmaceutical-grade crystals, lactic acid and botanicals just like the fancy pro treatments, but it’s cheaper and comes in a convenient tube. This gentle yet effective skin care treatment will give you noticeably reduced fine lines and wrinkles, acne scars and discolorations and leave you with a radiant glow. It's $78 at [drbrandtskincare.com](http://www.drbrandtskincare.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 5. Algenist Triple-Action Micropolish & Peel

![Algenist Triple-Action Micropolish & Peel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18214711_4601_249x246.jpg)

Algenist’s take on at-home microdermabrasion features a microdermabrasion peel with 10% Alguronic acid and glycolic acid, 10% fruit enzyme complex and micro-polishing crystals for the ultimate in surface cell turnover, dead skin cell removal and improvement of skin imperfections. All you need is about 5 minutes about once a week for a brighter, smoother complexion. Get it for $58 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com).

## 6. Boots No7 Total Renewal Micro-Dermabrasion Exfoliator

![Boots No7 Total Renewal Micro-Dermabrasion Exfoliator](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18214712_8033_449x432.jpg)

I know, this skin care system looks really simple and plain, but it’s got high marks and you can’t beat the price! With 4 treatments, this exfoliator is said to visibly reduce the appearance of dark spots and fine lines, to improve the condition of acne-prone skin and to even out your skin tone. In just a couple of minutes you can give yourself this treatment and your skin will be seriously glowing in no time. Snatch it up for $15.79 at [walgreens.com](https://goo.gl/eBnZyh).

## 7. NuBrilliance Microdermabrasion System

![NuBrilliance Microdermabrasion System](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/18214713_6882_599x563.jpg)

I can’t talk about at-home microdermabrasion without mentioning NuBrilliance. This little guy is much pricier than most other microdermabrasion products on the market, but it’s one of the more well-known. This skin care product features a diamond tip to exfoliate and firm up skin in addition to promoting collagen production and skin cell renewal. I’ve actually used this before and the process takes a little getting used to since you’re not just applying a scrub on your skin. I did notice that it did a good job in cleaning up the pores on my nose but I didn’t use it much afterward, so I can’t vouch for the overall effectiveness. However, if you’ve got the extra cash and want something different than your average, give it a try! You can get it for $249 at [beautybar.com](https://goo.gl/2DObm6).
