# 7 Facial Scrubs I Love ...

I do so love a good exfoliating facial scrub! There’s nothing like them for sloughing away dead, dull skin cells to reveal the glowing, gorgeous complexion underneath. But there are so many to choose form, literally thousands of them, so how do you know which to try? My dear, I can help! Here are a few of my own favorites… these are the facial scrubs I love!

## 1. Philosophy Microdelivery

![Philosophy Microdelivery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qw/vu/ioac6rmc54c9262a5c077632088110_250x246.jpg)

**Price:** $25.00 - $37.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is my every day facial scrub, the one I owe my gorgeous glowing skin to. It’s gentle, but still effective, and just a little bit goes a very long way. I recommend the big, 16-ounce size bottle, exclusive to Sephora.

## 2. Boscia Luminizing Black Mask

![Boscia Luminizing Black Mask](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o3/yl/k3eqxs5z54c9262b1a5ed279830614_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $34.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Most facial scrubs are washes, but this one’s a mask. Apply it in a thin layer to your face, wait for 15 minutes, then peel it away to reveal detoxified, smoother skin. One note: don’t be alarmed when you put it on… it’s supposed to be black!

## 3. Fresh Sugar Face Polish

**Price:** $55.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Is it silly or strange to say I love this facial scrub because it smells so good? It works well, too, but I especially love it for its light, somewhat citrusy scent. It’s not harsh, either, like some scrubs tend to be.

## 4. DDF Pumice Acne Scrub

![DDF Pumice Acne Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j3/jx/l7hvcysy54c9262bdafa5271902867_250x240.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
If you suffer from acne, I recommend this scrub, made with breakout-busting benzoyl peroxide and tiny exfoliating beads. Use it one to three times a week to keep acne away, and to soothe and cleanse your delicate skin.

## 5. Clinique 7-Day Scrub

![Clinique 7-Day Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r1/ta/m2llpjvc54c9262cad710366787338_250x232.jpg)

**Price:** $18.50 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I love this facial scrub because you can use it every day! It works to smooth away fine lines and rinks, while sloughing off dry, dead skin to reveal the radiant complexion underneath! I use it with my fave Clinique moisturizer in the summer to get an effortless, healthy glow.

## 6. Clarins One-Step Gentle Exfoliating Facial Cleanser

![Clarins One-Step Gentle Exfoliating Facial Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pl/lh/hty5i2te54c9262d54d0a048757811_250x236.jpg)

**Price:** $35.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I use this gentle but effective facial scrub before bed to get rid of all the dirt, make-up, and pollutants my skin has absorbed all day long. It works well without over-drying or over-scrubbing your skin. I love it!

## 7. Ole Henriksen Walnut Complexion Scrub

![Ole Henriksen Walnut Complexion Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kl/xz/kji2438w54c9262e0abc4322030335_200x224.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Reading this product description for the first time, I thought: It VACUUMS away dead skin cells? Well, I had to try it… and it DOES! I don’t know how the walnut shell works, but it does, and I love how my skin tingles and glows, and how refreshed it feels, after I use this facial scrub!

I absolutely ADORE that facial scrub by Philosophy, and the sugar one is a favorite, too! Try a few of these and see which you like best, and then please, sweetie, let me know! Or perhaps you’ve already tried a scrub? If so, which one do you like best, from my list or not?
