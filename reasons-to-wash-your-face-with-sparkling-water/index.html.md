# Reasons to Wash Your Face with Sparkling Water to Get Gorgeous Glowing Skin ...

What are the reasons to wash your face with sparkling water? Keep reading to find out.

Millions of dollars per year are spent by men and women alike in search of attaining better, healthier skin. From creams to oils to more invasive treatments, multiple methods are exhausted all over the world in an attempt to achieve the very best results.

So what if I were to tell you that there is a simple method that costs far less than all of the big brand names would want you to know? And it is simple sparkling carbonated water! Believe it or not, washing your face with sparkling water has been proven as an extremely beneficial way of achieving a long lasting glow in a short space of time. This is mainly due to the fact that it cleans and tightens the pores, giving your skin a smooth and firm appearance. Here are the best reasons to wash your face with sparkling water.

## 1. Cheap and Easy to Find

One of the biggest reasons to wash your face with sparkling water is the simple fact that it's easy buy and much cheaper than some of the beauty products that profess to do the same thing.

## 2. Refresh Your Skin

Whenever you are feeling tired or without energy, a little dab of sparkling water can help to rejuvenate your skin by bringing the collagen fibers together, adding much needed firmness.

## 3. Cleans Your Pores

The nature of carbonated water also means that it is really effective in washing out all of the dirt and debris from your pores that can accumulate over the course of a working day.

## 4. Perfect for All Skin Types

Unlike other beauty and skincare products, sparkling water is compatible with all kinds of skin types, which means that you do not have to worry about the risk of a rash or allergic reaction when using it.

## 5. Using for Oily and Normal Skin

If you have normal or oily skin, it is recommended that you fill a bowl with sparkling water and dip your face in it while holding your breath for as long as possible. You should aim to do this once a week for normal skin and three times a week for oily skin.

## 6. Using for Sensitive or Dry Skin

If you have sensitive or dry skin, it is recommended that you use half sparkling water, half still water in your bowl, soaking your face while holding your breath for as long as you can. To begin with, do this once a week to test how your skin reacts, and once you find that you are fine, you can then start to experiment with changing the measure of carbonated to still water in your bowl for even more glowing effects.
