# The Nitty Gritty Truths on Face Water Increasing Your Beauty ...

In my many years of researching and writing about all things beauty and skincare, I’ve noticed that there are always new trends and exciting new products to try. Face waters are something I’m excited to share with you because they can be so effective for so many girls. If you are committed to trying something new, I think you’ll love what face waters can do for you. Here’s everything you need to know about them to make the right choice for you.

## 1. They Are Super Easy on Skin so Anyone Can Use Face Waters

Face waters are ideal for even the most sensitive skin so pretty much any girl can use them. The ingredients are mild so they do their job of cleansing your face without drying your skin and causing irritation. Some girls are afraid of products like this because they don’t feel like they do a good job of cleaning, but the opposite is true. You’ll love how clean face waters get your skin!

## 2. Many Face Waters Are Infused with Important Nutrients

You know that you skin relies on certain vitamins and minerals to remain strong and healthy, right? While eating a wide variety of healthy foods, such as fruits and vegetables, is a prime way to get those nutrients to your skin, you can also use face waters infused with them. Look for those that contain vitamins A, C and E, which help combat the signs of aging, preserve cells to prevent skin cancer and keep your skin soft and smooth.

## 3. Face Waters Are Oil-Free

Awesome, right? If you have super oily skin, it pays to try a product that won’t add more oil to your skin. Because face waters don’t contain oils that absorb into your skin, you might find that they are just what you need to combat shine and keep your face clean at the same time. What more could a girl ask for?

## 4. Marine Botanicals Are Something You Should Look for Too

Not all brands of face waters contain marine botanicals, but if you can find one that does, that’s the one you want. Things like sea kelp and sea lettuce infuse your facial skin with nutrients you can’t find just anywhere. Face waters without are still effective, but if you want even more benefits, you want marine botanicals.

## 5. The Best Ones Will Remove Waterproof Makeup

If you wear waterproof mascara or other cosmetics, face waters can help you get them off at the end of the day. I hate how hard waterproof mascara is to get off, so if there’s something that will do the job, I definitely want to try it. Don’t you?

## 6. Dry Skin is a Thing of the past when You Use Face Waters

I can personally tell you how unpleasant dry skin on your face can be. To fight it, you have to make sure you’re pumping your face full of moisture. Face waters, true to their name, are super hydrating. When used in combination with a good facial moisturizer, you might notice that dry, flaking skin starts to improve and that tight, itchy feeling you get when your skin is dry starts to go away.

## 7. You Don’t Need a Ton of Product to do the Job

When it comes to face waters, you only need a bit of product to cleanse your skin and sweep away makeup, dirt and debris. That means you won’t have to constantly go back to the store for more and you won’t have to spend a ton of money on restocking all the time.

Have you ever tried face waters? After reading this, do you want to?
