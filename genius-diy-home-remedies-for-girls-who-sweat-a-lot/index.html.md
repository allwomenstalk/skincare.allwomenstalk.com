# Genius DIY Home Remedies for Girls Who Sweat a Lot ...

I hate getting all sloppy and sweaty and I bet you feel the same way too. There's no way to avoid the wetness when you work out or when the temperature soars outside. However, If you find yourself sweating all the time and you're sick and tired of it, you can use any of these simple DIY remedies to battle the sweat and spend your days dry and fresh.

## 1. Baking Soda is a Natural Antiperspirant

Antiperspirant products work by stopping sweat to cut down on stink and wetness. You can buy them over the counter in stick or spray form, but baking soda is a natural antiperspirant that is cheap and easy to use.
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon baking soda
Water
Directions:
Combine the baking soda with enough water to create a thick paste. Spread the mixture in your pits and wait 15 to 20 minutes before rinsing. Do this a few times a week.
Source: [top10homeremedies.com](https://www.emedihealth.com/skin-beauty/more-skin-conditions/stop-excessive-sweating)

## 2. Tomato Juice is a Tasty Remedy

Isn't it great when you can do something enjoy and battle a problem at the same time. Tomato juice is a great option for people who sweat a lot.
Ingredients:
1 cup tomato juice
Directions:
Simply drink a glass of tomato juice each day and you should notice a difference pretty quickly.
Source: [homeremedyshop.com](https://homeremedyshop.com/12-little-known-home-remedies-for-excessive-sweating/)

## 3. You Need Some Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar has quite a few health benefits and battling excessive sweat is one of them. Try this today and let me know how it goes.
Ingredients:
2 to 4 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
Directions:
Soak a couple of cotton balls in apple cider vinegar and then apply the vinegar to your armpits before bed. In the morning, rinse your armpits and get on with your day.
Source: [homeremediesforlife.com](https://homeremediesforlife.com/excessive-sweating/)

## 4. Sage Leaves Could Be the Miracle You're Looking for

Not only can sage battle excessive sweat, but it also smells pretty good, which can keep sweat smell at bay all through the day.
Ingredients:
1 teaspoon dried sage
2 cups water
Directions:
Bring the water to a boil and steep the dried sage for several minutes. Allow the mixture to cool, then use it as a cleanser where you sweat the most. Repeat the procedure three times per day.
Source: [top10homeremedies.com](https://www.emedihealth.com/skin-beauty/more-skin-conditions/stop-excessive-sweating)

## 5. Witch Hazel is a Viable Option

You've probably heard of using witch hazel for soothing hemorrhoids or the discomfort in your nether regions after giving birth. Turns out it's also great for stopping sweat.
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon witch hazel bark
Water
Directions:
Combine the witch hazel bark with enough water to form a paste. Spread the paste on your sweaty areas and leave it for an hour. Wait an hour, then rinse with cold water.
Source: [top10homeremedies.com](https://www.emedihealth.com/skin-beauty/more-skin-conditions/stop-excessive-sweating)

## 6. Try Lemon Today

I always keep lemons in my fridge and that's a great thing because they are useful for so many things. Excessive sweating is one of those things.
Ingredients:
1 lemon
Water
Directions:
Cut the lemon in half and squeeze it just a bit to get the juice flowing. Rub one half of the lemon in each of your armpits and leave it until morning. Mix with a bit of water if your skin is sensitive.
Source: [homeremediesforlife.com](https://homeremediesforlife.com/excessive-sweating/)

## 7. Potatoes Are an Easy Choice

Chances are you have potatoes in your pantry as we speak, so this remedy should be a super easy one for you.
Ingredients:
1 potato
Directions:
Slice the potato and rub the slices into your pits. All done!
Source: [homeremedyshop.com](https://homeremedyshop.com/12-little-known-home-remedies-for-excessive-sweating/)
