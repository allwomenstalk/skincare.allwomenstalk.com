## 1. Look for Something with Vitamin E

Vitamin E is a necessary ingredient for healthy skin. It helps build collagen and keep your skin looking young and fresh. The best part is that it’s perfect for people dealing with sensitive skin. Using products with vitamin E helps protect your skin from free radicals, while also boosting hydration so you can keep dryness at bay. Vitamin E can be found in cleansers and moisturizers, as well as in facial masks.

## 2. You’ll Love Cucumber Extract

In general, natural ingredients are better for sensitive skin than chemical ones. Though there are cases of a plant ingredient exacerbating skin issues, most girls with sensitive skin can safely use products that contain cucumber extract. It’s incredibly soothing to the skin and hydrates at the same time. If you want a flawless complexion, consider giving cucumber extract a try today.

## 3. Chamomile is a Fantastic Choice

You’ve probably heard that drinking chamomile tea is great for relaxation. The same is true when you apply it to your skin. Chamomile is anti-inflammatory, which is what makes it so perfect for sensitive skin. It’s great for people who suffer from acne and eczema in addition to sensitivity so be sure to talk it over with your dermatologist as a possibility for your skincare routine.

## 4. You Can’t Go Wrong with Oatmeal

There’s a reason you see oatmeal in so many skincare products. It works to soothe sensitive skin and is ideal for people who have eczema. It’s also a great way to exfoliate your skin without irritating it. There are loads of lotions and creams that list oatmeal as an ingredient and you should definitely try one of them today.

## 5. Try Something with Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a plant that has been used medicinally for generations. It’s most common use is to soothe a burn, but it’s also a fantastic ingredient if you suffer from sensitive skin. It packs a powerful punch by hydrating your skin, soothing irritation and easing inflammation at the same time. Products that contain aloe vera are pretty easy to find so you should have no trouble choosing something that works for you.

## 6. You Might like Something That Contains Seaweed

Seaweed is a powerful ingredient for fighting free radical damage on your skin. That helps battle skin cancer and the signs of aging. The great thing is that seaweed is also perfect for sensitive skin because it contains a whole host of nutrients that you need to keep your skin healthy so that it can stay looking and feeling its best every single day of the week.

## 7. Hyaluronic Acid

You might be wondering how an acid can possibly be good for sensitive skin, but experts say that hyaluronic acid is a great choice. It simultaneously moisturizes skin and helps keep tissues healthy. This ingredient is fairly common in skincare products so it’s easy to test it out on your skin.
