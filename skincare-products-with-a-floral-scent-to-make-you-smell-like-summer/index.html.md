# 7 Skincare Products with a Floral Scent to Make You Smell like Summer ...

I love skincare products with a floral scent because I can’t wear perfume due to allergies. By using these items, I can still smell fresh and summery without having to sneeze and break out. Plus, these great products can make you feel like the warm weather will last forever. Luckily, there is no shortage of skincare products with a floral scent so you can pick and choose which ones you like best. Start with these, but please share your other top picks with me.

## 1. Philosophy Loveswept 3-in1 Shower Gel

![Philosophy Loveswept 3-in1 Shower Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nu/vx/gexi994m_294x300.jpg)

You can use this great item as shampoo, bubble bath or body wash, which is why it tops my list of skincare products with a floral scent. It has a subtle flowery smell that won’t knock you over, but will leave you with a fresh, summery note that you won’t be able to get enough of. All that and it nourishes and moisturizes your skin and hair at the same time.

## 2. Perlier Floral Fragrance Hand Cream

![Perlier Floral Fragrance Hand Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s3/i8/byielna7_224x217.jpg)

This is a great lotion to keep in your purse. It comes in small tubes so you can slather on a floral scent anytime the mood strikes. Use it to moisturize and soften your hands on the go, or keep a tube at home to use after you wash your hands. Either way, you’ll catch a sweet whiff of a field full of flowers anytime you take the cap off.

## 3. Darphin 8 Flower Nectar

![Darphin 8 Flower Nectar](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/iu/tv/z99djnva_300x300.jpg)

This product is definitely a splurge, but you won’t regret it. It combines the scents of eight exotic flowers and works wonders for your skin. It’ll tone, reduce fine lines, detoxify and give you a natural glow that will help you stay youthful and radiant looking. Who could ask for more than a great smelling product that fights the signs of aging?

## 4. Sisley Botanical Floral Mist Spray

![Sisley Botanical Floral Mist Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fa/x3/0i4tsom8_286x329.jpg)

Have you ever been caught worried that you smell less than fresh? We all have! The answer is this great small bottle of spray that leaves you with just the right amount of flowery scent. You can cover up what you’re worried about without blowing people away with a strong smell. I can get behind that!

## 5. Organics Scented Soap in Romantic Floral

![Organics Scented Soap in Romantic Floral](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pf/y5/tsdp489c_322x200.jpg)

You can count on this bar soap to gently cleanse your skin while leaving it infused with all the ingredients you rely on for soft, supple skin. Not only that, but it leaves you with a wonderful smell that you’re going to love inhaling all day long.

## 6. Ahava Mineral Botanic Body Lotion in Lotus and Chestnuts

![Ahava Mineral Botanic Body Lotion in Lotus and Chestnuts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/me/gm/vz9qvnfs_300x360.jpg)

The name smells it out completely. Botanic and lotus are the keywords when it comes to this fantastic smelling lotion. This is a great choice to soothe your skin and the scent will leave you feeling calm and relaxed. The unique smell of the lotus flower is one you won’t find in just any product, so I guarantee you’re going to love this.

## 7. Dr. Haushka Skin Care Deodorant in Floral Fresh

![Dr. Haushka Skin Care Deodorant in Floral Fresh](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/t0/fhxoqjfq_300x300.jpg)

I tend to stay clear of any deodorant that smells like baby powder. That’s why I love this product. It helps ward off body odor, but leaves me with a flowery smell that feels and smells fresh. It’s safe for anyone, even those with sensitive skin, so give it a try!
