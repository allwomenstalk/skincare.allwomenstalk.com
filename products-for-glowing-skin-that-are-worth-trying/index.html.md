# 7 Products for Glowing Skin That Are Worth Trying for a Radiant Look

Finding the perfect routine can feel like an endless search, but the right products for glowing skin make all the difference. With dozens of products to choose from, it’s hard to find quality options that actually deliver results. I’ve used tons of brands, from drugstore staples to luxury splurges, over the years in all types of categories including makeup, face wash, and moisturizers. After years of trial and error, I have found 7 products for glowing skin that are a must-try, affordable, and easily available over the counter!

## 1. Aveeno Stress Relief Moisturizing Lotion

![Aveeno Stress Relief Moisturizing Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174420_7023_599x575.jpg)

This lotion is great for both day and night and remains one of my favorite products for glowing skin. I love it for the day because a couple of minutes after applying, the lotion is completely absorbed into your skin and there’s no greasy residue—this is absolutely perfect to wear underneath makeup. Plus, the ylang-ylang oils combined with colloidal oatmeal really help to nourish and soothe the skin. If you're looking for more ways to [get glowing skin](https://skincare.allwomenstalk.com/tips-for-glowing-skin/), starting with a reliable moisturizer is key.

## 2. Josie Maran Argan Oil

![Josie Maran Argan Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174441_5056_449x436.jpg)

After I’ve washed my makeup off at night, I usually combine a few drops of Argan oil with my face moisturizer. Since I’ve been using Josie Maran Argan oil, marks from previous pimples have started to fade faster. Also, the oil really helps to nourish my skin, thus preventing future breakouts. It is often asked [is Josie Maran non-comedogenic](https://www.josiemaran.com/products/100-pure-argan-oil)? Yes, it is designed to be gentle on pores. I’ve definitely seen an improvement in my skin and highly recommend this product to everyone looking for a natural boost.

## 3. St. Ives Apricot Scrub

![St. Ives Apricot Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174512_7882_449x411.jpg)

I only use this product 2-3 times a week (using too much will dry out your skin, causing irritation and clogged pores), and my face feels a lot smoother after each use. It’s very important to exfoliate your skin on a regular basis to delay the look of aging. Another reason to exfoliate your face is to rid your skin of old, dull skin cells and help reveal new cells which result in a glowing complexion. For those who prefer a different texture, the [St. Ives nourish and glow face moisturizer](https://www.stives.com/products/fresh-skin-apricot-scrub) can also be a great addition to your routine.

## 4. Cetaphil Moisturizing Lotion

![Cetaphil Moisturizing Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174535_4369_459x419.jpg)

I love this lotion for the summer because it is very light—it almost feels like I’m not wearing any lotion at all. Cetaphil Moisturizing Lotion contains zero parabens and is non-comedogenic. Plus, Cetaphil products are known for helping to reduce acne breakouts. This product is perfect if you’re acne-prone but are looking for a reliable [drugstore moisturizer](https://skincare.allwomenstalk.com/drugstore-moisturizers-for-acne-prone-skin/) to hydrate your skin without the heavy feel.

## 5. Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser

![Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174602_1903_272x396.jpg)

Before I had discovered Argan Oil, I used to have constant breakouts. I tried face washes from several brands carried by drugstores. Most dried out my skin and made no difference to the amount of breakouts I had. However, once I tried Cetaphil’s award-winning cleanser, my breakouts reduced by at least 75%. Although the price may seem steep at first, one bottle lasts 5-6 months so it is definitely worth the investment for anyone struggling with [acne scars](#2) or persistent blemishes.

## 6. Bare Minerals Ready SPF 20 Foundation

![Bare Minerals Ready SPF 20 Foundation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174646_8916_459x418.jpg)

For years I couldn’t find a makeup that could do the two things I wanted: give me good coverage and prevent my skin from breaking out. Fortunately, I discovered Bare Minerals Ready SPF 20 foundation (it’s a foundation in a mineral powder form). Unlike other mineral powders that I used in the past, Bare Minerals is matte and doesn’t get shiny over the course of the day. Also, it provides just as much coverage as liquid foundation and, best of all, it’s gentle enough to use every day. This is a must-have for a [flawless mineral finish](https://www.bareminerals.com/).

## 7. Neutrogena Oil-Free Cleansing Wipes

![Neutrogena Oil-Free Cleansing Wipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/06174705_5027_499x383.jpg)

For the nights that I’m too exhausted to wash my makeup off, I turn to my Neutrogena wipes. The wipes are perfect for removing all types of makeup, even waterproof formulas. Plus, they're great for women who travel a lot and can’t carry big bottles of makeup remover or face wash. Most importantly, they are ophthalmologist, dermatologist, and allergy-tested as well as alcohol-free.

- Removes waterproof mascara effortlessly
- Travel-friendly packaging
- Gentle on sensitive eyes

All of these products are over-the-counter and are all under $40. However, before trying too many new products, take some time to visit a dermatologist to learn more about your skin, any problem areas, and if there are any products you should stay away from.
