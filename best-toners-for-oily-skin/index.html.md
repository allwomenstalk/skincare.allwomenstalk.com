# The 7 Best Toners for Oily Skin All Girls Can Appreciate ...

The **best toners for oily skin** are the ones that do the job for you, no matter how much grease you have to deal with. No matter what season we are in, we all want healthy and flawless skin. In case you didn’t know, it’s quite simple to have fresh skin. All you need to do is add one product to your daily skin care routine and the secret weapon to healthy skin is a face toner. Keep reading to find out the benefits of using a face toner daily. Toners are most helpful for people with oily or acne prone skin. Here are the key benefits to using a toner and tips for choosing the **best toners for oily skin.**

**IT SHRINKS PORES**
The **best toners for oily skin help shrink pores.** Applying a small amount of toner to a soft cotton ball or pad and gently blotting your face with it will remove oil and give the appearance of smaller pores.

**IT RESTORES YOUR SKIN’S PH BALANCE**
Our skin is naturally acidic, typically with a PH balance of five or six, but the balance can get messed up after cleansing, due to the alkaline nature of soap. When this happens, our skin must work overtime to return to its normal level. The right toner can help do this.

**IT ADDS A LAYER OF PROTECTION**
Toners can help close pores and tighten cell gaps after cleansing, which also reduces the penetration of impurities and environmental contaminants into the skin. It can even protect and remove chlorine and minerals that are present in tap water.

**IT REFRESHES SKIN**
Toners can also be used instead of washing your skin when it’s oily or dirty. It will leave your skin revitalized, even when you’re on the go.

**IT CAN PREVENT INGROWN HAIRS**
Toners containing glycolic or other alpha hydroxy acids and can help to prevent ingrown hairs.
