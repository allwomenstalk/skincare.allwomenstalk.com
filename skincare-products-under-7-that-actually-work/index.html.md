# 7 Skincare Products under 7 That Actually Work ...

Ready for some skincare products under $7 that actually do what they say? I thought you’d be interested! Get ready to make a list of skincare products to buy on your next shopping trip because I’ve got some skin goodies that are effective and affordable! We all know that expensive doesn’t always equal good so prepare to be amazed with these budget buys!

## 1. Cetaphil Daily Facial Cleanser

_ **Price:** $5.99 at target.com_
This skincare product under $7 is well-loved by celebrities and us normal folks alike! It’s been reported that Natalie Portman, Olivia Wilde, Zoe Saldana and Cameron Diaz are all fans of this gentle cleanser and I can see why! Cetaphil is recommended by dermatologists and suits all skin types. If you’re looking for a soap-free cleanser that thoroughly cleanses and leaves your skin soft, this is a must-try!

## 2. Clean & Clear Oil Absorbing Sheets

![Clean & Clear Oil Absorbing Sheets](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/09/2_clean-clear-oil-absorbing-sheets_410x410.jpg)

**Price:** $4.49 at [target.com](https://www.target.com/p/clean-clear-instant-oil-absorbing-sheets-50-sheets/-/A-11537200)
These oil absorbing sheets might not be your typical skincare product, but it definitely makes this list of cheap skincare products that work! If you have oily or combination skin you’ll likely encounter plenty of shine throughout the day. Combat excess oil with these rubbery-looking blotting sheets so you get rid of the shine and let your beautiful skin take center stage!

## 3. St. Ives Blemish & Blackhead Control Apricot Scrub

![St. Ives Blemish & Blackhead Control Apricot Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/09/3_st-ives-blemish-blackhead-control-apricot-scrub_410x410.jpg)

**Price:** $3.04 at [target.com](https://www.target.com/p/st-ives-blemish-blackhead-control-apricot-scrub-6-oz/-/A-11129467)
Budget skincare products are getting more advanced so you can get quality acne control ingredients in a cheap facial scrub! St. Ives makes fabulous facial scrubs and one of their best is the Blemish & Blackhead Control Apricot Scrub. This scrub is just grainy enough to give a deep cleaning without being too abrasive and keeps pores clean to control blackheads!

## 4. Simple Cleansing Facial Wipes

![Simple Cleansing Facial Wipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/09/4_simple-cleansing-facial-wipes_410x324.jpg)

**Price:** $4.99 at [target.com](https://www.target.com/p/simple-cleansing-facial-wipes-25-count/-/A-13903998)
Any list of skincare products under $7 has to include facial cleansing wipes. These gentle yet effective wipes from Simple, which is the #1 skincare brand in the U.K., are awesome! These cleansing cloths are a must-have for any busy lady or any ladies who want to take good care of their skin regardless of their schedule.

## 5. Garnier Refreshing Gel Cream

_ **Price:** $6.99 at target.com_
This skincare product under $7 barely made the cut at $6.99 but it’s still worth mentioning! Garnier created a cool gel moisturizer perfect for hot weather or for those who prefer a moisturizer with a lighter texture. Don’t let the weightless formula fool you though, this gel locks in moisture and pampers dry skin for 'round the clock relief.

## 6. Gold Bond Ultimate Softening Lotion

_ **Price:** $5.69 at target.com_
This is one of those skincare products that you can’t live without, a good lotion. Whenever I think of Gold Bond it takes me back to their orange bottles that smelled like medicine, but this newer version smells nothing like medication and moisturizes for days! Keep a small one in your bag and a full-size version on your nightstand!

## 7. Boots Expert Instant Matte Shine Control

_ **Price:** $6.29 at target.com_
Another one of the fine budget skincare products from the U.K. is Boots’ Expert Instant Matte Shine Control! Many shine control products are in the double digit price range so this budget-friendly buy is a great find. Even if you don’t have super oily skin but like to look fresh and polished, this liquid should offer good shine control for the whole day.

I’ll admit it, it was hard to make this list of skincare products under $7 and limit it to only seven products! There are so many fab drugstore skincare items but these seven were definitely some of the crème de la crème! What’s your favorite budget skincare product?
