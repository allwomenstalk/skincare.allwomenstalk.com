# Celebrate Autumn with Some Fragrant Fall Scented Lotions ...

One of my favorite aspects of fall is trying out new scented lotions inspired by the cool season. I love browsing the shelves at my local pharmacy and beauty shop for these lotions that make my skin smell utterly divine. Now that we're jumping right into the school season, there's no better excuse to go shopping for skincare products that actually work. Ladies and gentlemen, get ready to say goodbye to dry and flaky skin with these amazing scented lotions!

## 1. The Righteous Butter Body Lotion

![The Righteous Butter Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f4/ci/a5rihp7e_600x597.jpg)

Scented lotions that contain shea butter and almond oil are a great place to start! Soap & Glory's "The Righteous Butter" body lotion has the perfect blend of bergamot, peach, vanilla and sandalwood, which just so happen to be my favorite notes! The aloe vera really helps soothe cracked skin and seals in moisture. You can head over to Sephora to grab a bottle of this fabulous product (which incidentally, also received a beauty award from Elle Magazine.)

## 2. Apple Blossom and Lavender Body Lotion

![Apple Blossom and Lavender Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gj/gb/q6m1hsl0_600x744.jpg)

Am I the only one who's excited that apple picking season is here? Trust me when I say that you'll be floored by how amazing this lovely mix of crisp green apple, fresh lavender, and apricot nectar smells. When paired with the matching fragrance mist, it can feel really calming and relaxing. Did I mention that it contains a fair amount of shea butter and vitamin E?

## 3. Sweet Cinnamon Pumpkin Gift Set

![Sweet Cinnamon Pumpkin Gift Set](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b4/dr/ai02j9o4_600x597.jpg)

Pumpkin lovers will get a kick out of this one. This set from Bath and Body Works has the perfect blend of cinnamon, pumpkin and vanilla. I seriously can't wait to test out the full line! The balance between the spicy and sweet notes makes the lotions and hand soaps included really unique. Plus, how cute is that packaging?

## 4. Coconut Milk Body Lotion

![Coconut Milk Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/zy/t9j1mtk1_600x576.jpg)

If your skin gets really dry, this is a really awesome moisturizer to have on hand. I love how rich and hydrating this lotion is; the fact that it doesn't leave a creamy residue behind makes it even better. While most people associate coconuts with summer, I think that this lotion is totally worth trying. There's a reason why the Body Shop is my go-to store for skin products.

## 5. Shea Whip Body Lotion

![Shea Whip Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pe/gl/uuj94tbk_600x537.jpg)

I could never resist something that makes my hands feel so incredibly soft and replenished. Also available at the body shop, the formula is light but highly effective. With its nutty scent, this one is perfect for all seasons!

## 6. Dark Orchid Fragrance Lotion

![Dark Orchid Fragrance Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zf/lr/m56mk847_600x743.jpg)

From the minute I read that Victoria's Secret "Dark Orchid Fragrance Lotion" contains crushed plum, red ginger orchid and jasmine notes, I was sold. As a part of the Victoria's Secret Seduction series, it certainly lives up to its name; this fragrance would be great to wear out on a date or night out with your pals.

## 7. Daisy Body Lotion

![Daisy Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ti/vj/ydet1cp6_600x873.jpg)

Lastly, fruity and floral scent lovers can get the best of both worlds with this one. It's hard not to become addicted to the strawberry, violet and gardenia notes in Marc Jacobs' masterpiece. One thing you should be aware of is that this lotion has glitter sprinkled throughout.

Equipped with these marvelous finds, there's no doubt in my mind that autumn will go out with a bang! What are some of your favorite scents to wear in the fall? Do you have a go-to scent to rock at school and on date night?
