# 7 Excellent Drugstore Sunscreens to Slather on ...

When it comes to the finding the best in sun protection for your face and body, don’t forget about drugstore sunscreens. In fact, sunscreen from the drugstore might even be more popular than higher end brands, but there are literally tons of choices of drugstore sunscreens so it can be a bit challenging to find the best one for you in a sea of sunscreen. With that in mind, I’ve rounded up 7 of the finest in drugstore sunscreens so you can focus on having fun in the sun!

## 1. Neutrogena Ultra Sheer Face + Body Stick Sunscreen Broad Spectrum SPF 70

![Neutrogena Ultra Sheer Face + Body Stick Sunscreen Broad Spectrum SPF 70](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tj/v9/bdc8yon2_320x292.jpg)

There’s no question that Neutrogena is one of the best-selling brands for drugstore sunscreens and skin care out there. In my opinion, they come pretty close to being high-end quality but without the high price tag. Take their new Ultra Sheer Face + Body Stick Sunscreen. This might look like a deodorant, but it makes applying and reapplying sunscreen a breeze. The lightweight formula makes it simple to use on your face and body and gives you all the protection you need. Available at Ulta for $9.99.

## 2. Garnier Ombrelle Sport Clear Spray

![Garnier Ombrelle Sport Clear Spray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q6/ym/k4458sfp_403x307.jpg)

This drugstore sunscreen is for all the Canadian beauties. Garnier’s Ombrelle Sport Clear Spray was developed specially for active ladies who require a water resistant and fast-drying formula to keep them safe from the sun’s harmful rays. This dry mist sunscreen will leave skin comfortable and never sticky. Available at well.ca for $21.99 CAD.

## 3. California Baby Sunscreen Super Sensitive Lotion SPF 30+, No Fragrance

![California Baby Sunscreen Super Sensitive Lotion SPF 30+, No Fragrance](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k3/s8/k84a178p_500x452.jpg)

If you’ve got little ones, it’s important to find a quality sunscreen that understands the needs of baby’s skin. California Baby created an organic and water-resistant sunscreen that the whole family can use. This award-winning product is vegan, contains no chemicals, won’t stain and is totally hypoallergenic. This is much higher priced than your average drugstore sunscreen, but if you’re in need of a natural product, this could be a winner. Available at Target for $17.99.

## 4. Hawaiian Tropic Sheer Touch Sunscreen Lotion

![Hawaiian Tropic Sheer Touch Sunscreen Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/pw/dghjodmt_410x391.jpg)

One of the things I like about sunscreens with fragrance is that no matter what time of the year you apply it, it instantly transports you to a summer day. Hawaiian Tropic’s Sheer Touch Sunscreen Lotion is not only water-resistant and goes on silky smooth, but it has this intoxicating summery beach scent that just makes you feel like you’re going on vacation. Available at Target for $7.99.

## 5. Alba Botanica Hawaiian Sunscreen, SPF 45 Revitalizing Green Tea

![Alba Botanica Hawaiian Sunscreen, SPF 45 Revitalizing Green Tea](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/fn/teylu76c_500x476.jpg)

This is another excellent natural drugstore sunscreen that’s also moisturizing and nourishing. With green tea, sunflower seed oil and aloe vera, this non-greasy sunscreen will leave your skin feeling soft, supple and revitalized. It’s great to use for daily wear or when you’re out frolicking on the beach. With effective broad spectrum UVA/UVB protection, this water resistant sunscreen will be your go-to no matter where you are. Available at Walgreens for $9.99.

## 6. Coppertone Sensitive Skin Sunscreen Lotion

![Coppertone Sensitive Skin Sunscreen Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yn/kw/v2fyitor_500x462.jpg)

Sometimes, having sensitive skin can make finding a first-rate sunscreen a little difficult, but not to worry. Many drugstore sunscreen companies are wising up and realizing that sunscreen should cater to our unique skin care needs as well. Coppertone’s Sensitive Skin Sunscreen Lotion is fragrance-free, water-resistant and free of alcohol, dyes and oils. This gentle formula delivers effective broad spectrum protection that won’t clog your pores. Available at Walmart for $7.42.

## 7. Ocean Potion Suncare anti-Aging Sunblock

![Ocean Potion Suncare anti-Aging Sunblock](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/ym/dxfn42gj_500x500.jpg)

This lightweight sunscreen was formulated specifically for fair-skinned ladies who want powerful anti-aging properties along with their sun protection. It’s non-greasy, absorbs quickly and contains antioxidants to reduce damage from free radicals so your skin stays youthful and radiant. Available at Walmart for $7.47.

Be sure to check out some of these fantastic drugstore sunscreens the next time you’re looking for new sunscreen! There are so many great sun protection products from the drugstore that are definitely worth trying out before you shell out the big bucks for the brand names.
