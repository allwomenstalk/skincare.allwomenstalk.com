# 7 Skincare Secrets from Elle MacPherson ...

She may have been before your time, but this mega supermodel is still around even if she isn’t strutting down runways anymore. She is glamorous and beautiful and you’d never be able to tell that she has passed her 50th birthday. She looks great! So, in light of her beauty and obvious handle on all things skincare, I’ve gathered some of her greatest tips so that you too can enjoy her lovely complexion. Try these tips and see how much better your skin is. Let me know how it goes!

## 1. Exfoliate Your Skin Every Single Day

Elle says she exfoliates her face once a day and the rest of her body twice a day. Maybe that’s the secret to such gorgeous skin. Exfoliation helps remove dead skin cells, residual product, dirt and bacteria from the surface of your skin as well as deep inside your pores. That means fresh, clean skin without blemishes and breakouts. Try boosting your exfoliation routine and see how it works for you.

## 2. Use a Broad Spectrum SPF 50 Sunscreen Every Day

Sunscreen is an easy way to slow the signs of aging and help keep your skin from wrinkling and getting age spots. Of course, you must wear it anytime you are outside, but did you know that you should also slather it on under your make-up every day? Think about the time you spend walking to and from the car, driving and sitting in a sunny window. Without sunscreen, your skin is being damaged in these instances so slather up daily.

## 3. Exercise Anywhere You Are, No Matter What

MacPherson never slacks on her workout schedule, even if she isn’t near home. By getting regular physical activity, you keep your circulation pumping and your weight down, both of which protect and promote healthy skin. Even if you can’t hit the gym, take a walk on your lunch break or play in the backyard with your kids.

## 4. Use a Concealer to Mask Any Spots on Your Face

Even supermodels have occasional skin issues. Elle says she uses a concealer to cover up any spots on her face. This is an easy way to keep your complexion looking fabulous for your everyday activities. She also says to go for a creamy concealer and skip the powder, which settles into fine lines and makes them more pronounced.

## 5. Try Using a Body Brush to Boost Skin Circulation

This is a really easy way to get the blood flowing through your body so that your skin looks young and fresh. Elle uses her body brush twice a day, but once is likely enough. Make sure you follow the directions for the brush you buy to get the best results. Body brushing is easy to do and is super relaxing too. You’ll probably find that you look forward to it at the end of the day.

## 6. Always Put on Moisturizer after Your Shower

You’ve probably heard this before, but if Elle is doing it, you know it’s got to work. After you towel dry, rub in a good quality skin cream or lotion to help hold the moisture inside your skin. This keeps your entire body hydrated and healthy and eliminates dry skin and tightness so you can look youthful and radiant, no matter what.

## 7. Make Healthy Eating a Normal Part of Your Day

No one stays looking as gorgeous as Elle MacPherson by eating a ton of fast food, candy and soda. Take a page from her book and overhaul your eating habits. Include plenty of variety from each food group, including dairy, protein, fruits, veggies and whole grains. This pumps your skin full of nutrients it needs to stay healthy and young.

Ever heard of Elle MacPherson? Are you Googling her right now? Which of these steps are you planning to incorporate into your routine?
