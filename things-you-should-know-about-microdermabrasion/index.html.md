# The Most Important Things to Know about Microdermabrasion ...

There are some things you should know about microdermabrasion that are handy before you undergo the procedure. The treatment involves blasting your skin with small particles that help slough away dead skin cells and refresh your skin. It’s often done to the face, but is sometimes available for other parts of your body as well. Most spas and some dermatologists will do microdermabrasion and it’s a mild treatment that you’ll recover from quickly so that you can get back to your life right away. Here are the things you should know about microdermabrasion.

## 1. Works for the Signs of Aging

No one wants to look in the mirror and see fine lines, wrinkles and age spots. However, it’s a fact of life, but that doesn’t mean you have to grin and bear it. One of the things you should know about microdermabrasion is that it can be used to keep you looking young. It will improve the discoloration caused by aging and can help combat lines too.

## 2. Treats Stretch Marks

Stretch marks are a common occurrence during pregnancy, but can also crop up with weight gain that isn’t associated with pregnancy. They are formed when the skin stretches beyond its limits. Many a mother has cursed her striations and while there is no way to prevent or cure them, microdermabrasion can help lighten stretch marks and make them a lot less noticeable.

## 3. Treats Acne

When you have acne breakouts, it can sometimes leave behind scars. While you can often cover them up with make-up, microdermabrasion can help lessen their appearance and make it easier to keep them covered up. When you undergo the procedure, it helps remove the top layers of your skin, which lets the younger, better looking skin shine through.

## 4. Thickens Collagen

Collagen is the substance in your skin that keeps it tight, fresh and radiant. As you get older, your collagen thins out, which is what causes wrinkled, droopy skin. When you have a microdermabrasion treatment, it helps keep your collagen thick and healthy. That makes your skin look strong, smooth and healthy all the time.

## 5. Uses Different Particles

There are a couple of kinds of particles that technicians use for microdermabrasion. The most common ones are crystals and diamond flakes. You might think that these rough pieces would cause tears and wounds, but they are pretty tiny and actually work to gently exfoliate your skin, removing dead skin cells and making your complexion bright and healthy.

## 6. It Doesn’t Hurt

Microdermabrasion might sound painful, but it really isn’t. Many women have it done on their lunch break, then head back to work. It can be a bit uncomfortable, but for most women it feels similar to a light exfoliation at home. If it does hurt, stop the technician and let her know. She can probably adjust so it feels better.

## 7. You Can do It at Home

Did you know that you can buy home microdermabrasion kits? They aren’t as powerful as the treatments you can have done at the office, but can do a pretty good job. This allows you to do the procedure yourself at home anytime you have a few minutes. It’ll save you money and time, and who doesn’t want that?
