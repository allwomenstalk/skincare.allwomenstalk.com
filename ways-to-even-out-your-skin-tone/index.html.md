# 5 Quick Ways to Even out Your Skin Tone for Girls Who Want Glowing Skin ...

As a woman, you are probably always on the lookout for **ways to even out your skin tone.**

You are a very lucky lady if you go through life without having some kind of battle with your facial skin. Whether its roughness, blotchiness, discolouration or dryness, your skin tone can really effect your self confidence. Many women resort to makeup to cover the issues, which is not a problem in theory, but it doesn’t address the actual problem. There are **ways to even out your skin tone** so that you don’t have to rely on heavy makeup and so you feel confident enough to go makeup free. Here are five quick ways to even out your skin tone.

## 1. Healthy Eating

There is no getting away from it, healthy eating is one of the cornerstones for pretty much everything to do with body improvement and is one of the best **ways to even out your skin tone**! Junk food is filled with ingredients that are unkind to your skin and clog your pores, which can really effect the tone and texture. Instead of unhealthy fast food and processed snacks, fill up on foods that are rich in antioxidants, like cranberries, blueberries, blackberries and even beans and potatoes. These foods will help to clear your skin and make it look much more even in colour and tone.

## 2. Moisturise

You would be shocked at just how many women ignore moisturising as part of their regular beauty routine. You should aim to moisturize twice a day, once in the morning and once at night. Look for products rich in things like coconut oil, vitamin E, shea butter and jojoba oil, as they will penetrate your skin and really get to work where you need it most.

## 3. Exfoliate

Many people’s skin problems stem from the fact that there is a build up dead skin and debris that is not being removed, and you can solve this issue with regular exfoliating. Going too hard can actually cause a negative, abrasive effect on your skin, but incorporating gentle exfoliation into your beauty routine will only help to improve the look of your skin, thanks to clearer pores and a smoother, fresher surface.

## 4. Honey Products

Honey products aren’t just a fad, they really can be helpful when it comes to improving your skin tone. Honey acts as a natural moisturiser to reverse dryness and flakiness on the surface, and it also boasts antibacterial properties that will fight blemishes, discolouration and acne scars.

## 5. Toners

A good facial toner is the perfect quick fix for solving skin tone issues, as it will help to make your complexion appear much more even. Many of the better brands also contain natural plant extracts that will work to hydrate and soften your skin at the same time.
