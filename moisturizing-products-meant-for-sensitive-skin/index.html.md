# Moisturizing Products Meant for Sensitive Skin ...

Having sensitive skin is a drag. When your sensitive skin gets dry and needs moisture, it can be even worse because you have to be so careful about the products you choose. These are 7 moisturizing products that are meant for dry skin. They’re formulated specifically with the needs of sensitive skin sufferers in mind.

## 1. Dove Deep Moisture Body Wash

![Dove Deep Moisture Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xo/v2/fv5yo2u7562aa37dd13dc401854269_600x571.jpg)

Let’s start with your choice of body wash. Dove Deep Moisture Body Wash is one that delivers the moisture your skin is craving. It’s made with gentle cleansers to help your skin stay hydrated. Dove products are a good choice for your skin with the exception of the ones that contain added fragrances in the Go Fresh line. There’s even a specific body wash for sensitive skin sufferers. Dove products are often recommended by dermatologists.

## 2. EOS Ultra Moisturizing Shaving Cream for Sensitive Skin

![EOS Ultra Moisturizing Shaving Cream for Sensitive Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/si/q6/xf0vhjsw562aa42ecaf69879741174_450x443.jpg)

This shaving cream has tons of good for your skin ingredients. Shaving creams and gels seem to be especially difficult for sensitive skin sufferers to use because so many of them are irritating. This is one that’s formulated with your sensitive skin in mind. It has aloe, oat and shea butter to soothe and moisturize your skin. Bonus: it’s in a no rust can so there’s no scrubbing later.

## 3. Almay Sensitive Skin Antiperspirant and Deodorant

![Almay Sensitive Skin Antiperspirant and Deodorant](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h6/uk/rxxtg3ze562aa466618e9057890011_450x449.jpg)

Even your underarms can cause you issues when you have sensitive skin. That’s not an issue with this choice of deodorant. It’s hypoallergenic and fragrance free. However, it still does the job of keeping odor and wetness at bay no matter how tough your workout. It also won’t dry out your underarms like other deodorants can.

## 4. Lubriderm Daily Moisture for Sensitive Skin

![Lubriderm Daily Moisture for Sensitive Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u7/aq/b7xqk2pa562aa4ac1baa1505537290_450x448.jpg)

This is where it’s at, ladies. Everyone needs a daily lotion and this is the one for you. It’s fragrance free and made with ingredients to moisturize and boost your skin, such as Vitamin B5. The moisturizing effect lasts 24 hours and doesn’t leave your skin feeling greasy, which is a big point in its favor. Lubriderm is a great product line that you can trust.

## 5. Neutrogena Oil Free Moisture for Sensitive Skin

![Neutrogena Oil Free Moisture for Sensitive Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jz/j9/mru0rqu9562aa5580128f888680573_600x572.jpg)

You need a special moisturizer for your face. This one gives you the moisture you need without causing irritation or a break out. The skin on your face can be even more sensitive than that of the rest of your body. It’s fragrance-free, alcohol-free and hypoallergenic. Neutrogena is one of the top products dermatologists recommend.

## 6. Eucerin Skin Calming Daily Moisturizing Cream

![Eucerin Skin Calming Daily Moisturizing Cream](https://img.allw.mn/content/bb/nd/we2lo9vq560bed9a1c909266442379_300x300.gif)

Many times people with sensitive skin have other skin conditions such as dermatitis or eczema. This is a product that addresses those concerns. If your skin is flaring up then this is the one you want to reach for. This product contains oatmeal, which is soothing to irritated or itchy skin. It’s also dye-free and fragrance-free.

## 7. Aquaphor Healing Ointment for Dry Cracked and Irritated Skin

![Aquaphor Healing Ointment for Dry Cracked and Irritated Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/dt/gggh1ri6562aa57351a7e558014178_450x448.jpg)

Most of us have some problem areas on our skin. Hands, elbows, knees, ankles and feet are common areas that require extra moisture and care. Aquaphor is amazing at taking care of those special areas. This product is extra moisturizing to heal those difficult areas of your skin. It’s also great for healing chapping, scrapes and burns. It’s just an all around great product that's good to have.
