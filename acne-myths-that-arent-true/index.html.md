# 7 Acne Myths That Arent True ...

Acne is annoying. We all know it. Unfortunately, we also know a few myths about it that can actually make your acne worse. According to [Teen Vogue](https://www.teenvogue.com/gallery/clearing-up-ten-acne-myths) here are a few acne myths you should stop believing:

## 1. Diet Has Nothing to do with Breakouts

![Diet Has Nothing to do with Breakouts](https://img.allw.mn/content/jz/x5/qfizub2e569cea25c4569067991243_467x350.gif)

If you've been drinking too much dairy, it could be causing acne. “If a client is eating poorly, then breakouts and cysts will continue no matter what."

## 2. You Can Never Wash Your Face Too Much

![You Can Never Wash Your Face Too Much](https://img.allw.mn/content/ca/wh/jz8cvssg569cea25f1dd7739689900_450x274.gif)

You shouldn't actually wash your face more than twice per day. Only wash in the morning, and then wash off your makeup before you go to bed.

## 3. You Should Put Toothpaste on a Pimple to Clear It up

![You Should Put Toothpaste on a Pimple to Clear It up](https://img.allw.mn/content/mo/bf/hw69np0t569cea30f2eed098883652_500x245.gif)

This can actually irritate your skin. There are other homemade remedies, like using apple cider vinegar, that'll do the trick instead.

## 4. If You Can’t Get to a Derm, Just Pop Your Pimples Yourself

![If You Can’t Get to a Derm, Just Pop Your Pimples Yourself](https://img.allw.mn/content/m4/yz/y2ns73fj569cea26c1a97305213537_300x216.gif)

You're not actually meant to pop your pimples. They'll go away, eventually. Besides, it's better to have a smooth bump on your skin than a bleeding scab, don't you think?

## 5. You Should Lay out in the Sun to Clear up Your Acne

![You Should Lay out in the Sun to Clear up Your Acne](https://img.allw.mn/content/y8/fa/cyqqi6gs569cea30660f5293878691_416x312.gif)

## 6. It’s Impossible to Fade Acne Scarring

![It’s Impossible to Fade Acne Scarring](https://img.allw.mn/content/h8/qd/fd503ige569cea26b7551862335393_500x220.gif)

You can try wearing SPF. You can try "a product that has glycolic and lactic acid." Or you could try laser treatments and peels.

## 7. Exfoliate as Much as Possible

![Exfoliate as Much as Possible](https://img.allw.mn/content/cn/ky/fc5fcnox569cea26e50d3196676160_500x227.gif)

Only exfoliate two or three times per week. You don't want to go overboard with it, just like you don't want to go overboard when washing your face.
