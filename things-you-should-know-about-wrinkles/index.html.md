# Genius Things to Know about Wrinkles ...

There are lots **things you should know about wrinkles**. Fine lines and wrinkles are worrisome for women of all ages. They exist in the dermis and take years to work their way up to the epidermis. No matter how smooth and plump your skin looks, there may be wrinkles forming in your dermis. Here's are the most important **things you should know about wrinkles**.

## 1. Sunlight

Sunlight is great for photosynthesis and Vitamin D, but spending long hours in the sun damages your skin’s DNA. The sun is responsible for 90% of the skin’s aging and cell damage. DNA or cell damage has several consequences, from aging signs like wrinkles to melasma, to skin cancer. This is definitely one of the most important **things you should know about wrinkles**.

## 2. Muscle Movements in the Face

We all have the same number of muscles in our face, which aids our smile, frown, and speech. Wrinkles form from repeated actions by the muscles around our eyes, mouth, cheeks, jaws, and neckline.

## 3. Genetics, Collagen, and Elastin

Your DNA determines the strength of your skin’s protein - both collagen and elastin. Youthful collagen and elastin restoration is the objective of anti-aging treatments.

## 5. Preventative Care - Hyaluronic Acid

Younger skin has great collagen and elastin, so boosting an already great collagen matrix with hyaluronic acid gives superb results.

## 6. Preventative Care – Physical Block

Look for natural sunscreens containing active ingredients of 23-25% zinc oxide and provides broad spectrum protection.

## 7. Preventative Care – Peptides

Peptides are amino acid or proteins, which remold and repair the skin’s damaged proteins - collagen and elastin. Choose products with peptides for preventative and future wrinkle care.
