# 5 Easy Steps to Get a Healthy Glow to Your Skin ...

Are you looking for some **natural ways to get a healthy glow**? Why rely on expensive💱 salon treatments when you can get the same results with natural ingredients. Here are 5 **natural ways to get a healthy glow**.

## 1. MORNING FACE MASK

Take a half banana, 🍌 cut 🔪it into cubes and smash it to create a fine paste. Add 1tbsp of milk🥛🥄and mix together until a smooth paste is formed. Gently apply on your face and neck with help of a brush. Leave it for about 15 minutes. Then wash off with cool water. Do this for feeling fresh. You will immediately feel the difference as your face will feel soft and well hydrated. This is one of the best **natural ways to get a healthy glow**.

## 2. RICE WATER TONER

Take a glass of water and add 1\2 cup of rice to it. Let the rice soak for 30 minutes 🕞. Strain the water and put it in a spray bottle. Your RICE WATER TONER is ready for use.

Spray 💦some water on your face and massage for a couple of minutes. Let your face dry. With regular use, you will notice skin feeling soft and tight. Use it as part of your skin routine.

## 3. POTATO ICE CUBES

Take a potato peel and grate it. Put grated potato in a mixer, add 1\2 cup pomegranate seeds and 1 tbsp of water. Blend the mixture into juice and squeeze 1\2 a lemon into it. Pour the mixture into an ice cube tray and fill each section. Freeze it. Once frozen remove from freezer. They are ready to use.

Massage using 2 or 3 cubes on your face in a circular motion. It will not only remove dark spots but also helps to minimize large open pores. It also improves blood circulation.

## 4. CUCUMBER 🥒TONER

Take a fresh cucumber and cut into small pieces. Put them in a mixer jar and blend it into a juice. Strain the juice, squeeze in lemon juice🍋 and 2 or 3 tbsp of rose water. Mix them together and transfer into a spray bottle. The toner is ready to use. Keep it in the refrigerator. You can use as many times you want.

Spray it on your face, neck hands and legs. Let it dry naturally. Prevent from getting into eyes 👀as it contains lemon juice which can cause irritation.

## 5. APPLY a MOISTURIZER

Rinse your face with water after 10 minutes of using cucumber toner. Then apply a good moisturizer and leave it overnight.🌜

Do all the steps for 5 days and enjoy the difference.👍
