# How to Get Skin so Gorgeous You Won't Need Foundation ...

There are loads of answers for **how to get gorgeous skin**. Of all the different elements and industries that come together to make up the entire health and beauty universe, it would be fair to say that skincare is something that the majority of the world’s population is concerned with to at least some degree. You might not wear much eye or lip makeup, but that does not mean that you don’t want your skin to look as clear and healthy as possible. One way to provide an easy but temporary fix to the look of your skin is to use a lot of foundation, but there are certain things that you can in order to help your skin in a more natural way. Here's **how to get gorgeous skin** so you don't even need foundation.

## 1. Drink Water

Drinking plenty of water is one of the best answers for **how to get gorgeous skin**. Staying hydrated is one of the very best things that you can do to keep your skin healthy and glowing. More than 60% of our bodies are water, and that means that the first place dehydration becomes noticeable is on the skin! Try to drink two litres of water every single day.

## 2. Superfoods

It can help your skin enormously to eat a lot of so-called ‘superfoods’ that are rich in antioxidants. Things like spinach, almonds, blueberries, lemons and even fish are all great for your skin, as well any green supplement if you want to find a little ‘cheat’ way to get those antioxidants into your body!

## 3. Know Your Skin

In order to get your skin looking its best, it's important to first know exactly what you are dealing with. Get to know your specific skin type in order to know how to treat it. Is your skin dry? Flaky? Oily? Each different type has numerous skin care products that can help to improve it.

## 4. Exfoliate

Exfoliating is a must for anyone who is looking for the healthiest, most glowing skin. If you don’t exfoliate, the layers of skin and debris that accumulate over the course of a normal day can begin to clog up your pores and work to encourage acne and other breakouts. The more you exfoliate to remove the dead layers and debris, the more even your skin tone will start to become.

## 5. Stick to the Same Routine

Your skin will flourish best if you commit to undertaking an identical and regular routine. The more strict and more consistent you keep your skincare regime, the more your skin will benefit from regular care. Don’t mix and match your products too often. Once you find a set of products that seem to work for you personally, don’t try to be too adventurous and try new brands too often. Skincare isn’t always about trying the newest products to see what fits, it’s about finding what is best for your own skin and sticking to it for the best results.
