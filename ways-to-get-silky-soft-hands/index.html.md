# Check out These Ways to Get Silky Soft Hands ...

Every girl needs to know some **ways to get silky soft hands**. Every season needs protection. In winter hands look dull, yellow, black and older than your age. In the summer hands get dry. It's also said that if you want to find someone's true age just look at their hands.

If your hands look dull and dry, these perfect **ways to get silky soft hands** are for you.

## 1. Water and Lemon

Fill a bowl with warm water. Add lemon juice. Put your hands in and rub them with lemon residue. Your hands will be soft clean because this combination removes dead skin naturally and lightens skin. Keep your hands in the water for 5 minutes in water. But if you have any cuts or injuries on your hands, don't use this method.

## 2. NATURAL CREAM

1 tbsp mustard oil
1 tbsp milk or cream
Lemon

Take an empty bowl and squeeze 1 tbsp lemon juice then and add the other ingredients. Mix them well. Apply to your hands. Rub for 2-3 minutes. When it feels dry, put on a pair of cotton gloves. If possible leave it overnight and wash in the morning. If you can't wait, 1 hour is OK. Lemon removes blackness very quickly from hands, feet, elbows and knees. Milk is very useful for dryness and makes hands smooth and soft. It also heals the skin if cracked. Mustard oil has antibacterial and antifungal properties. It also has tons of vitamins A, B complex and E, which make skin young. Mustard oil is a natural sunscreen also.

## 3. GLYCERIN and ROSEWATER

Mix glycerin and rose water, keep in a bottle. Apply this mixture to your hands two or three times a day. You will be surprised to see your hands because it gives amazing and quick results. Use regularly for the best results.
