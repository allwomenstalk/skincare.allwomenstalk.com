# 7 of the Best Facial Sunscreens for Sensitive Skin ...

As anyone with fair skin knows, sometimes you need a little extra care during the summer months, and that's where sunscreens for sensitive skin come into play! I flush easily and suffer from mild rosacea, but normal sunscreens often break me out, so finding a facial sunscreen that offers high SPF and is kind to sensitive skin is important to me! I thought I would share with you a few of my favorite sunscreens for sensitive skin - try them and see what works for you!

## 1. La Roche-Posay Anthelios XL SPF50+ Melt in Cream

![La Roche-Posay Anthelios XL SPF50+ Melt in Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/hd/jo8oknjc_300x296.jpg)

This has to be one of the best sunscreens for sensitive skin, and it's designed for facial use, so it won't leave your skin greasy or break you out. Offering very high protection against UVA and UVB rays, this is ideal for fair skins that burn easily - it's also water resistant and melts instantly into skin, so it can be used as a base for makeup. There's even a tinted version available if you want to wear it as a tinted moisturizer!

## 2. Nivea Sensitive Day Cream SPF15

![Nivea Sensitive Day Cream SPF15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u2/s3/vi9o6fwo_388x363.jpg)

For an everyday moisturizer with sunscreen, this SPF15 cream is ideal. It's designed to work with sensitive skin and contains liquorice to fight redness and irritation. Speaking as someone whose skin is easily irritated by most moisturizers, I have to say this is a wonder product! It does take a while longer to sink into skin than some other creams, but apply it first thing and brush your teeth or get dressed whilst it is absorbed and skin is left soft and protected from the sun. It's also a great makeup base.

## 3. Clinique Super City Block SPF40

![Clinique Super City Block SPF40](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gn/ja/urwn182p_450x448.jpg)

For high level protection everyday, this is perfect. Its weightless formula means it sinks in quickly and it makes a great base for makeup. It provides SPF40 protection and antioxidants to protect against premature aging. It's also 100% fragrance free, so it's ideal for sensitive skin!

## 4. Dermalogica Protection 50 Sport

![Dermalogica Protection 50 Sport](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ye/hx/ps2xzq5a_600x549.jpg)

Another SPF50 cream that's ideal for fair, sensitive skin, this sheer formula feels light and non-sticky on your face, which means it can be worn alone or as a base for makeup. It contains special microspheres, which help the SPF to work and ensure your skin stays moisturised. Lovely!

## 5. BareMinerals SPF30 Sunscreen

![BareMinerals SPF30 Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ts/zx/qtmlwiw5_400x365.jpg)

For a non-traditional approach to sunscreen, why not try this makeup/sunscreen hybrid? Worn like your traditional bareMinerals powder foundation, this lightweight, sheer mineral powder contains SPF30 to protect even the most sensitive skin from sun damage and premature aging. This has to be one of my favorites for the summer months, although you may need to wear a light moisturizer beneath it if you have dry skin like me!

## 6. Shiseido Ultimate Sun Protection Cream SPF55

![Shiseido Ultimate Sun Protection Cream SPF55](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ft/d3/omfg9ry6_250x248.jpg)

If you're planning an active summer, you'll need a sunscreen that holds its own. This waterproof formula is heavy duty yet light enough to be worn under makeup. Its light scent makes it ideal for sensitive skin - but it comes at a price!

## 7. Milk by Michael Klim Face Moisturizer and Sunscreen SPF 15

![Milk by Michael Klim Face Moisturizer and Sunscreen SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ul/m0/fkk3rh1r_313x320.jpg)

It's true that I was initially attracted to this sunscreen for its cool, minimalist white packaging, but it does a good job of protecting my sensitive skin from the London sunshine! Rich in vitamins A, B and E, it has a fresh aroma of mint and rosemary that leaves skin feeling moisturized and protected. A top buy this summer and easy on your wallet too!

These are my 7 favorite sunscreens for sensitive skin - maybe you have your own tried-and-tested favorites? Why not share them with me? I'd love to hear your ideas!
