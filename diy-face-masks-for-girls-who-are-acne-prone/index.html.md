# DIY Facemasks for Girls with Acne Prone Skin ...

Having acne prone skin can be a real bummer. It’s hard to feel good about the way you look when you have a breakout all over face. Fortunately, there are many things you can do at home to help fight acne and leave your skin looking smooth and clean every day. One such thing is to apply a face mask that is designed for acne prone skin. You can certainly buy one at the drugstore, but there are also several DIY masks that you can whip up anytime you need one.

## 1. Use Cinnamon and Honey

Both cinnamon and honey are great for so much more than just eating. Sure, they taste great, but both of these foods also have great benefits for your skin. Combining them helps battle acne and give you the clear complexion you crave.

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions:

Combine the two ingredients and smear the mixture all over your face. Leave in place for 15 minutes, then rinse.

Source: [enkivillage.com](https://www.enkiverywell.com/diy-face-mask-for-acne.html)

## 2. Try Baking Soda

Baking soda helps draw out the bacteria and junk that clogs your pores and leads to acne. This simple mask is one you can use as often as you need it. Get ready to see the results you want.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon baking soda
1 tablespoon water

Directions:

Combine the water and baking soda to form a thick paste. Spread it on your face and allow it to sit for about 20 minutes before rinsing with warm water.

Source: [enkivillage.com](https://www.enkiverywell.com/diy-face-mask-for-acne.html)

## 3. How about Apple Cider Vinegar?

Apple cider vinegar has mega benefits for your skin. Not only can it tone and exfoliate your skin, but it also helps battle acne. Try this easy mask for mega benefits.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
3 tablespoons water

Directions:

Mix the vinegar and water and splash the mixture on your skin or rub it on with a cotton ball. Wait 10 minutes, then wash your face. You can do this a couple of times per day for stubborn pimples.

Source: [everydayroots.com](http://everydayroots.com/acne-remedies)

## 4. Egg Whites Are Perfect

Eggs are good for so much more than breakfast. The whites have great skin benefits and are the perfect choice if you want to get rid of your acne once and for all.

Ingredients:

3 egg whites

Directions:

Whisk the egg whites until they are frothy. Apply several thin layers of the whites, allowing each to dry in between. Wait 20 minutes after the final layer, then rinse.

Source: [everydayroots.com](http://everydayroots.com/acne-remedies)

## 5. Apply Tea Tree Oil

Tea tree oil can be found at health food stores and can do wonders for your skin too. It helps draw out impurities and keep your pores clean.

Ingredients:

5 drops tea tree oil
40 drops witch hazel

Directions:

Combine the oils, then apply them to your face with a cotton ball. Repeat twice per day.

Source: [rd.com](https://www.thehealthy.com/skin-health/acne/home-remedies-for-acne/)

## 6. Try Green Tea Bags

You’ve probably heard of the wonder that is green tea. It’s packed with antioxidants, which makes it an ideal choice or treating skin issues. This mask is super easy and totally effective.

Ingredients:

2 cooled green tea bags

Directions:

Lay two cooled green bags on your acne prone skin. Allow them to sit for several minutes before discarding. Repeat at necessary.

Source: [rd.com](https://www.thehealthy.com/skin-health/acne/home-remedies-for-acne/)

## 7. Mint is Great for Unclogging Pores

If you want to unclog acne prone pores, mint is the perfect choice. It has properties that help keep skin clear and fresh.

Ingredients:

2 tablespoons plain yogurt
2 tablespoons oatmeal powder
2 tablespoons finely minced mint leaves

Directions:

Combine the ingredients well to form a paste. Slather the mixture over your skin and allow it to work for 10 minutes. Wash well.

Source: [rd.com](https://www.thehealthy.com/skin-health/acne/home-remedies-for-acne/)

## 8. You'll Love Milk and Honey

Both milk and honey have nutrients in them that your skin needs, but they also soothe irritated skin and reduce redness caused by acne.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon whole milk
1 tablespoon raw honey

Directions:

Allow the milk to come to room temperature, then combine it with the honey. Spread the mixture on your face and let it sit for 10 to 15 minutes. Use a wet washcloth to gently remove the mask and exfoliate your skin at the same time.

Source: [everydayroots.com](https://everydayroots.com/acne-remedies)

## 9. Brew up Some Echinacea

Echinacea has powerful anti-inflammatory properties, which makes it a valuable remedy for battling acne. It's really easy to use and super effective too.

Ingredients:

Brewed echinacea tea

Directions:

Soak a cotton ball in the tea and gently apply it to your face. You can also spot treat blemishes this way. Let the tea dry, then rinse with warm water.

Source: [rd.com](https://www.thehealthy.com/skin-health/acne/home-remedies-for-acne/)

## 10. Peel a Papaya

Papaya is delicious to eat, but it's also the perfect ingredient for a face mask to treat acne. That's because it removes dead skin cells and soak up excess oil. It smells really good too!

Ingredients:

1 fresh papaya

Directions:

Mash the flesh of the papaya into a paste like consistency. Spread it on your face and wait for 10 to 15 minutes. Then gently rinse with warm water. Moisturize once your skin dries.

Source: [everydayroots.com](https://everydayroots.com/acne-remedies)
