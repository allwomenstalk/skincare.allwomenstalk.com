# Bad College Habits That Ruin Your Skin ...

College is a time in your life where you don’t always make the best decisions and live the healthiest lifestyle! You’re studying hard and partying hard at the same time, which can often lead to developing more than a few bad habits that can be detrimental in a number of different ways. Specifically, I’m talking about your skin. Your skin is something that needs a lot of care and attention in order to stay healthy, so college life doesn’t always agree with it! Here are some **bad college habits that ruin your skin**.

## 1. Insufficient Sleep

Not getting enough sleep can really wreak havoc on your skin. Being a student who is both up all night partying at weekends and up all night studying on weekdays can encourage the buildup of oils and residue that can turn in to acne. Taking care of your skin and getting good sleep every night is a must.

## 2. Stress

Exams and deadlines can be stressful, as can the newfound social life of being a college student. Unfortunately, stress is something that can cause skin outbreaks, so you want to do as much as you can to eliminate unnecessary anxieties and worries from your daily routine. Practising mindfulness can be a good place to start.

## 3. Diet

College students can very easily fall in to a diet of mainly processed and junk foods, but all of those unhealthy fats and oils will show directly on your skin after a while. Try not to have microwave pizza every night in your dorm room, because your body will be screaming out for some fresh nutrients!

## 4. Alcohol

You might drink a lot when you are at college, that’s no secret. What you need to look out for though is the redness and look of premature ageing that can often come from too much alcohol. Try to limit your consumption to appropriate levels if you want to preserve the health of your skin. You don’t have to be wasted every single weekend!
