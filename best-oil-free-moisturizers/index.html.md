# 10 Best Oil\-Free Moisturizers ...

Oil-free moisturizers can be a god-send, especially if you have sensitive or acne-prone skin. You want hydration, but not a break-put or a rash, right? That’s why having a good oil-free moisturizer is important. But there are so many to choose from… how will you know which work, and which are a waste, without spending a small fortune? I can help. Here are my picks for the 10 best oil-free moisturizers, some with a tint, some without.

## 10. Korres Watermelon Lightweight Tinted Moisturiser SPF 30

![Korres Watermelon Lightweight Tinted Moisturiser SPF 30](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xz/u4/zysrvagb54c9317f793a8732203892_250x248.jpg)
