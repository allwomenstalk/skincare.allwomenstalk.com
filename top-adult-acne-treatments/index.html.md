# 7 Top Adult Acne Treatments ...

Adult acne treatments are a must-have if you’re currently battling wrinkles and acne, like me. I’ve often complained about how unfair it is that I have to worry about both at the same time, but rather than just whine about it, I’ve been sampling as many adult acne treatments as I can get my hands on, so now I know which products to use, and which to avoid in the future. Want to know more? Keep reading! Here are the 7 top adult acne treatments.

## 2. Murad Acne Spot Treatment

![Murad Acne Spot Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/zb/jb9bff2a54c93151a6899448873030_250x232.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is another of my must-have adult acne treatments. Simple apply it to a problem area one to three times a day, and watch as the blemishes, literally, disappear. If you have dry or sensitive skin, you might want to just use it once. But if you have a tough hide, like me, feel free to use it all three times a day, without worry.

![Murad anti-Aging Moisturizer SPF 20 PA++](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w3/cb/aqj1qw0k54c93152490fd112611677_250x240.jpg)

If you’re worried about aging, you want a moisturizer with SPF to protect your skin from the aging effects of the sun. But if you have acne-prone skin, you’ll want a greaseless, fast-absorbing moisturizer. This is both! I apply it in a thin, even later after the Acne & Wrinkle reducer. I love it!

## 4. Murad Time Release Acne Cleanser

![Murad Time Release Acne Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qh/nr/zlrqu34m54c93152efef4222791549_250x247.jpg)

**Price:** $30.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I use this adult acne treatment every morning in the shower, and it keeps my skin in acne-fighting mode for hours, long after it’s been rinsed away. It’s a thick cream, one that doesn’t lather, so don’t be surprised when you use it the first time. It’s supposed to be like that!

## 5. Murad Post-Acne Spot Lightening Gel

![Murad Post-Acne Spot Lightening Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mz/kg/lye9ifjn54c931539ade8112375453_250x242.jpg)

**Price:** $60.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Sometimes, after a blemish fades, there’s a discoloration, called “hyper-pigmentation,” that can last for weeks. These dark spots look and act like sun spots, or age spots, so I use this lightening gel to combat all three.

## 6. Murad Oil-Control Mattifier SPF 15

![Murad Oil-Control Mattifier SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xb/gn/fnevfvhi54c931544dd8d628223992_250x237.jpg)

Looking for an ultra-smooth airbrushed look? Then this oil-control product might be made just for you. It absorbs oil, giving you a matte, porcelain finish. I use this, and then I don’t even need foundation!

Available in two shades, this concealer is one of my favorite multi-tasking secret weapons against adult acne. While it fights behind the scenes to treat and prevent acne, it also conceals current breakouts. So useful!
