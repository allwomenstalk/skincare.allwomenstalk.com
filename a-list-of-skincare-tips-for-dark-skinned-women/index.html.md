# Dark Skinned Women Rejoice over These Skincare Tips ...

For all you dark-skinned beauties out there, where are your skincare tips? I scoured the internet and collected the very best skincare tips out there so you can have them all in one place. So, take a look and let me know what skincare tips you ladies have that have worked.

## 1. Do Not Pick Your Pimples!

![Do Not Pick Your Pimples!](https://img.allw.mn/content/k3/ex/cwyn6jty56ac5639c77df165158565_500x375.gif)

Girls, dark skin is more prone to acne and pimple scars, keep your beautiful skin glowing and leave those pimples alone!

## 2. Moisturize – Seriously, Moisturize

![Moisturize – Seriously, Moisturize](https://img.allw.mn/content/di/vd/lth22p1t56ac563b8a4b3824593518_500x282.gif)

Typically, dark skinned girls know, your skin can look a little bit ashy at times, if you don't keep it hydrated and moisturized properly. Lotion it up ladies!

## 3. Sunscreen is Your Friend

![Sunscreen is Your Friend](https://img.allw.mn/content/eo/h1/ic01xbnh56ac563d5332c953822965_500x230.gif)

Seriously. Don't forget it! Just because you are a dark skinned beauty doesn't mean the sun can't mess with that beautiful skin.

## 4. Skin Toner? Yep!

![Skin Toner? Yep!](https://img.allw.mn/content/f0/mc/ei5mzsto56ac563c8e7b2206217280_483x262.gif)

You'll need one, it'll keep your skin clear and clean from pimples and zits.

## 5. Face Masks Weekly Are a Must

![Face Masks Weekly Are a Must](https://img.allw.mn/content/y9/tl/zj6qducv56ac563c48633220941452_500x281.gif)

Lush has some fantastic options for you to choose from or you can make your own with honey and one egg!

## 6. Watch out for Ingrown Hairs

![Watch out for Ingrown Hairs](https://img.allw.mn/content/p8/lj/qeqk7b9056ac563d30687551725810_500x283.gif)

Oh yes, in darker skinned beauties, you are more prone to ingrown hairs, so just be careful!

## 7. Exfoliating is VERY Important

![Exfoliating is VERY Important](https://img.allw.mn/content/zu/fd/x7rgjn2n56ac563d97e1e245885751_500x281.gif)

Dark skinned ladies are more prone to scarring, which can make your skin look uneven. Exfoliating is going to make your skin look smooth and fresh!

## 8. Don't Fall into the Trap of Products Made for Dark Skin

![Don't Fall into the Trap of Products Made for Dark Skin](https://img.allw.mn/content/t6/r6/jwqtd32u56ac563de1e98696261154_500x323.gif)

If you have oily skin, why not use a product made for oily skin? Skin is skin ladies! Keep that in mind when you're in the store.

## 9. Look for Tone Correcting Moisturizer

![Look for Tone Correcting Moisturizer](https://img.allw.mn/content/ij/v7/s5ba544e56ac563dd7a2a151022760_500x368.gif)

Trust me on this, if you use it at night? It'll help hydrate your skin beautifully!

## 10. Cream Based Cleanser Works Better

![Cream Based Cleanser Works Better](https://img.allw.mn/content/km/ct/fn2m4eoz56ac563dc20e2616634535_468x304.gif)

Why? Because they moisturize quite a bit while cleaning up all the oil and grit and grime from your skin.

## 11. Only Cleanse Once a Day

![Only Cleanse Once a Day](https://img.allw.mn/content/tf/d3/oz29ssr756ac563f570a7169589915_500x281.gif)

Seriously. You don't want to give your skin a chalky appearance and cleansing more than that, it can happen.

## 12. Cocoa Butter is God!

![Cocoa Butter is God!](https://img.allw.mn/content/hj/m1/l7u0rbh356ac563f159c8093025283_500x281.gif)

I wouldn't say that you should use cocoa butter on your face, but on the rest of your body? Elbows and knees? Perfect!

## 13. Mineral Makeup in the Summer is Awesome!

![Mineral Makeup in the Summer is Awesome!](https://img.allw.mn/content/qg/wk/hlm1m85z56ac563f7d231757656620_490x262.gif)

It's light, it's airy and it won't weigh your skin down at all.

## 14. Skip the Hot, Hot, Hot Showers

![Skip the Hot, Hot, Hot Showers](https://img.allw.mn/content/c9/t1/bd7yxhqu56ac563f71f16907139625_477x268.gif)

Why? It'll cause more dry skin. Switch it to a lukewarm shower and your skin will thank you!

## 15. Use Oil-free Moisturizers

![Use Oil-free Moisturizers](https://img.allw.mn/content/uq/q2/vsehfvh456ac56404c915951685572_500x360.gif)

This is especially true if you have oily skin. It'll help eliminate the clogged pores and breakouts that could happen.

## 16. Hydroquinone is Your Friend

![Hydroquinone is Your Friend](https://img.allw.mn/content/pl/x1/iq367bs756ac5640653ef935577853_500x247.gif)

Anything with hydroquinone is going to help your skin glow and look beautiful!

## 17. Look for Brightening Products

![Look for Brightening Products](https://img.allw.mn/content/pi/jx/kagjnn3h56ac56416ee5e508543038_479x319.gif)

Why? Because all natural brighteners won't damage your skin, but will help to regulate pigmentation of it.
