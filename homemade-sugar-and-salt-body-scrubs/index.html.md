# 7 Homemade Sugar and Salt Body Scrubs for Glowing Skin ...

**Homemade sugar and salt body scrubs** are all the rage right now. You've got to give them a try!

It's that time of year! Is your skin dry and dull, yet? Rejuvenate with these delicious body scrubs and discover glowing, radiant skin! These DIY scrubs are super easy to make, and smell scrumptious! Most of them can be made with what is in your pantry.

If you don't plan to use all of your scrub right away, store the unused product in a container that seals well. Be sure to clean your spoon or scoop and containers when finished to prevent bacteria growth in your delightful new scrub. These also make great gifts for pampering loved ones! Just add a cute bow and tag and VOILA! Here are my favorite **homemade sugar and salt body scrubs.**
