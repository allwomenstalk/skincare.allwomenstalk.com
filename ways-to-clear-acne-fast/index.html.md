# Perfect Ways to Clear Acne Fast  ...

Do you want to know some **ways to clear acne fast**?

In order to have healthier and clearer skin, you should know your skin type and the cause of your acne. Acne refers to the spots, blemishes and pus-filled zits that appear commonly on the face, neck and back of the affected person. This skin disorder is normally seen in teenagers, though adults can also get pimples.

Acne occurs when the sebaceous glands of your skin release excess amounts of oil that traps bacteria and dirt, thus causing skin inflammation. When acne is not treated correctly and timely, it leaves unsightly scars on the affected area.

Anyone who has experienced acne would do anything to get back their clear skin. The secret to curing acne and skin spots quickly is by taking good care of your skin. To clear your skin of acne, you should first adopt ways to eliminate bacteria that causes acne. If you are wondering [how to get rid of pimples fast](https://www.leanhealthyandwise.com/how-to-get-rid-of-pimples-acne-overnight/), try these tested and tried **ways to clear acne fast**.

## 1. Cleanse Your Face Regularly

Use pure water and a gentle cleanser to wash your face, in order to remove oil and germs twice a day. This will prevent the accumulation of bacteria and oil onto your skin and is one of the best **ways to clear acne fast**.

## 2. Keep Your Hands off Your Face

Do not touch your facial skin with dirty hands. When you touch your skin, you are unknowingly transferring bacteria and dirt, and this triggers acne.

## 3. Go for Holistic Methods

It is best to opt for natural treatments, instead of over-the-counter medications to cure acne. Tea tree oil works effectively for acne since it has potent antibacterial, antiseptic and antifungal properties. Tea tree oil heals and rejuvenates the skin, and helps to bring its natural look back. You just need to apply a few drops of tea tree oil on the affected areas of your skin twice daily to see noticeable results.

## 4. Don’t Touch Your Zits

Most people impatiently squeeze their zits and worsen the problem. When you pinch your zits, you drive dirt and bacteria deeper into the skin.

## 5. Stay Hydrated

Water is a universal solvent that has the capacity to flush out the toxins from your system. Drink lots of water and juices to help cleanse your gut, as well as your skin.

## 6. Make Healthy Changes to Your Diet

Avoid eating oily, sugary and fatty foods, as they disturb the healthy flora of your gut, and gives rise to various ailments such as acne. Include green vegetables, fiber-rich foods, vitamins and minerals in your diet to keep your skin glowing and healthy.

## 7. Shake Your Body

One of the common causes of acne is clogged pores. When you work out, your skin sweats and unclogs the pores, thus releasing dirt and grime. This prevents the outbreak of acne and the formation of scars.

## 8. Calm Your Nerves

When you are stressed, your skin gets irritated and produces acne and pimples. If you are frazzled, find ways to relax and calm yourself. Embrace yogic breathing techniques, go for a long walk, spend time with your loved ones and meditate to unclutter your mind from disturbing thoughts.

Most people do not know how to get rid of acne overnight, and they also get perplexed because of the vast amount of information available on the internet about acne treatment. The tips mentioned in the article are simple, yet effective for treating acne and preventing future breakouts.
