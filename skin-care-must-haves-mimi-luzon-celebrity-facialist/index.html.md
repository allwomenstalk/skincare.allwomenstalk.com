## 5. The Cream That Understands Hydration - Fresh Face Hyaluronic Super Cream

Hydration is often discussed as though it were a simple matter of adding moisture. In reality, it is far more complex - a delicate balance of retention, replenishment, and barrier support.

[The Fresh Face Hyaluronic Super Cream](https://www.fwrd.com/mobile/product-mimi-luzon-fresh-face-hyaluronic-super-cream/MZOF-WU2/) approaches this balance with quiet sophistication.

Light yet deeply nourishing, it delivers hydration in layers, ensuring that the skin feels both immediately comforted and continuously supported. Hyaluronic acid draws moisture into the skin, while squalane, jojoba oil, and apricot oil work to seal it in, preventing transepidermal water loss.

The texture is particularly noteworthy - soft, smooth, and effortlessly elegant. It glides onto the skin, absorbing without residue, leaving behind a finish that is supple rather than slick.
