## 1. Mask for Oily and Combination Skin

![eyebrow, face, hair, nose, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mu/e2/ta43ezs8590cd874a522d563988716_600x600.jpg)

Here’s what really sucks about oily skin – some girls also suffer from dryness. This mask can address both issues and will help soak up the shine on your face. It’s easy to make and will have a positive impact on your face.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon cucumber juice
1 egg white
Lemon juice
½ teaspoon clay

Directions:

Combine the ingredients to create a paste. Massage into your face and allow to sit for 15 minutes before rinsing with warm water.

## 2. Banana Face Mask

![hair, face, person, black hair, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k7/ke/nf5zrp0a590cd88eac512757067580_600x750.jpg)

This face mask is great because you probably have the ingredients in your kitchen already and it reduces oil while also moisturizing and soothing your skin. Once you use this mask a few times, you should start seeing a real difference in your skin.

Ingredients:

1 banana, mashed
1 tablespoon honey
Juice from one orange

Directions:

Add the honey to the mashed banana and mix well. Add a few drops of fresh orange juice and stir to combine. Spread the mixture on your face and leave it for 15 minutes, then rinse with lukewarm water.

Source: [naturalbeautytips.co](https://naturalbeautytips.co/best-homemade-face-masks-for-oily-skin/)

## 3. Clay and Witch Hazel Mask

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oa/pn/lsg78enq590cd89f60d17520526405_600x749.jpg)

Clay is one of your best bets for soaking up grease on your face so it makes sense that it should be in your masks. This one is sure to give you the results you want after just a few uses.

Ingredients:

1 tablespoon witch hazel
1 teaspoon bentonite clay
2 drops lemon oil

Directions:

Use your fingers to combine the ingredients into a paste. Apply them to your face and wait 20 minutes before rinsing with warm water.

Source: [helloglow.co](https://helloglow.co/oily-skin-masks/)

## 5. Milk and Lemon Mask

![hair, face, black hair, eyebrow, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/mc/uz8sr7rm590cd8dd0efc4779055906_600x600.jpg)

This mask is perfect for oily skin because the ingredients are oil-free. They’ll help break up grease in your pores and leave you with a lovely complexion that you will love showing off to the world.

Ingredients:

½ cup milk
Few drops lemon juice

Directions:

Combine the milk and lemon juice and splash the mixture on your face. Allow it to dry and rinse with warm water.

## 7. Simple Salt Mask

![face, eyebrow, cheek, nose, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ox/vn/w7kz9qis590cd94404f33312666377_600x600.jpg)

This mask is super easy to make because you have everything you need right at home. It’s safe to use a couple of times per day and will help soak up grease and get it off your skin for a perfect complexion.

Ingredients:

Water
Salt

Directions:

Fill a spray bottle with lukewarm water. Dissolve salt into the water and spritz it gently on your face. Allow to dry, then rinse.
