# Top 10 Skin Care Products for New Moms and Babies ...

If you thought skin care products for moms and babies are limited to just baby lotion and powder, think again! There are lots of awesome skin care products that are perfect for baby’s delicate skin and a real treat for moms! So whether you’re a new mom or know someone whose skin could use some pampering, keep reading for all the details about the best skin products for mom and baby!

## 1. Aveeno Baby Eczema Therapy Moisturizing Cream

![Aveeno Baby Eczema Therapy Moisturizing Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/1_aveeno-baby-eczema-therapy-moisturizing-cream_300x300.jpg)

A great skin care product for moms and babies with eczema is Aveeno’s gentle and soothing cream. Aveeno is known for their natural skin care formulas, which are very gentle. Their moisturizing cream with oatmeal will calm and soothe eczema and dry skin on your precious little bundle of joy!

## 2. Mustela Post-Partum Body Restructuring Gel

![Mustela Post-Partum Body Restructuring Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/2_mustela-post-partum-body-restructuring-gel_250x250.jpg)

While we want to do our best to protect baby’s sensitive skin, we don’t want to forget about skin care for moms! Your body has been through a lot and this post-partum body restructuring gel can help restore and firm up your skin. This gel is soothing with a great fragrance that you’ll look forward to using!

## 3. California Baby Soothing & Healing Mist for Face and Body

![California Baby Soothing & Healing Mist for Face and Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/3_california-baby-soothing-healing-mist-for-face-and-body_300x300.jpg)

This calming mist is one of the skin care products for moms and babies that is great for all ages! If you or your baby have sensitive skin or just like to freshen skin up after a day out, you’ll love this all-natural, lavender-scented mist. Use this after you spend some time out in the sun, to calm irritated skin or just to moisturize!

## 4. Lavanila Laboratories the Healthy Baby Wash

![Lavanila Laboratories the Healthy Baby Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/4_lavanila-laboratories-the-healthy-baby-wash_250x250.jpg)

Make bath time and skin care for babies fun and healthy with this all-natural hair and body wash! The Healthy Baby Wash is more expensive than your average baby wash but this is formulated with tons of vitamins, minerals and is totally tear-free! This would make a perfect gift for babies with sensitive skin!

## 5. Yes to Baby Carrots Naturally Cleansing Bubble Bath

![Yes to Baby Carrots Naturally Cleansing Bubble Bath](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/5_yes-to-baby-carrots-naturally-cleansing-bubble-bath_300x300.jpg)

Who doesn’t love a bubble bath? This gentle yet effective cleansing bath is a must-have skin care product for moms and babies! Forget all the irritating fragrances and ingredients you find in typical baby care products, Yes To uses only organic and skin-soothing ingredients that baby will love!

## 6. Dr. Lipp Original Nipple Balm for Lips

![Dr. Lipp Original Nipple Balm for Lips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/6_dr-lipp-original-nipple-balm-for-lips_250x250.jpg)

I know what you’re thinking, this is NOT a skin care product for moms and babies, it’s for lips. But wait, despite the name of the product, you can actually use this on a number of places including lips, nipples, cuticles and calluses! This balm is 100% natural and while it’s great for chapped lips, it’s a fab product to use on your body as well!

## 7. Earth Mama Angel Baby Angel Baby Bottom Balm

![Earth Mama Angel Baby Angel Baby Bottom Balm](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/7_earth-mama-angel-baby-angel-baby-bottom-balm_300x300.jpg)

Phew, despite the product’s long name, the effects of this herbal salve are quite simple and impressive. This vegan, multi-purpose balm will make a fabulous addition to your collection of skin care products for moms and babies. Use this whenever your angel baby gets a rash, dry skin, cuts or scrapes-it’s got your baby covered!

## 8. Babyganics Smooth Moves Lotion

![Babyganics Smooth Moves Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/8_babyganics-smooth-moves-lotion_300x300.jpg)

Babyganics doesn’t just make fabulous cleaning products, they’re also known for making excellent skin care products for moms and babies! Smooth Moves lotion is naturally safe, gentle and babies everywhere are raving about the clean fragrance! This stuff works so well that you might have to get a bottle for the family!

## 9. Mustela Dermo-Soothing Wipes

![Mustela Dermo-Soothing Wipes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/9_mustela-dermo-soothing-wipes_240x112.jpg)

Skin care for babies typically includes using some kind of wipe. And while there’s nothing wrong with drugstore wipes, Mustela did us one better by creating wipes with soothing extracts and plant-based cleansing milk. Obviously you can use these on baby’s diaper area but some moms like to use them to remove makeup also!

## 10. Episencial Sunny Sunscreen

_ Using sunscreen isn’t just about skin care for moms, babies need sun protection too! Episencial makes an excellent organic, long-wearing and water resistant sunscreen perfect for active babies and their moms! Remember to always keep you and your baby’s skin safe and smooth with a great sunscreen all year round!

There are lots of choices when it comes to skin care for moms and babies and the preceding items were just a few of some of the greatest out there! The great thing about skin care for babies is that many of the products work well on adult skin also, so you’ll get lots of use out of it!
