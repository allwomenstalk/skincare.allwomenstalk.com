# Easy DIY Face Masks to Suit Every Skin Type for Girls Who Want to Glow ...

I'm here to share some **DIY face masks for each skin type**. If you love face masks, you've probably tried tons of them. Here are **DIY face masks for each skin type** that you are going to love.

## 1. DRY SKIN

My favorite mask for dry skin and also an easy one.
Avocado+Olive oil
This mask is full of healthy oils to have put on dry skin. Take a half avocado and 1 tbsp of olive oil. Mix it until creamy. Apply and wait for 30 minutes. Super easy to make. It is a protective skin mask that calms and soothes even the driest skin.

## 2. OILY SKIN

If you have oilier skin you want something that helps to pull out impurities. This mask instantly rebalances the skin. Apply to that areas where you have large pores.
Egg white+ honey
Crack an egg and use only the egg white and add honey. Mix together. Apply to face and wait for 20 minutes. Rinse with warm water. It is super easy to do this mask when you need a quick fix for acne prone and oily skin. I was really impressed with this mask. Straight after using my skin looked plumper and had a definite ‘glow’ to it.

## 3. COMBO SKIN

If you have a bit dry and oily skin this mask is a great option for you.
Yogurt+Orange juice
Take 1 tbsp of yogurt and squeeze a slice of orange in it. Mix it up. Apply and wait 30 minutes. Rinse with cool water. This is a super easy mask which gives you tight pores and hydrates the skin.

Multi masking is great way to utilize different masks for different areas. So get colorful.
