# 7 Awesomely Divine Top Skincare Products I Can't Possibly Live without ...

My list consists of everything from all natural products to scrubs and cleansers you can find anywhere. Ladies, buckle up, I'm going to share my 7 top skincare products that I'd die if they stopped making them!

## 2. St. Ives Apricot Scrub

![St. Ives Apricot Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o1/a6/kkz0d80u54c9331d81d74999212834_350x329.jpg)

**Price:** $3.04 at [target.com](https://www.target.com/p/St-Ives-Apricot-Invigorating-Scrub/-/A-11185895)
Before I slip into my makeup though, I scrub all of those dead skin cells away with this awesome scrub. The one thing I like about St. Ives and their apricot scrub is that it's all natural. It leaves my skin feeling rejuvenated and fresh-feeling. It'll also get rid of all of those acne bumps that you might be feeling – after just one use!

## 3. Biore Blemish Fighting Ice Cleanser

![Biore Blemish Fighting Ice Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dx/mo/avd10mdk54c9331e51580179656382_350x332.jpg)

**Price:** $5.99 at [target.com](https://web.archive.org/web/https://www.target.com/p/Biore-Blemish-Fighting-Ice-Cleanser-6-7-oz/-/A-12174332)
While the scrub is great, I can't use it everyday. I don't want to over exfoliate my skin – so the days I am looking for something a little lighter and more gentle, I turn to Biore. This blemish fighter works great and actually helps get rid of some of the build up my makeup leaves. It's not all that expensive either, which is a huge plus!

## 4. Breath of Fresh Air Toner

![Breath of Fresh Air Toner](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qu/ex/i6epoegc54c9331f293b8442919671_365x328.jpg)

**Price:** $19.95 at [lushusa.com](https://goo.gl/E4jB3G)
Finding the right top skincare products that work for you isn't always the easiest in the world, but when I found this toner? I was sold! It's all natural and is actually used to hydrate your skin – softly. It's super gentle and can be used on all skin types!

## 5. Imperialis Moisturizers

_ **Price:** $23.95 at lushusa.com_
Part of my skincare treatment is having the very best moisturizer – and for me, it all boils down to Imperialis! For real chicas, this is one moisturizer that is at the top of my list of 'top skincare products that I can't live without'! It smells incredible, stops all of my dry skin and soothes all of my irritations.

## 6. Enchanted Eye Cream

_ **Price:** $25.95 at lushusa.com_
One thing that your top skincare products should do for you is make your face look beautiful. For me, the enchanted eye cream is definitely my top choice! This cream lifts, reduces fine lines, gets rid of wrinkles and leaves my skin so, so soft! It's totally a dream!

## 7. Love Lettuce Face Mask

_ **Price:** $6.95 at lushusa.com_
All right ladies, we all do it – we all slather on a face mask of some sort to keep our skin in check. For me? It all boils down to freshness and how it works. I LOVE – repeat: LOVE this face mask. It instantly leaves my skin silky fresh and I swear, if Lush stopped making it, I'd totally die!

I swear, all of these top skincare products are definitely one way that I keep my beauty in check. Whether it is all natural or something I buy form Rite Aid, these items are constantly in my bathroom.
