## 1. Exfoliate

Exfoliating is essential for achieving gorgeous skin because it takes away all of the dead skin cells that are causing your skin to be rough and dry. The build-up on your skin can result in excess oil and clogged pores that can generate acne and blemishes. Try to exfoliate at least two times a week for the best results. That's one of the best tips for gorgeous skin you'll ever find.

## 2. Drink Plenty of Water

Water is important for our body and our skin because it flushes away toxins. It also gives your skin a natural glow because it's hydrated. It helps your skin to appear smooth and soft, while getting rid of acne. Try to drink at least 8 cups of water a day to clear your skin of dirt and bacteria.

## 3. Exercise

Exercising is great for your overall health. It gives you energy and helps you relieve stress. Cardio is one of the best ways to achieve gorgeous skin because it increases blood flow and nourishes skin cells. It can help you feel good about yourself and prevents aging.

## 4. Get Enough Sleep

Staying up late at night can have negative effects on your skin. If your body doesn’t get enough rest, it can increase cortisol, which is a stress hormone. Having at least 8 hours of sleep each day will help improve your skin's complexion and helps your body create new collagen.
