# 8 Simple Ways to Finally Get Clear, Radiant, Glowing Skin That You Always Wanted ...

These are tried and tested tips & tricks that have FINALLY given me the clear, radiant, glowing skin I always wanted. This is more oriented toward oily skin types, but everybody could learn a thing or two. Try these tips and see your skin transform!

## 1. CLEANSE

Cleanse 2x a day (am & pm). Get a deep pore cleanser and use it on a regular basis.

## 2. USE a CLEANSING BRUSH

Use a cleansing brush with a rotating brush head. Massage each section of your face with the brush and cleanser for 10 seconds and rinse.

## 3. TONE, TONE, TONE!!

This is my TRUE SECRET. Mix 1/2 cup Rosewater to 1/4 Witch Hazel. Spray over face after cleansing. This has CLEARED my acne and evened and brightened my skin. My skin is literally GLOWING without any makeup!

## 4. SPOT TREATMENT

Find something that works for you. I love Clean and Clear because it does the job and can be worn under makeup since it's clear. So it's on my face ALL the time.

## 5. MOISTURIZE!

Moisturizing is a must! For EVERY skin type. Since I have oily skin, I use a mattifying moisturizer during the day, and a regular one at night.

## 6. MIXING MY SUNSCREEN and LIQUID HIGHLIGHTER

Since sunscreen is the last thing I apply before foundation, I mix in a bit of my liquid highlighter/primer because it's too heavy to use as a primer by itself. It has tiny shimmers throughout so it helps my skin glow.

## 7. MIXING PRIMER and FOUNDATION

One trick I love using for flawless skin is mixing equal parts of my foundation and primer. Since both are pore minimizing, my skin looks super smooth and flawless.

## 8. BRONZE IT up!

I Love bronzer! It looks natural and has some shimmer to give me a healthy glow. Swipe your favorite across your forehead, cheekbones, nose, and neck.
