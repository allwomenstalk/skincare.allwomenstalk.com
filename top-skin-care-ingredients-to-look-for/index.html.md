# Top 7 Skin Care Ingredients to Look for ...

Get ready to jot down the names of some of the top skin care ingredients around! I’ve gathered a list of 7 of the most beneficial, skin-nourishing ingredients to look for when you’re shopping for skin care products. Now you can be a smart and informed shopper that will be able to navigate through all the long, complicated list of ingredients found in skin care products and get what you want!

## 1. Vitamins

This is an easy one; vitamins are good for our bodies as well as being some of the beneficial skin care ingredients available. Which vitamins to look for? Check for A, B, C, E and K. Vitamin E is known for being the most common skin vitamin for moisturizing and anti-aging. Vitamin A is great for anti-aging and bringing new cells to the surface, B is awesome for dry skin or for evening out your skin tone. As for Vitamin C, it’s good for anti-aging and to decrease the effects of aging. Count on Vitamin K for helping the skin around the eye area and help reduce the look of dark circles around the eyes.

## 2. Alpha Hydroxy Acids

Here’s another familiar name in skin care ingredients to look for-Alpha Hydroxy Acids or AHA. We hear this term thrown around a lot but do we really know all that it’s capable of doing? AHAs stimulate collagen production, help skin to retain its elasticity and accelerate exfoliation to reveal fresh skin! They actually do a whole lot more than I expected so I’ll be sure to include them in my skin care supply!

## 3. Peptides

Peptides are one of the top skin care ingredients that you might not hear of as often as the other two but it’s just as beneficial! Peptides in skin care can help increase the production of collagen and reduce the signs of aging. The average skin care product containing peptides contains several different types of peptides such as Argirilene or Pentapeptide-3 so don’t expect to just see the word peptide on the ingredient list.

## 4. Retinol

Retinol is another one of the skin care ingredients to look for to stimulate skin cell renewal! A derivative of Vitamin A, retinol can actually penetrate the outer layer of skin and help skin to repair and rouse collagen and elastin in the lower levels of skin. Collagen and elastin are responsible for helping your skin look smooth and firm so we definitely want some retinol in our lives!

## 5. Salicylic Acid

Salicylic Acid which is also known as Beta-hydroxy Acid is another top skin care ingredient. While this ingredient also serves to reveal new skin by exfoliation like the others, it’s most helpful for those with skin that’s aged prematurely from UV rays. Salicylic acid can help improve your skin’s texture and color and is said to be much easier on the skin than AHAs so it’s something to keep in mind for ladies with sensitive skin!

## 6. Green Tea Extract

When we think of green tea we tend to think of antioxidants and that’s why it’s a top skin care ingredient. Green tea is truly beneficial for you inside and out! Using skin care products containing green tea extract offers a natural anti-inflammatory property that will help to soothe your skin. Also, green tea extract can help fight off free radicals and help to reverse sun damage from skin!

## 7. Soy

This list of beneficial skin care ingredients wouldn’t be complete without an ingredient that helps to brighten and even out our skin tone! Soy products are abundant in grocery stores but soy-based products are also great for our skin. If you want a gentler and more affordable option for prescription anti-aging and skin lightening, definitely add a soy-based product to your skin care lineup!

Now you know about seven of the top skin care ingredients and I hope you found these helpful. Ingredients in skin care can be so overwhelming and confusing due to their multisyllabic scientific names as well as just the sheer number of ingredients in one product!
