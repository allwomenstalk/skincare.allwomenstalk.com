# 8 Dry Skin Remedies That Will Work for You ...

**Dry Skin Remedies** are helpful any time, but they're especially beneficial during the winter. If you have dry skin, you are probably on the hunt for **dry skin remedies** that will give your skin some much needed relief. Let’s be honest, dry skin is awful. As the weather cools down and winter sneaks in, dry skin gets worse. What you really want to know are some **dry skin remedies** that actually work. Luckily I have put together these 8 **dry skin remedies** that will work for you.

## 1. Bathe Less

Some dry skin remedies don't seem very palatable at first. This is one of them, but stick with me. The idea of not taking a bath or a shower everyday freaks some people out, but if you have dry skin, you are washing away the natural oils your skin creates. It doesn’t hurt to bathe every other day, especially in the winter months.

## 2. Take Shorter Showers

If bathing less frequently isn’t a dry skin remedy you are willing to consider, try spending less time in the shower or bath. Keep your showers around 5 minutes. The heat and water play a big factor in your dry skin.

## 3. Use Less Soap

Soap can also dry out your skin. Many experts suggest only using soap to wash your face, armpits, and genitals. Also, switching to a milder cleanser may help with your dry skin.

## 4. Pat Dry

As you can see, many dry skin remedies have to do with bathing. When it comes to drying off after a shower or bath it is important to remember to pat the skin dry instead of rubbing. Rubbing can damage the skin and cause it to dry out even more.

## 5. Apply Lotions and Bath Oils

Cover the skin with lotion or oil every day to help replenish the skin with moisture. Heavy moisturizers work well on dry skin. You can apply lotion to your hands every time you wash them to help fight dry skin on your hands.

## 6. Use a Humidifier

Dry air in your home may be another reason for your dry skin. Some people suggest using a humidifier as a dry skin remedy. Running the heater during the winter can easily dry out the air in your home. A humidifier will help put the moisture back into the air.

## 7. Drink Water

The number one dry skin remedy is to drink plenty of water. Your body can't keep your skin nourished and moisturized if you aren’t keeping your body hydrated. Drink at least 8 glasses of water each day for the best result.

## 8. Dress Properly

The cold weather and harsh winds can also dry out your skin. Make sure to properly cover up when going outside. Be sure to keep your face and hands covered to avoid unnecessary dry skin. Prevention is one of the best dry skin remedies you can use.

**Dry skin remedies** are easy to follow once you know what to do. Let me know how these **dry skin remedies** have worked for you. You don't have to deal with itchy, flaky, or irritated skin in any season, and certainly not in the winter. If you have any other tips you would like to add to my list of 8 **dry skin remedies**, feel free to add them.
