# How to Cocktail Your Skincare Products for Optimal Layering ...

Bet you didn't know that you can mix your skincare products to create new things that offer all the best perks for your skin. You shouldn't just start combining stuff without knowing what you're doing because some things won't mix well and others shouldn't be combined at all. Use these handy tips and tricks to cocktail your skincare products and you'll be ready to layer like the best of them. Of course, if you have any skin issues going on, be sure to let your dermatologist know so they can be taken care of.

## 1. Moisture Mask + Clay Mask

![eyebrow, face, hair, nose, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fh/bm/xmm0uduh5838b5a783240963357093_500x500.jpg)

This skincare cocktail is perfect for balancing combination skin on your face. If you have several issues to contend with, it only makes sense to combine a couple of things to take care of them all at once. The moisture mask helps treat dry patches, while the clay mask soaks up grease and shine on the oily parts of your face. Place the mixture on your face, allow it work for 10 minutes, then rinse with warm water.

## 2. Moisturizer + Benzoyl Peroxide

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wd/wf/w8nzk6yb5838b5b63bdef631887463_500x500.jpg)

You might skip moisturizer if you're prone to acne in an attempt to keep pores clear. The truth is that you need to moisturize your skin regularly because dry skin increases the risk of acne. When you use a plain moisturizer cocktailed with a little bit of benzoyl peroxide on top, you get better results since hydrated skin allows the benzoyl peroxide to infuse and produce the results you want to see.

## 3. Niacinamide + Retinoids

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k8/fp/bwl41bzl5838b5c40c465998370074_400x400.jpg)

These are two products that fight age spots, discoloration and wrinkles so it makes sense to double them up for an extra whammy. Pairing retinoids with niacinamide helps take the intensity from them and helps battle the irritation they can cause. When you combine the two, the retinoids help fight the signs of aging and the niacinamide helps calm irritation and redness.

## 4. Facial Oil + Night Cream

![hair, face, black hair, eyebrow, person,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/g2/pr/xfi3f2ew5838b60c4eb50115044607_600x600.jpg)

You probably already use a night cream every night before bed. After all, they're great for fighting wrinkles and boosting hydration while you sleep. Mix in a few drops of a facial oil and you get even more benefits. The oil works with the night cream to smooth your complexion and helps you wake up to young, fresh and plumped skin every morning.

## 5. Peptides + Silicones

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/md/bx/rbvechgh5838b65d1549c839041982_600x588.jpg)

If you're battling fine lines and wrinkles, this is a cocktail you need in your life. While neither product completely erases wrinkles, they both help smooth and plump skin so that lines are less noticeable. This is because they help increase collagen production and soften your skin at the same time. Perfect!

## 6. Redness Relief Gel + Acne Controlling Cream

![hair, human hair color, face, blond, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kb/xw/c7vfrfq75838b5fc860bf270902624_600x600.jpg)

If you're someone who battles breakouts, here's the mixture for you. Acne is often very red and can even cause redness in the surrounding areas. By using a combination of redness relief gel and acne controlling cream you can even out your skin tone and get rid of zits at the same time. Cool!

## 7. Antioxidants + Sunscreen

![face, hair, eyebrow, person, woman,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p5/kd/kxs4tggm5838b68769892770298587_600x600.jpg)

You know that you should be protecting your skin from the sun by wearing sunscreen anytime you'll be outside. When you mix in antioxidants, you give your skin the power to fight any damage that gets through the sunscreen barrier. In fact, most skincare experts will tell you that skincare cocktailing should always end with sunscreen and antioxidants provide that little extra boost for healthy, youthful skin.
