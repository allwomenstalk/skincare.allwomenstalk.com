# 7 Amazing Skincare Routines to Follow in Your 20s ...

Searching for the right skincare routines when you are in your 20s isn’t easy! It’s something that you’ve got to research – unless you read AWS and we do the research for you! I’ve looked all over and I’ve found some of the best ways to keep your skin looking amazing and some of the top-notch skincare routines to follow! So, you ready to stop destroying your skin and instead, take care of it? Take a look below!

## 1. Don’t Sleep in Makeup

By far, one of the top skincare routines and habits you should get into is all about not sleeping in your makeup. When you sleep in your makeup, it clogs up your pores and you could wake up with all kinds of oil and dirt on your face! Just a quick wash of your face before you head to bed can save your skin from tons of breakouts and will make it look beautiful!

## 2. Protect Your Face, Neck and Chest

Just because the sun isn’t out doesn’t mean that you shouldn’t protect your face, neck and chest from it. SPF lotions and creams are essential whenever you are stepping foot outside. That way, you can protect your skin and you can keep it looking as amazing as it does in your 20s!

## 3. Foaming Cleanser

When I was in my early 20s, I had no idea what to use for my face, so I ended up using soap. That was the wrong choice! Never ever use just soap on your face. Instead, invest in a great foaming cleanser so that your skin can really be taken care of. You don’t want to overdo it, just something simple is fine!

## 4. Lightweight Moisturizer Everyday

Again, when I was in my early 20s, I had no clue that I needed to wear moisturizer of any sort! You don’t need a really heavy moisturizer either; something that is super light and airy is perfect! I love Aveeno moisturizers or something from LUSH that is all natural.

## 5. Don’t Pick Any Acne

I know that it happens – you get a zit and the first thing that you want to do is pick at it. Don’t! You’ve got to leave it alone, otherwise it could scar and it truly could cause more problems than it is worth. Just use some acne-blasting cream and leave it alone!

## 6. Apply a Nightly Serum

I had no idea that a nightly serum should have been incorporated into my skincare routine until I noticed that when I got up in the morning, my face was so dry! When I started to clean off all of my makeup from the day and then started to apply a nightly serum, I wake up now and my face is in amazing shape. Give it a try, girlies, and see how you like it!

## 7. Don’t Overdo It

Finally, don’t ever overdo your routine, especially when you are in your 20s. Just keep it light, airy and make sure that you protect your skin. Don’t put too much cream on, use lightweight items and make sure that you take off that makeup at night!

So, there you have it, all of the best skincare routines that you can follow to keep your skin looking amazing! No matter how old you are, you should have a skincare routine of some sort. So, what sorts of skincare routines do you have? Any to share?
