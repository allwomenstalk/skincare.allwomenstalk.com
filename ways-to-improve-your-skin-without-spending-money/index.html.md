# 7 Ways to Improve Your Skin without Spending Money ...

If you are like me, you are probably always looking for ways to improve your skin. However, a lot of skin care treatments are quite expensive. Some even cost three hundred dollars! Thankfully, you don’t have to spend three hundred dollars to have great skin. There are plenty of ways to improve your skin that don’t cost anything!

## 1. Drink Water

Being dehydrated can make your skin look dull and dry. By drinking plenty of water, you can prevent this from happening and restore radiance to your skin. When your skin in hydrated, it looks bright, clear, and young. To ensure your skin does stay hydrated, you need to make sure you drink at least 64 ounces of water a day. Drinking plenty of water is one of the easiest ways to improve your skin, but there are plenty of other tips that are free and will also help your skin look its best.

## 2. Use Lukewarm Water

While washing your face with hot water may feel good, especially when it is cold out, it can actually make your skin dry. Hot water dries skin out and leaves it looking dull. To prevent this from happening, wash your face with tepid water. It may not feel nice and warm, but your skin will look much better.

## 3. Change Pillowcases

If you have acne prone skin, changing your pillowcases often can be very helpful. Dirt, oil, and dead skin can collect on your pillowcase each night and clog your pores. As a result, your skin can break out more easily. An easy, free solution is to make sure you have a clean pillowcase every couple of nights. I did this when I was a teenager, and within several days I noticed a big improvement in my skin.

## 4. Nix Dairy

Dairy products can cause skin problems for many people. Acne, irritation, and even rosacea can all be a result of a dairy intolerance. If you suspect dairy might be causing some of your skin problems, try giving it up for a month. You may be surprised at how much your skin improves.

## 5. Scrub Vigorously

Glycolic acid peels, electric skin brushes, and exfoliators are all used to remove dead skin cells and speed the turnover of skin cells. As great as these products can be, they are also expensive. A free alternative is to use the rough side of a wash cloth and scrub vigorously. This is a gentle way to exfoliate your skin that will give you similar results without any irritation.

## 6. Raid Your Kitchen

There are many things in your kitchen that you can use to improve your skin without spending any extra money. You can exfoliate your skin with aluminum free baking soda. You can also use coconut oil or olive oil to take off make up and moisturize your skin. Yogurt is another item you may have in your kitchen that you can use as a face mask to improve dullness. As you can see, there are many items in your kitchen that you can use for your skin.

## 7. Always Take Your Makeup off

If you are guilty of sleeping in your makeup, you may want to reconsider this practice. Sleeping in makeup can clog pores and cause acne. It can also irritate your skin and result in redness and dullness. Any easy way to remedy these problems is to make sure you always take a few minutes before bed to wash your makeup off. Your skin will thank you!

Having beautiful skin doesn’t have to cost a fortune. There are plenty of ways you can improve your skin without spending any money. All of these tips are quite effective, and if you put them into practice, you just might see your skin improving. What free skincare tips do you have?
