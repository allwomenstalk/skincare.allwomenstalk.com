# 7 Ways Aloe Vera Can Transform Your Skin ...

There are lots of **reasons to use aloe vera in your skincare routine**. The aloe vera plant originated on the Arabian peninsula and is now grown around the world in various warm climates. It can be found as a houseplant in many homes and has some surprising benefits. When it comes to skincare, the aloe in the leaves of the succulent aloe vera plant contains vitamins B-12, A, C, and E. Vitamins A, C, and E are antioxidants which help protect the human cells against damage that leads to premature aging. Here are the best **reasons to use aloe vera in your skincare routine**.

## 1. Sunburn Relief

The aloe vera plant is well-known for its ability to soothe a sunburn. Not only does aloe soothe your sunburnt skin, it also hydrates it. It is a must have plant to have around in the summer. Sunburn relief is one of the top **reasons to use aloe vera in your skincare routine**.

## 2. Blister Relief

When you wear your heels for too long or break in a new pair on a fun night out, you know what is going to come next…blisters. Aloe can help relieve the pain from the blister while hydrating the skin. The blister will heal quicker, so you can get back in those favorite new heels and have some fun.

## 3. Stretchmarks and Scar Reduction and Prevention

Everyone is going to get them at one time or another. Stretchmarks can be diminished (and in some cases prevented) by using aloe. This all natural technique can be used on minor scarring to slowly diminish the appearance of the scar over time.

## 4. Acne Relief

Along with the aloe vera plant's rich wealth of antioxidants, the plant also offers antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. Mild to moderate acne can be treated with aloe. Leave the aloe on overnight to receive the full benefits this plant has to offer. This inexpensive treatment will save your pocketbook and leave your skin glowing.

## 5. Dry Skin Relief

Dry skin afflicts many of us and the winter season is when it is the worst. Dry air can cause the skin to become dry, itchy, and unbelievably uncomfortable. If you don’t want to use moisturizers that contain synthetic chemicals to quench your skin, you can choose an all natural alternative like aloe.

## 6. Premature Aging

Many spend a bundle on anti-aging serums but if you want to save your money you can use aloe instead. The antioxidants contained within the aloe vera plant fight the damage caused by free radicals. Aloe can be used to fight early signs of wrinkles with antioxidants and moisturizing properties that are all natural and effective.

## 7. Minor Burn Relief

The best reason to have an aloe vera plant in your home is the relief that it can give to minor burns, such as those commonly experienced when cooking. First and second-degree burns can be soothed by using the leaves of an aloe vera plant. All you do is cut a leaf off and [squeeze it](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/foods-from-your-childhood-that-you-probably-miss/) or cut it in half length-wise and scoop out the contents.
