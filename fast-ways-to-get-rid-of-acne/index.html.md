# The Fastest ⏱ and Cheapest Ways to Kill Acne Overnight ...

Are you looking for **fast ways to get rid of acne**? We have all had that time when we had a school party or occasion being disturbed by acne or a pimple. Makeup sometimes cannot hide everything and so you do not have any choice, you have to go out while trying to hide the ugly creature on your skin. Well, following these steps you will never face it again because these are some simple **ways to get rid of pimples** really quickly.

## 1. Toothpaste

Yes, the one you put on your teeth. Using toothpaste can kill the bacteria and dry the pimple. All you have to do is wash your face to clear the dirt off. Dry your face, then put some toothpaste on the pimple. Wait 15 mins (or until it dries). Wash the toothpaste off with water.

## 2. Steam/warm Water

You have to open you pores to clean away the dirt and bacteria that causes pimples. To do this, you can use either steam or warm water (both are the same concept). With warm water, simply wash your face. When using a steam approach, boil a pot of water on the stove, let it cool 5-10 minutes then hold your face above the steam with a towel over your head for 10-20 minutes. BONUS! Throw some eucalyptus in there when you have a cold and steam clear your sinuses at the same time.

## 3. Ice Cubes

The toothpaste might hurt your skin or make you feel it is burnt, so there's nothing better to soothe than an ice cube massage. And to close the pores after steaming (while softening your skin) you have to use ice or cold water to prevent pimples for a few extra days. To start, grab some ice cubes, put the cubes in a cloth, then on your skin and move in circles. Keep moving around your skin for two minutes or until the cube is melted.

Never have a bad skin day again!
