## 1. NDYAG Laser Facial

Traditional facials may be popular as anti-aging and rejuvenating treatments for the skin, but they only treat the surface. NDYAG laser facial is a revolutionary treatment that uses a sophisticated laser to penetrate the skin’s surface layer and works deep down. This way, cellular regeneration can occur deep within the skin, giving way to brighter, healthier-looking, and smoother skin. The results of NDYAG laser facials last longer than conventional facials as trained nurses and laser technicians carefully provide a more targeted treatment to work on wrinkles, fine lines, scars, and other skin issues you may have.

## 2. Why Laser-based Facials?

Because they are non-invasive and they are shown to be effective in promoting new skin from inside-out. One of the most important benefits of NDYAG laser facial is it stimulates collagen production within the skin. As we age, collagen levels decline naturally. The laser stimulates new collagen to grow, making it effective at reducing the signs of aging. Collagen is important in filling out lines and wrinkles, and it also helps even out the skin tone on the skin’s surface. This results in radiant and glowing, youthful looking skin.

- Brightening sheet mask
