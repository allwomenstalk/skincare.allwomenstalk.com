# 5 Little Known Skincare Hacks That Work like a Charm ...

To cover crinkles, pimples, acne, wrinkles and aging, women almost always tend to dress their faces with excessive makeup.

However, makeup cosmetics are expensive and can leave your skin vulnerable. But as long as you’re taking skincare seriously, you will get that amazing complexion. I have collected 5 useful skin care hacks that work magically and most importantly, you can easily do them at home!

## 1. Salty Water

![face, skin, eyebrow, chin, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/yv/l4qp8dk3595bfd9b28093110254507_600x417.jpg)

Dissolve salt with water and apply the mixture on your face for 10 minutes to have a fresher look while reducing swelling.

## 2. Oil for Lips

![lip, face, skin, cheek, eyebrow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ol/zx/ru5eqkyc595bfdad6466f342380416_600x449.jpg)

Add some oil to your lips to avoid lip dryness. Also, oil makes your lips look fuller and sexier. Massage you lips gently for 1 minute for maximum benefits.

## 3. Olive Oil for Face

![eyebrow, face, skin, beauty, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sv/u8/msrty7o5595bfdbcb3d8d361292030_600x629.jpg)

If you’re seeking for a good moisture, but commercial ones irritate your skin, olive oil will serve you best. Massage the oil to your face for 7 minutes every 4 days to have a cleaner, softer and smoother skin.

## 4. Honey

![face, skin, beauty, cheek, eyelash,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b7/yd/fe6nurq0595bfd0802340800985814_600x379.jpg)

The best thing about using honey is its quick results. It will decrease the size of pimples and acne and may even get rid of those zits completely! Leave the honey on the pimple for 15 minutes and then wash your face with warm water.

## 5. Baking Soda

![eyebrow, eyelash, eye, forehead, close up,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zf/at/rc32edun595bfd73b1611278205050_600x303.jpg)

Baking soda is used to remove dark circles and under eye bags, which we all try to mask. To make the mixture, use a teaspoon of baking soda and mix it into a glass of hot water. Using cotton balls, apply the mixture directly under your eyes and keep for approximately 15 minutes. Voila! Brighter, eyes and no more eye bags!
