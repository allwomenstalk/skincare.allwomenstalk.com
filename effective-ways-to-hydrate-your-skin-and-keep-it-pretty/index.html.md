# 8 Effective Ways to Hydrate Your Skin and Keep It Pretty ...

There are tons of ways to hydrate your skin that will make it soft, plump and look glowing! Are you trying to make your skin look beautiful without paying a ton of money? Are you trying to keep your skin perfect and hydrated? Well ladies, I've got the top 8 effective ways to hydrate your skin that might not cost you all that much money! Check out my ways to hydrate your skin to keep it fresh and pretty!

## 1. Drink Water

I know, you've probably heard that drinking water has all kinds of different benefits, including keeping your entire body healthy right? Well ladies, drinking water is also one of the best ways to hydrate your skin perfectly! Just drink an average of 8 glasses of water a day and see just how glowing and hydrated your skin looks!

## 2. Avocado

The food that you eat also one of the ways to hydrate your skin. Switching up and adding avocado to your diet will make your skin even out and make it beautiful! Avocado has all kinds of rich good fats to it and it's so good for your skin and your body. Try it!

## 3. Pumpkin

Pumpkin is another way to hydrate your skin. Just adding a bit of pumpkin to your diet will really cause your skin to glow and make sure that it looks beautiful. For me, pumpkin is a great fall treat that I make a ton of sauces and soups out of!

## 4. Take Omega 3 Supplements

Omega 3 is one of those things that you might not think will hydrate your skin but it does. Taking Omega 3 supplements will ensure that your skin looks glowing and not a bit dry! You can also beef up on the salmon or fish that you eat, because fish is full of Omega 3!

## 5. No Hot Showers

If you're trying to find simple and easy ways to hydrate your skin, try avoiding taking an extremely hot shower everyday. Hot showers can actually take dehydrate your skin instead of hydrate it. Try to have lukewarm water or if you can stand it, colder water.

## 6. High Fat, Natural Body Moisturizers

Moisturizing your skin is another great way to hydrate your skin. You want moisturizers that are high in fat and that are all natural. These moisturizers are generally the best and will make your skin look so much better and it won't dry out!

## 7. Use a Humidifier at Night

Finding ways to hydrate your skin doesn't have to be hard, it can be as simple as using a humidifier at night. The air in your house could be really dry and could be drying out your skin. A humidifier will add that moisture back into the air and will really ensure that your skin stays extremely hydrated and beautiful!

## 8. Limit Use of Air Conditioning and Heating

Finally, remember how I mentioned the air being dry? That can come from too much A/C or too much heat, so limit your use on that and you'll see a huge difference in just how dry your skin really is!

Locating the best way to keep your skin hydrated doesn't have to be difficult ladies, you just have to find a way that works best for you! So girls, what are some of the ways to keep your skin hydrated that you've used that have worked?
