# 7 No\-Wait Ways to Instantly anti\-Age Your Skin ...

If you’re anything like me, you want to look young, no matter what age you really are. While there are lots of things you can do to prevent the signs of aging in the long run, there are also some pretty great things you can try to instantly look younger and fresher. None of these tips is all that hard, but the payoffs are huge. Add these things to your routine and you could look younger and younger every day. Perfect!

## 1. Highlight Your Brows for an Instant Lift

![Highlight Your Brows for an Instant Lift](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nw/wf/b6af0uwu570cf71b4d34a521948658_600x600.jpg)

If you look in the mirror and see tired, saggy eyes, you are going to love what this tip can do for you. Simply dab a bit of highlighter at the arch of each of your eyebrows and blend it with your eye shadow. That tiny bit of lighter and shimmery color below the arch of your brows will help open up your eyes and make them look wide awake. Easy, right?

## 2. Do a Daily Peel Treatment

![Do a Daily Peel Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f6/ly/htq3dgvd570cf75f3d0f3201996445_600x600.jpg)

You don’t have to make an appointment at the salon every day, but you can use daily micro peel products right at home on a daily basis. The products work by gently dissolving your top layers of skin cells, which reveals the younger and fresher skin beneath. An overnight peel is a great option because you smooth it on before bed and allow it to work while you sleep.

## 3. Make Sure to Pay Attention to Your Entire Body

![Make Sure to Pay Attention to Your Entire Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ie/vo/vgfkqymv570cf79512ef6762180841_600x600.jpg)

Don’t make the mistake of focusing on your face at the expense of the rest of your body. The skin on the rest of your body can also show your age, so make sure you moisturize your entire body every single day. The best time to do this is right after a shower when your skin is still damp. It only takes a few minutes, so there’s no reason to skip it.

## 4. Switch to Cream Makeup Instead of the Powder Ones

![Switch to Cream Makeup Instead of the Powder Ones](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ik/jp/n1mbwqby570cf7b55facd154331250_600x600.jpg)

The problem with powder makeup is that it can settle into creases and make wrinkles and fine lines much more pronounced. Swapping it out for a creamy formula instead can help eliminate this problem. Not only that, but creamy makeup can also give you a youthful and dewy complexion, while also masking wrinkles and blemishes.

## 5. Moisturize Your Face Twice Daily

![Moisturize Your Face Twice Daily](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bi/k1/xujl6s33570cf877a6a5e870692854_600x600.jpg)

The way moisturizers work is that they instantly plump the lines on your face, which makes it pretty important to incorporate into your skincare routine. You don’t need fancy, expensive products either. Many affordable moisturizers do the job you want them to without costing your entire paycheck. Smooth one on in the morning and after washing your face at night.

## 6. Don’t Forget Your Jaw and Neck

![Don’t Forget Your Jaw and Neck](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b0/m0/i5inbg5u570cf9f462814768424121_506x506.jpg)

It’s easy to remember to take care of your facial skin, but your jaw and neck can betray your true age if you don’t give them some tender loving care too. When you apply product to your face, make sure you extend it down over your jaw and neck. This will help prevent crepey skin, wrinkles and age spots in these areas. Once you get into the habit, it won’t be hard to keep up with this tip.

## 7. Use Primer to Smooth out Wrinkles and Fine Lines

Primer is a magical product that can help your makeup stay in place, but it can also smooth out your face and make your wrinkles less pronounced. The silicones in these products helps to plump your skin and fill in lines. You can use it alone, but you can also use it under your makeup for an instantly youthful appearance.

Which of these tips do you think you’ll add to your routine today?
