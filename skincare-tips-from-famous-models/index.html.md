# Hottest Skincare Tips for Women Obsessed with Models ...

Models pretty much always look their best, which means you can take their advice anytime you want to look gorgeous. Not only do they have great makeup and hair tips, but you can learn everything you need to know about flawless skin too. However, it’s also important to keep in mind that models aren’t perfect and they usually have a team of people working on them. Long story short – use these tips, but don’t expect absolute perfection.

## 1. Practice before You Go out in the World

![hair, clothing, black hair, lady, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ep/vk/hnxck5595878782120673249266189_600x665.jpg)

Top model, Coco Rocha, strongly encourages you to try something new at home when you aren’t planning on going anywhere. That way, if it doesn’t work out, you aren’t stuck with a look that you have to completely redo or a look that you hate, but have to suffer through because you have a meeting this morning. Try a look to make sure it works for you before debuting it for the world to see.

## 2. Wear Bronzer Every Single Day

![Emirates Woman, hair, face, eyebrow, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e5/qw/wb5gtxh15878812073cc9312752546_500x500.jpg)

According to Iman, you have to wear bronzer every day, no matter what color your skin is. Brush it on your cheekbones to give you that little glow that will make you look like you just came back from a trip the Bahamas. This is so easy, there’s no excuse not to give it a try.

## 3. Mix Night Cream with Your Foundation

![hair, photograph, person, image, woman,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/un/nn/mbp5wn825878878cc04cd649138878_600x600.jpg)

Who says you have to relegate your night cream to bedtime? According to model, Arizona Muse, mixing a really great night cream with your foundation gives you a flawless sheer look that creates the perfect slate for all of your makeup.

## 4. Drink a Lot of Water during the Day

![hair, face, eyebrow, hairstyle, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e9/rw/jzkx093x5878888957aa3161219860_600x600.jpg)

Your skin relies on water for adequate hydration, which helps keep it plumped and smooth. Skimp on the fluids and your face is likely to show it by being dull and dry. Top model, Carolyn Murphy swears by drinking lots of water to keep her skin looking completely flawless. Carry a water bottle everywhere you go and you shouldn’t have any trouble getting the same gorgeous skin.

## 5. Stop Pulling All Nighters and Get Some Sleep

![hair, hairstyle, beauty, black hair, photo shoot,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zq/aw/exh1yy59587888f0d20fc274594465_472x472.jpg)

Hilary Rhoda says that she counts on enough sleep as her number one skincare secret. She’s on to something since adequate shut-eye can help your skin repair and restore itself while you slumber. Aim for at least seven hours every night so that you can wake up to the lovely skin you crave every single morning.

## 6. Make Sure You Wear a Lot of Moisturizer

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, beauty, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a5/zk/chyv7dap587889e4d602b175616743_400x400.jpg)

If it works for Joan Small, it should work for you too. Make sure you slather on plenty of moisturizer each day to help keep your skin soft and supple. At the very least, you need to moisturize in the morning and at night before you go to bed. If you exercise or have very dry skin, you can probably benefit from another application in the middle of the day. This step is so easy to do and will give you awesome results!

## 7. Fake It with a Fabulous Red Lipstick

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kn/sz/vmzzx58258788be3e7b8d418431998_600x600.jpg)

Red lipstick might not give you the best skin you’ve ever seen, but it can help fake the look you want. Make sure you wear a red that complements your skin tone so you don’t look all washed out. On days when you have trouble loving your skin, just slather on some red lipstick and you’ll instantly feel better.

Which model’s skin do you covet? Are you going to try any of these tips?
