# The Right Way to Use Your Night Cream ...

Night cream can do wonders for your skin. Choosing the right one infuses your entire face with nutrients and antioxidants that fight wrinkles, fine lines and age spots while also keeping your skin looking healthy and refreshed. The best part? This all happens while you sleep! You might feel like applying night cream is pretty much a no-brainer, but tweaking your technique can pump up the benefits. Check out this info and you’ll be ready to revolutionize your nighttime skincare routine.

## 1. Talk to Your Dermatologist about the Right Night Cream for You

No two girls have the same skin and therefore your needs might differ dramatically from those of your sister or best friend. If you’re having specific skin issues or you want to target a certain issue (say anti-aging), you may need something different. Make an appointment with your dermatologist to talk things over and the two of you should be able to figure out what the right night cream for you should be.

## 2. Read the Ingredients Label and Make Sure Your Night Cream Contains Retinol

Retinol is an ingredient that has magical powers when it comes to anti-aging. The primary reason why women wear night cream is to fight fine lines and wrinkles so it makes sense to ensure that yours contains the ingredients that will get the job done. Retinol is backed by science as a way to smooth skin and make your signs of aging less visible.

## 3. Try Something That Contains Antioxidants for Your Best Skin Ever

Antioxidants are plant compounds that fight free radical damage, which can save your skin from the signs of aging and the cellular damage that can lead to skin cancer. By smoothing on a night cream packed with antioxidants, you allow your skin to reap the benefits on a nightly basis. Look for night creams that contain resveratrol and vitamins C and E.

## 4. Make Sure Your Night Cream Will Also Moisturize Your Skin

Dehydrated skin will look dull, dry and lifeless, which is why your night cream also needs to be a moisturizer. By moisturizing your skin every night before bed, you ensure that it stays plumped up and healthy looking. Moisturizing ingredients include hyaluronic acid, shea butter, glycerin and ceramides.

## 5. Don’t Skimp on Your Night Cream or It Won’t Be as Effective

It might be tempting to make your night cream last longer by wearing the bare minimum, but your skin will thank you if you slather on just a bit more than that. Going too light won’t maximize the benefits of the night cream product so use about a tablespoon every night.

## 6. Put Your Night Cream onto Damp Skin for the Best Results

Right after you wash your face at night is the best time to put on your night cream. When your skin is damp it helps create a barrier that ensures that your night cream absorbs into your face and you can get the most out of it. Whether you wash in the shower or at the sink, slather on your night cream right afterwards and you’ll love the results.

## 7. Use a Gentle Hand when You Apply Your Night Cream

The skin on your face is more delicate than the skin on the rest of your body, which means you should be gentle when you apply your night cream. Don’t pull and push on your skin too hard or you’ll stretch the delicate collagen beneath, making fine lines and wrinkles more pronounced.
