# 8 Super Serums for Your Skin ...

We often see serums for skin care but do we really know what they’re for? Facial serums are actually best at adding nutrients to the deeper layers of your skin. Other skin care products like creams have larger molecules and can’t quite reach the depths and really penetrate our skin like a serum can. So with that said, let’s explore 8 super serums for skin that’ll work wonders on our skin!

## 1. Olay Regenerist Daily Regenerating Serum

![Olay Regenerist Daily Regenerating Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/1_olay-regenerist-daily-regenerating-serum_410x410.jpg)

When we think of serums for skin care we tend to think of expensive, high end products but drugstore brands offer some great alternatives! Olay’s regenerating serum offers a deep moisturizer along with their exclusive amino-peptide complex to deliver regenerated and renewed skin. Don’t let the brand name fool ya, many women choose this over higher priced brands!

## 2. Ole Henrikson Truth Serum Collagen Booster

![Ole Henrikson Truth Serum Collagen Booster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/2_ole-henrikson-truth-serum-collagen-booster_250x250.jpg)

If you’re looking for a facial serum that delivers anti-aging and skin brightening properties, this could be your perfect match! Truth Serum collagen booster does just that-boosts collagen while firming, nourishing and moisturizing even to ladies with sensitive skin types! If you’re lucky enough to not have any fine lines yet, you can use this as a preventative measure or for all the other skin care benefits!

## 3. RoC Retinol Correxion Deep Wrinkle Serum

![RoC Retinol Correxion Deep Wrinkle Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/3_roc-retinol-correxion-deep-wrinkle-serum_410x410.jpg)

Many skin care experts recommend RoC products for their quality skin care products that are effective and affordable. RoC’s Deep Wrinkle serum is said to visibly reduce fine lines and deep wrinkles, moisturize and reduce the signs of photo aging with consistent use. If you’re new to serums, try out a drugstore brand like RoC before you invest in a higher priced equivalent. You might end up preferring the drugstore brand!

## 4. Dr. Andrew Weil for Origins Mega-Bright Skin Tone Correcting Serum

![Dr. Andrew Weil for Origins Mega-Bright Skin Tone Correcting Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/4_dr-andrew-weil-for-origins-mega-bright-skin-tone-correcting-serum_250x250.jpg)

Facial serums aren’t all about wrinkles, we can use them to brighten our complexion and reduce the appearance of dark spots, acne scars and UV damage. Dr. Weil’s Mega-Bright serum helps correct and prevent skin discolorations from reappearing in four short weeks plus it’s safe to use on all skin types! Use this to brighten up your complexion or just to soften and nourish your skin!

## 5. Neutrogena Ageless Intensives Deep Wrinkle Serum

![Neutrogena Ageless Intensives Deep Wrinkle Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/5_neutrogena-ageless-intensives-deep-wrinkle-serum_184x500.jpg)

Neutrogena is another reliable drugstore skin care brand that many of us rely on for natural yet effective products. This non-comedogenic formula won’t clog pores and it can be used to minimize the appearance of fine line and wrinkles as well as decrease the look of age spots. Use this retinol and vitamin enriched serum to smooth and repair your skin!

## 6. Bare Minerals Active Cell Renewal Night Serum

![Bare Minerals Active Cell Renewal Night Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/6_bare-minerals-active-cell-renewal-night-serum_250x250.jpg)

Use this skin care serum while before you hit the hay to wake up to firmer and healthier skin in the morning! This serum utilizes the brand's exclusive ActiveSoil Complex to accelerate skin cell renewal and increase moisture while we snooze! Many users reported brighter, smoother and softer skin after just a few uses!

## 7. CAUDALÍE Vinosource 'S.O.S.' Thirst Quenching Serum

![CAUDALÍE Vinosource 'S.O.S.' Thirst Quenching Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/7_caudal-e-vinosource-s-o-s-thirst-quenching-serum_350x537.jpg)

If dry skin is one of your skin care issues, Caudalie’s Thirst Quenching serum could be your new skin BFF! This lightweight serum asserts that it can help capture and retain moisture, balance out our skin and leave it feeling silky and smooth. If you’re looking for a moisturizer that absorbs fast and doesn’t leave skin feeling oily, definitely give this a try!

## 8. Mario Badescu Vitamin C Serum

![Mario Badescu Vitamin C Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/12/8_mario-badescu-vitamin-c-serum_350x537.jpg)

Vitamin C is one of the key ingredients in many facial serums as it can correct the signs of aging while brightening the skin. Mario Badescu’s serum also does that while improving the texture of your skin and leaving it smoother, softer and even more radiant! You can use this alone or under moisturizer for your skin’s daily dose of vitamins!

Depending on your skin care needs, serums can play an invaluable role in helping brighten, firm, moisturize and reduce the signs of aging. I love using serums because it’s an added layer of skin goodies that I can seal in with my moisturizer. Do you use facial serums? What’s your favorite serum?
