# 7 Awesome Exfoliating Skin Care Products ...

The Best Exfoliating Skin Care Products will keep your skin polished and radiant. Exfoliants remove dull, dead skin cells from the surface layer of the skin so that new, healthy skin cells will show. You only need to exfoliate 2 to 3 times per week, so these 7 best exfoliating skin care products will last a long time!

## 1. Clinical Care Skin Solutions Scrubzit

![Clinical Care Skin Solutions Scrubzit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/1_clinical-care-skin-solutions-scrubzit_282x165.jpg)

**Price:** $48.50 at [thesalonlink.com](https://www.thesalonlink.com/clinical-care-skin-solutions-scrubzit-exfoliant-2-oz/)
I love this exfoliating product because it exfoliates in two ways: with tiny crystals like those used in microdermabrasion and with pineapple enzymes that eat away dead skin cells. Not only does this product work in two ways, it also has an amazing pineapple scent! This exfoliant is gentle enough to use on skin with acne, unlike other harsher scrubs, so it’s also great for sensitive skin.

## 2. Lush Dark Angels Deep Cleansing Charcoal Scrub

![Lush Dark Angels Deep Cleansing Charcoal Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/2_lush-dark-angels-deep-cleansing-charcoal-scrub_350x240.jpg)

**Price:** $15 at [lushusa.com](https://www.lushusa.com/face/cleansers-scrubs/dark-angels/9999906954.html?amp%3Bcgid=cleansers&start=13)
This best exfoliating skin care product might look dirty, but it will leave your skin feeling fresh and smooth! Dark Angels has ingredients to exfoliate, cleanse, moisturize, and diminish pimples. It leaves the skin feeling soft, supple, and smooth, It tends to be a bit messy though, so I recommend using it in the shower rather than at the sink!

## 3. Simple Smoothing Face Scrub

![Simple Smoothing Face Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/3_simple-smoothing-face-scrub_300x300.jpg)

**Price:** $6.99 at [drugstore.com](https://www.walgreens.com/)
The goal of Simple Skin Care is to keep their products down to as few ingredients as possible to eliminate skin irritants. Their exfoliant uses rice granules to slough away dead skin cells. This product also contains vitamins E and B5 to nourish and protect your skin. A little bit of this gentle, non-irritating scrub goes a long way!

## 4. Bliss Hot Salt Scrub

**Price:** $36 at [beautybar.com](https://goo.gl/GSJzhs)
This best exfoliating skin care product heats up as you massage it onto the skin. The warmth helps relax your muscles and your senses. Bliss Hot Salt Scrub is oil free, yet super softening. Since salt is a bit more harsh than other types of scrubs, it’s best to only use this product on the body and not the face. The eucalyptus and rosemary scented sea salt is refreshing and rejuvenating!

## 5. L’Occitane Shea Ultra Rich Body Scrub

![L’Occitane Shea Ultra Rich Body Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/5_loccitane-shea-ultra-rich-body-scrub_600x661.jpg)

**Price:** $36 at [beautybar.com](https://goo.gl/43HPEV)
This exfoliant not only removes dead skin cells with finely ground nutshells to help soften skin, it also adds moisture back in since it contains ultra-rich shea butter. L’Occitane formulates their products following the principles of phytotherapy, the art of healing through plants and are proven to be effective. This best exfoliating skin care product is especially great for dry skin.

## 6. Fresh Brown Sugar Body Polish

**Price:** $65 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Fresh Brown Sugar Body Polish has an invigorating and refreshing lemon scent to help wake you up during your morning shower! This best exfoliating skin care product uses sugar as its scrubbing agent. Sugar’s antibacterial properties help cleanse the skin while scrubbing away dead skin cells.

## 7. The Body Shop Peppermint Cooling Pumice Foot Scrub

![The Body Shop Peppermint Cooling Pumice Foot Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2012/11/7_the-body-shop-peppermint-cooling-pumice-foot-scrub_301x275.jpg)

**Price:** $14 at [thebodyshop-usa.com](https://www.thebodyshop.com/en-us/)
This gel based best exfoliating skin care product uses volcanic rock to scrub away the dead skin on your feet. Soften your callouses while preparing your feet for sandals and playing footsy with this cooling peppermint scrub. Peppermint oil and menthol cool and soothe your skin while taking care of your tired feet.

Get super smooth skin from head to toe by using these best exfoliating skin care products! Remember, it’s only necessary to exfoliate 2 to 3 times per week. Over-exfoliating the skin can make it sensitive and raw. What is your favorite exfoliating skin care product?
