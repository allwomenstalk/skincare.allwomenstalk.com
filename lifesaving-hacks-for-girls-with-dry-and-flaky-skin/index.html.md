# 7 Life Changing Hacks for Girls with Horribly Dry Skin ...

If you ask me, last winter was pretty brutal and I got quiet a bit of dead skin to prove it! It might not seem like a really big problem, but having dry patches that even make up can’t cover can be pretty problematic. It’s not a pretty sight and it must be treated from within because sometimes even the best moisturizes are not enough to overcome the problem. So if the winter cold has done some lasting damage to your skin, don’t worry you’re not alone in your misery and thankfully we’ve still got a few tricks at our disposal!

## 1. Invest in a Humidifier

I didn’t actually believe in investing in a humidifier until my roommate purchased purchased one and indirectly, so did I. And while I couldn’t really tell whether it was making any difference from the start, I noticed a few pleasant changes over time. My skin felt more hydrated, firm and just a little more alive than usual. Based on my experience I’d definitely recommend buying one, it’s handy and worth the purchase!

## 2. Check out Hydrating Face Mists

Recently I decided to broaden my experience with beauty products and delve into trying new things on the market, or at least new from my perspective. Face mists immediately caught my eye as they are becoming pretty popular and are really relevant to my recent beauty struggles. I highly recommend finding one the best one for your skin as it’s easy to use even on top of make up, it’s refreshing and overall a good addition to your moisturizing regime.

## 3. Protect Your Skin with SPF Products

No matter the forecast, never forget to protect your skin from the harsh UV rays. Not only will you save your skin from aging long term but also prevent further damage. After all, if your skin is flaky and dry, all you can do is heal and protect! Finding the perfect sunscreen might take some time but what may seem like a never ending search will leave you with a holy grail product.

## 4. Lower Your Shower Temperature and Moisturize Right after

If you are suffering from dry skin, the last thing you want to do is to deplete it of moisture that is left over. This can often happen unintentionally when you take long hot showers and strip your skin of natural oils. Therefore, as part of your recovery, try to lower your shower temperature and lock in the moisture by moisturizing all over right after!

## 5. Tone down Exfoliation

Exfoliating might be your first instinct when facing dry skin, but remember to hold yourself back! Over-exfoliation can lead to more irritation and even further flakiness; you’ll enter a never-ending cycle you do not want to be a part of. So instead limit the number of times you spend exfoliating and opt for a gentler scrub when you do.

## 6. Intensify Hydration Overnight

If there is ever a time you want to slather thick dense moisturizer to really get that moisture back into your skin, it should be right before bed. This is when you’ll get the most out of your night cream and won’t have to worry about looking oily or shiny! As a rule of thumb, always reach for more intensely hydrating products as part of your night routine.

## 7. Hydrate from within and Drink More Water

Of course nothing works better than fixing the problem internally. Chances are that if your skin is super dry, you are not drinking enough water to replenish your body! So take it as a sign that you are not meeting your body’s daily needs and work towards improvement. If your body is happy and healthy on the inside, your appearance will reflect that!
