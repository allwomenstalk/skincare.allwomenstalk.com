# Your Face Mask Should Have These 7 Qualities ...

A great face mask can be a girl’s best friend! There are many wonderful things that a face mask can do for you. That includes exfoliation, moisturizing and getting rid of the signs of aging. With so many masks on store shelves, it can be totally overwhelming to figure out which one is best for you. They aren’t all created equal, so it’s a good idea to do your research before making a choice. The following qualities are ones that you should always look for when it comes to a face mask.

## 1. The First Thing to Look for is Your Skin Type

Even the best face mask in the world isn’t going to work for you if it’s designed for dry skin and you have oily skin. Evaluate the products at your disposal and make sure the one you ultimately choose is one that can help with your skin type, whether it’s greasy, dry or normal. Often, manufacturers sell the same face mask, but for each different skin type so you might be able to find your preferred mask in a different formulation.

## 2. A Gentle Exfoliator is Always a Good Bet

Exfoliation helps remove dead skin cells and built up product residue on your skin. That results in a glowing and radiant complexion when you wash the mask off. You don’t need anything too hefty because the skin on your face is so delicate, but a gentle bit of exfoliation in your face mask is a great buy!

## 3. A Facial Mask Should Always Be Moisturizing

Applying a facial mask should be done to infuse nutrients into your skin and leave it fresher and better than it was before. For that reason, your facial mask should be moisturizing. Look for one that promises to fight dry skin and pump your skin full of moisture so it looks young and clean at the same time.

## 4. Look for a Calming Mask to Fight All Kinds of Woes

A calming mask can soothe a wide range of skin issues, from redness to dry patches. Calming ingredients to look for include natural botanicals and aloe vera. You want to avoid additives and preservatives like parabens and fragrances, which can irritate skin and exacerbate skin issues. You’re going to love how much better your skin feels after using a calming mask!

## 5. A Renewal Mask Will Be Your New Best Friend

Here’s what’s so great about a renewal mask: it’s made to help your skin look younger than before you used it. At my age, I love that! Look for a renewal mask that fades age spots, battles wrinkles and fine lines and promotes that glow that makes you look years younger.

## 6. Feeling Lazy? Buy a Sleeping Mask and Let It do the Work for You

Let’s face it. At the end of the day, we all lack the energy to go through with a traditional face mask that must be applied, left to penetrate, then rinsed. That’s why you need one of the newfangled sleeping masks. They are designed to go on like a lotion or cream and then left on while you sleep. You want one that is pumped full of antioxidants and other nutrients that your skin needs to stay healthy and fresh.

## 7. Consider Eye Masks with Retinol for the Area around Your Eyes

Retinol is a miracle ingredient that can fight wrinkles and fine lines. It works by plumping the area, helping to keep your eye area smooth and young. You can find cream masks, but there are also patches that you apply to the area and allow to work before peeling them off. Which one would you choose?
