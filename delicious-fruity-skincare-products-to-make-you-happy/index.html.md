# 7 Delicious Fruity Skincare Products to Make You Happy ...

There’s no shortage of fruity skincare products out there, but sometimes fruity can be too much. I like the subtle and heavenly scents of fruit in my body products, but I don’t like to smell like a piece of candy. So, I’ve put together a short list of some of the best smelling fruity skincare products on the market to help you find some that you’ll love too. Please feel free to share your favorites with me if they haven't made the list.

## 1. Simi Essentials Sweet Pura Polish

![Simi Essentials Sweet Pura Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u6/dc/gkc0er2a_600x407.jpg)

This product is perfect for a quick home microdermabrasion treatment and it will leave you with just the right amount of fruitiness. It’ll gently remove all that rough dead skin from your body and leave you with youthful, fresh skin glowing through. All that and you’ll smell like pineapples too! That's why it makes the #1 spot on my list of fruity skincare products.

## 2. Meishoku DET Clear Bright & Peel Peeling Jelly

![Meishoku DET Clear Bright & Peel Peeling Jelly](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vg/vr/igx9kr82_211x569.jpg)

The mixed fruity smell of this facial product isn’t the only reason you should totally try this stuff. It works to remove blackheads and clear your pores. You can use it on all or just part of your face. The best part is that the fruity smell won’t overwhelm you – it’ll just relax you and leave you feeling fresh and beautiful.

## 3. Moisture Fruit Gel Cream

![Moisture Fruit Gel Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/0s/ko/g12jm6v2_260x255.jpg)

I like this cream because it contains a great blend of fruits that offer up a delicious aroma, but the antioxidants and other compounds found in those fruits also work to give you great looking skin. While you’re enjoying the scents of kiwi, grapefruit, apple, peach and raspberry, the cream goes to work moisturizing your skin and leaving it supple and beautiful. What more could you ask for?

## 4. Fake Bake Body Polish Passion Fruit

![Fake Bake Body Polish Passion Fruit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p7/x3/tfsg8a5o_292x292.jpg)

I love the smell of [passion fruit](https://food.allwomenstalk.com/wait-til-you-taste-these-passion-fruit-recipes/) and if you do too, this is the product for you. It’s the ideal choice if you use bottled tanner. It exfoliates your skin, leaving it fresh and ready to absorb your tanning cream. And it smells good too!

## 5. Kiehl’s Orange Flower and Lychee Softening Body Cleanser

![Kiehl’s Orange Flower and Lychee Softening Body Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f3/j1/dh7ri3ia_86x320.jpg)

If you tend to gravitate toward tropical smelling fruits, you’re going to absolutely love this spray. It’s used to cleanse your skin, but it’ll leave your entire body softened and smooth after each application. Close your eyes and you might just be able to feel a bit like you’re lying on the beach on some tropical island.

## 6. Alba Botanica Hawaiian Body Cream

![Alba Botanica Hawaiian Body Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bs/vr/gzkjeicv_450x321.jpg)

Hawaii is home to loads of fresh, delicious smelling fruits and this body cream captures two of my favorites – mango and papaya. The cream is perfect for softening and smoothing dry skin. It will get rid of those dreaded flakes and leave you with glowing, radiant skin in no time.

## 7. Laura Mercier Fresh Fig Crème Body Wash

![Laura Mercier Fresh Fig Crème Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rg/0z/vuxe1qig_287x297.jpg)

If you’re looking for a yummy and fruity smelling body wash to use in the shower, look no further than this one from Laura Mercier. It combines the lovely smell of fresh figs with coconut and even has undertones of apricot. It’s touted for cleaning your body without leaving your skin dry and with a scent like this, I think it’s a great choice for anyone.

What’s your favorite fruity smelling skincare product? I’m always on the lookout for new things to try so feel free to let me know why it’s so great.
