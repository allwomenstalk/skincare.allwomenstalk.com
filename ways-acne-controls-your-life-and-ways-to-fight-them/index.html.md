# 7 Ways Acne Controls Your Life and Ways to Fight Back ...

Acne can affect your life in many different ways. In fact, there may be times that you feel like it controls your life. But there are ways to deal with the different situations that acne makes you feel uncomfortable in. These are 7 ways acne may be controlling your life and some ways to fight back.

## 1. The Way You Wear Your Makeup

Acne may affect the way you wear your makeup. You may try to cover up your acne with your makeup or you may avoid wearing makeup at all in fear that it will make your acne worse. First, try to get you acne under control. If you’ve tried everything available over-the-counter then it may be time to see a dermatologist. It’s also good to note that there are some brands that offer makeup specifically for acne-prone skin.

## 2. Your Clothing Choices

Acne can control the clothing that you choose to wear. If you have acne on your chest or back then you may feel self-conscious about wearing clothing that shows those areas. While that’s understandable, it can help to remind yourself that acne is something many people deal with. This is especially true when you’re a teenager or young adult. You can decide not to let your acne hold you back and wear the clothing that you love.

## 3. Your Hairstyle

Acne can control the hairstyle that you wear. You may be choosing to wear a hairstyle that you really don’t like because it hides your acne or you feel like you can hide behind it. Don’t let acne box you in. There’re ways to deal with acne besides using your hair to hide it. Go for the hairstyle that you really love.

## 4. You Avoid Social Situations

Acne can control you by making you avoid social situations. You may feel so self-conscious that you avoid going out with friends or hanging out with others. This is where you have to realize something. You have acne but acne does not have you. You have to make a decision to not let acne control your social life.

## 5. It Affects Your Love Life

Acne can also affect your love life. You may be so embarrassed about your acne that you don’t think you can have a boyfriend. There may be guys that you’re interested in but you won’t let them know how you feel. Remember that your acne is something you have but it isn’t who you are. It’s also something that’ll most likely go away as you age into your early to mid-twenties.

## 6. You Avoid the Spotlight

Your acne may make you avoid the spotlight. You may have a secret desire to try out for school plays or to lead a group project but your acne holds you back. Here’s something to remember. Everyone is self-conscious about something. It’s also very likely that you notice your acne much more than other people notice it.

## 7. Your Self-esteem Takes a Hit

This is probably the worst thing about dealing with acne. Your self-esteem can take a big hit. While acne can be frustrating to deal with, remember that it’s just a skin issue, not who you are. You’re a beautiful, special person who isn’t going to let acne hold you back. You have to make the choice to remind yourself how wonderful you are.

These are 7 ways acne can control your life and some ways to fight back. How does acne most impact your life? Share your stories here.
