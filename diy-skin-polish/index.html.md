# DIY Skin Polish for a Gorgeous Glow ...

Summer is almost here. Are you looking for a **DIY skin polish**?
I came up with two easy homemade face polishes that you will love. Don't waste money on face and skin polishing products. There are no side effects and the ingredients are natural and beneficial for your skin.

## 1. Strawberry Skin Polish

Ingredients:
BROWN SUGAR
STRAWBERRIES
PLUM SEED OIL

Smooth strawberries until creamy and add the other ingredients. Mix them well.

Gently massage on clean skin. Avoid the eye area. Massage to increase the effect. Leave on for 5-10 minutes and then wash with warm water.

Another one that you will need only two ingredients for it:

## 2. Baking Soda and Coconut Oil

BAKING SODA
COCONUT OIL

Take 2 tbsp of baking soda and add 1 tbsp of coconut oil to this. Mix well. Put the creamy mixture on the palm of your hand and apply from your neck toward your face. Don't apply on eyelashes and eye area. Massage your face upward and in a circular direction. Apply to your nose as well to get rid of blackheads and dead skin.

If you have oily skin wash your face, then use only baking soda.

If you have dry skin use coconut oil as mentioned. Then wash your face. You will see your face glowing.

Use this DIY face polish only twice a week, not more. This is because baking soda is a little bit harsh for your skin.

Protect your skin from sunlight for 2-4 hours after polishing. It's better to do this before bedtime.
