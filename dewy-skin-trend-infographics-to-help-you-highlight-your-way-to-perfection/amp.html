Frequently Asked Questions

What is dewy skin?
Dewy skin is a fresh, glowing look characterized by a radiant and hydrated complexion.
How can I achieve dewy skin?
Use hydrating skincare products, lightweight moisturizers, and illuminating makeup to create a natural glow.
Which skincare ingredients help with dewy skin?
Look for hyaluronic acid, glycerin, and natural oils that boost hydration and shine.
Is dewy skin suitable for oily skin types?
Yes, but choose oil-free, non-comedogenic products to maintain shine without causing breakouts.
Can I achieve dewy skin without makeup?
Absolutely! Proper skincare, hydration, and healthy lifestyle habits can naturally enhance your skin's glow.
How do I highlight my face for a dewy look?
Apply a liquid or cream highlighter on the high points of your face like cheekbones, brow bones, and the bridge of the nose.
How often should I exfoliate for dewy skin?
Exfoliate 1-2 times a week to remove dead skin cells and maintain a smooth, glowing surface.
Can I get dewy skin in winter?
Yes, by using richer moisturizers and hydrating masks to combat dryness during colder months.
Are there any makeup mistakes to avoid with dewy skin?
Avoid heavy powders and matte foundations that can dull the glow; instead, choose sheer, luminous formulas.
Does diet affect achieving dewy skin?
Yes, staying hydrated and eating nutrient-rich foods like fruits and healthy fats supports radiant skin.
