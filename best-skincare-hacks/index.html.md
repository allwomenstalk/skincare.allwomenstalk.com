# Mind Blowing Skincare Hacks for Any Girl Who Wants Perfect Skin ...

The way you look is the first thing people notice about you, which is why I'm always on the hunt for the **best skincare hacks**. Why? Because when your skin is healthy, it makes you look youthful, glowing and beautiful. There are skincare tips all over the place, but you can't trust all of them. That's why I went to the experts and gathered this collection of **perfect skincare tips** for you.

## 1. Try Facial Massage

Skincare experts all over the world love what massage can do for your body. Facial massage is widely recognized as being one of the **best skincare hacks**. Massaging your skin boosts circulation, which helps give it that radiant glow you want so much. You can do a self massage anytime, anywhere. Use a gentle touch and massage in a circular motion for the best result.

## 2. Treat Acne with Honey and Aspirin

You don't have to load your medicine cabinet or shower shelf with a pile of skincare products to fight acne. Some simple products that you probably already own can make all the difference. Start by crushing an aspirin in a small amount of toner and apply the paste to your pimples. After five minutes, rinse it off and dab some honey on the same spots. Rinse after 10 minutes.

## 3. Grab the Yogurt for a Sunburn

It's summertime so getting a sunburn is always a possibility (though you should be wearing sunscreen to prevent it). If you've turned a little red, slather some plain yogurt on your skin. This will soothe irritated skin and help draw some of the heat out of the burn so you're more comfortable.

## 4. Make a DIY Face Peel

No more having to shell out big dollars to get a professional peel! This simple DIY face peel is fast and easy and you can do it all at home. Mix a couple of teaspoons of baking soda with a teaspoon of water. Add a bit of fresh lemon juice and make a paste. Spread the mixture on your face and let it sit for five minutes. Rinse with warm water and get ready for a glowing complexion.

## 5. Clean and Exfoliate at the Same Time

There's no reason why you can't kill two birds with one stone when it comes to skincare. This simple DIY cleanser also exfoliates, helping give you clean, clear skin every single day. Simply cut a lemon in half, sprinkle it with sugar and gently use it wash your face. Rinse and you're done.

## 6. Cut the Grease with Ice

If you have oily skin, you probably spend some time everyday blotting the excess shine and wishing for a magic cure to get rid of the problem. Next time oil is getting you down, try rubbing your skin with some ice cubes to soak up the grease and refresh your entire face.

## 7. Cool and Refresh Your Skin with Watermelon

Next time you peel a watermelon, hang on to those rinds. Experts say they are perfect for infusing skin with nutrients, cooling you off and refreshing your face. Gently rub a bit of the rind over your face, then rinse with cool water. Easy, right?
