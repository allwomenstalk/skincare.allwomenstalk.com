# 7 Essential Ways to Take Care of Your Skin ...

There are tons of ways to take care of your skin but not all of them are essential are they? Below, I've got the top 7 essential ways to take care of your skin that will make your skin glisten and shine brilliantly! So ladies, you ready to explore the top 7 ways to take care of your skin to make it beautiful?

## 1. Hydrate

When you are looking for the top ways to take care of your skin, the first step is to hydrate! Drinking water is going to be the best way to ensure that your skin looks beautiful and shines! Just a bottle of water can really make all of the difference in the world!

## 2. Moisturize

A lot of women actually forget that they are supposed to moisturize their skin everyday. Ladies, this is the next way to take care of your skin! For me, a like a moisturizer that is light but still works. Water-based moisturizers are the best ladies, keep that in mind!

## 3. Very Little Sun

While it is impossible to stay out of the sun completely – and who would want to! Getting very little sun or at least slathering up with a bit of sunblock is very, very important. You want to make sure that you are taking care of your skin and not letting it bake!

## 4. No Smoke

Smoking is something that can actually sink right into your pores and truly make your skin appear older than it actually is. Ladies, if you want a great way to take care of your skin, cutting out smoking will do it! It's also great for your health!

## 5. Limit Bath Time

This is one way to take care of your skin that I have a hard time with – I love my bath time! Limiting the time that you are soaking in hot or warm water will ensure that your skin doesn't lose a lot of moisture. Remember, after you get out of the bath, moisturize up so that way you can rehydrate a bit!

## 6. Avoid Strong Soaps

Another great way to take care of your skin is to avoid a lot of strong soaps, instead, make sure that you have a soap that is soft and that is full of moisture. That way you aren't losing out!

## 7. Eat Healthy

Finally ladies, another way to take care of your skin all boils down to your diet. Eating healthy with lots of fruits and veggies will keep your skin looking beautiful and glowing! I know that my skin feels so much better after I've eaten a healthy meal versus when I've eaten a bunch of fried foods.

There you have it ladies! The top 7 ways to take care of your skin that will ensure it stays beautiful all of the time! So, do you have any other ways to take care of your skin that I didn't cover? Sound off in the comments!
