6 How to Use

Using The Skin Infusion is straightforward yet precise. Begin with a clean slate-start with your favorite cleanser and toner. Apply a small amount of The Skin Infusion in upward, sweeping motions over your face, neck, and décolleté. Do this twice daily, morning and evening, consistently for four weeks. Each bottle is designed to be used within one month to ensure its potency, so don’t dawdle. Follow up with your go-to serum and moisturizer for maximum effect. For those needing an extra boost, consider using this regimen 3-4 times a year, especially during season changes or after exposure to harsh conditions.
