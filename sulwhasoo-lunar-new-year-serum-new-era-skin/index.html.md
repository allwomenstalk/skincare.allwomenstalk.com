Sulwhasoo was among the first to recognize its transformative potential in skincare, elevating it from a traditional remedy to a modern luxury. Today, the brand’s proprietary research culminates in the Sulwhasoo Master Complex™, a refined blend that harnesses the power of ginseng to strengthen the skin’s natural abilities - a phrase that sounds deceptively simple, yet carries profound implications.

Because truly exceptional skincare does not overwhelm the skin. It teaches it.
