# 7 Best Sunscreens That Baby Your Skin ...

Sun protection is a must, but that doesn’t mean sacrificing your skin, so I’ve collected some of the best sunscreens that baby your skin. Not only do they offer you the protection from sunburn and aging that you want, but they don’t make your skin oily, dry or otherwise different. Always wear sunscreen on exposed skin any time you spend time outside. You’ll stave off skin cancer and keep the signs of aging at bay. I know it takes a couple of minutes, but wearing the best sunscreens that baby your skin will make it a bit more enjoyable.

## 1. Citrix Vitamin C Antioxidant Sunscreen

![Citrix Vitamin C Antioxidant Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/sl/n21203ku_210x233.jpg)

If that filmy residue that lots of sunscreen leave behind is something that keeps you from wearing it, this is the answer. This is one of the best sunscreens that baby your skin. It rubs on smooth and leaves your skin feeling the way you want it to, but it also pumps your dermis full of vitamin C, which is an antioxidant that helps protect you from skin cancer.

## 2. Neutrogena Sensitive Skin Sunscreen

![Neutrogena Sensitive Skin Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/va/vh/vgslo86k_397x397.jpg)

Sensitive skin people unite! Neutrogena is a well-known skincare brand that comes highly ranked by dermatologists. Use this sunscreen for all your sun protection needs without worrying about irritating your skin. This super-light formula blends in easily and smoothly and protects you from both UVA and UVB rays so you can feel good about your time in the sunshine.

## 3. CeraVe Sunscreen Face Lotion

![CeraVe Sunscreen Face Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lk/vo/oq2mrtft_393x393.jpg)

I like to use a face specific sunscreen on my face because the skin is so delicate. If you want to baby your facial skin too, try this great product from CeraVe. It’s affordable, but it’s developed by dermatologists so you know you can count on it for all your sun protection needs. It’s lightweight and protects the skin on your face with daily wear. Slick it on under your make-up and you’re good to go.

## 4. Kiss My Face Sunscreen with Oat Protein Complex

![Kiss My Face Sunscreen with Oat Protein Complex](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ta/le/08miqzb0_400x400.jpg)

It sounds high end, but this sunscreen is super-affordable and comes from a reputable skincare company. This a paraben-free formula so if you want something natural, this is the product for you. At the same time, the oat protein nourishes and moisturizes your skin while it protects you from the sun.

## 5. Clarins Sunscreen for Face Wrinkle Control Cream

![Clarins Sunscreen for Face Wrinkle Control Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t4/hi/y1kplp3p_300x288.jpg)

What could be better than sun protection mixed with wrinkle control? Not a heck of a lot, if you ask me. That’s what makes this sunscreen such a great choice. It has an SPF30 that blocks the sun’s rays, but helps battle wrinkles too. So if you want to preserve your radiant young skin, I suggest giving this a try on your face.

## 6. Aveeno Baby Natural Protection Lotion Sunscreen

![Aveeno Baby Natural Protection Lotion Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ee/a4/d7u9uh5p_412x412.jpg)

Whether you have kids or not, this sunscreen is great for babying your skin. Babies have naturally delicate skin, so you can often use infant products to protect your sensitive skin. Aveeno is a high-end brand that won’t break the bank, but is well known for its many benefits. This sunscreen is great for your whole body and will give you peace of mind when it comes to sun protection.

## 7. Coppertone Sensitive Skin Sunscreen

![Coppertone Sensitive Skin Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hz/ma/ze2wjg7a_450x450.jpg)

Coppertone is a brand that’s super simple to find at most drugstores or supermarkets. That’s because it’s so popular due to its well-known benefits. Use this product on your face anytime you head outdoors and you’ll never have to worry about breakouts or skin irritation ever again. This is my go-to product!
