# 7 Creams for Soft Skin That Work like a Charm ...

With all of the different creams for soft skin out there, how can you possibly figure out what creams are the best? I’ve got really dry skin, especially in the winter and if you are anything like me, you are constantly on the lookout for creams for soft skin and that will get rid of that dry skin. Well girlies, I’ve got all of the best creams for soft skin below. I’ve tried each and every one of them and I like them for different reasons. Take a look!

## 1. Lubriderm Daily Moisture Shea + Enriching Cocoa Butter Lotion

![Lubriderm Daily Moisture Shea + Enriching Cocoa Butter Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095334_6097_299x293.jpg)

Lubriderm is a brand that I’ve grown to trust over the years and the reason for that is because it delivers on exactly what it says: it gets rid of of dry skin! This is one of my favorite creams for soft skin and it’s not greasy at all. I like to use it on my legs as it leaves them feeling absolutely luscious and oh-so-soft!

## 2. Jergens Ultra Healing Lotion

![Jergens Ultra Healing Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095257_3914_599x575.jpg)

While I am dwelling away in my cubicle at work, I have found that I pick at my fingers a lot. Do you do that? Pick at hang nails and cuticles all of the time? Well, that’s when I decided to invest in this amazing-smelling lotion! This lotion not only makes my hands super soft, but I use it on my arms too! It’s not greasy and within minutes of putting it on, I can type and answer the phone without a problem.

## 3. Nivea Smooth Sensation

![Nivea Smooth Sensation](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095257_8339_599x587.jpg)

This is my body cream! I use this for my shoulders and for my stomach – even my feet! It’s a cream that is not greasy (I hate greasy lotions and creams!) and it absorbs super-fast. If you’re looking for a cream that is thick and that will deliver on knocking that dry skin out of there, this is the cream for you!

## 4. Olay Ultra Moisture in-Shower Body Lotion

![Olay Ultra Moisture in-Shower Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095256_1349_499x488.jpg)

I’ve never even thought about how much moisture we lose in the shower, just from our soap. Have you ever thought about moisturizing while you are inside the shower? Olay did! They came up with this in-shower lotion that is honest to gosh, amazing! Just spread it on and leave it on – you’ll feel hydrated and your skin will look awesome.

## 5. Eucerin Skin Calming Daily Crème

![Eucerin Skin Calming Daily Crème](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095256_6428_409x387.jpg)

If you have a really bad flare up of dry skin that you can’t seem to get rid of, this is the cream for you. This cream is designed to calm down your skin, to really penetrate deeply and it will get to the root of your skin’s problem. If you’re dying to kick the flakes of dry skin, this is the cream for you.

## 6. Aveeno Daily Moisturizing Lotion

![Aveeno Daily Moisturizing Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095258_2154_599x628.jpg)

Aveeno lotion has always been greasy – until they came out with this particularly amazing lotion! This lotion is all-natural and it’s designed to keep your skin hydrated for hours after you put it on. What could be better? I use this lotion for my back.

## 7. Burt’s Bees Soothingly Sensitive Aloe & Buttermilk Lotion

![Burt’s Bees Soothingly Sensitive Aloe & Buttermilk Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/10/16095257_9752_459x395.jpg)

If you are looking for a lotion that is completely all natural – this is it. The smell alone is something that you’ll fall deeply in love with and girlies, it’s an amazing lotion. It moisturizes, it soothes and it’ll keep your skin looking amazing.

Now you have all of the best creams out there that will keep your skin so soft. Do you have any more creams you use? Give ‘em up! I’m always looking to switch!
