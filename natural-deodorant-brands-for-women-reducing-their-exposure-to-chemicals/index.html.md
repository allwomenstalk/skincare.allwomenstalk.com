# Natural Deoderants for Women Tossing the Chemicals in Their Life ...

I recently wrote an article about the benefits of natural deodorants. [It's Time to Consider Switching to a Natural Deodorant ... @Neecey](https://skincare.allwomenstalk.com/its-time-to-consider-switching-to-a-natural-deodorant/) I thought it would useful to follow it up with natural deodorants brands for those of you who are considering making the switch. They are becoming more widely available and this is just a selection.

## 1. Dr Hauschka

![Dr Hauschka](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x8/i2/xamz5psr57334048d239a589110331_500x445.jpg)

This one is expensive so you have to expect it to work. Available in two formulations: Rose and Sage Mint. Neither contain aluminum.
[dr.hauschka.com](http://blacklist.no-tins.com/?r=7&h=www.dr.hauschka.com&q=/en_US/natural-skin-care-products/sage-mint-deodorant/%3f)

$5.99

$7.49
