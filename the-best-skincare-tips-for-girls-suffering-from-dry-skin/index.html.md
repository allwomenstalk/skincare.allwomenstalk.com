# The Best Skincare Tips for Girls Suffering from Dry Skin ...

When it comes to common skin complaints that many of us have to contend with, dryness is probably one of the top three on the list. You might think that oiliness is a worse thing to have to deal with, but if you have ever had to go through it, you will know just how debilitating it can be. Of course, like with any other type of skin problem, you can keep things at bay if you know what kind of action to take. Here are some of the **best skincare tips for dry skin**.

## 1. Drink Water

Something you can do easily is make sure you stay nice and hydrated by drinking your recommended eight glasses of water per day. Your skin is the biggest organ your body has, so the better you can replenish and moisturise it, the less dry it will tend to be.

## 2. Lukewarm Wash

Another great trick is giving your face a wash with lukewarm water rather than cold or super hot. This gentle temperature prevents the extreme damage from both ends of the spectrum, avoiding things like redness and irritation. The more irritated your skin it, the more likely it is to be dry and flaky.

## 3. Face Wash

Consider the type of face wash that you use in your regular routine. Anyone with a specific skin problem needs to make sure they have a product that is designed specially to help them, so in your case, make sure that you have a face wash product that is designed specifically with dryness in mind.

## 4. Oils

Think about using various oils to counteract the natural dryness of your skin. There are plenty of serums on the market that are perfect for applying to dry skin to put some moisture back into it, but that don’t go overboard and end up doing things like clogging your pores and creating an issue at the other end of the spectrum.

## 5. Humidifier

It might be worth getting a humidifier for your home. They are nifty little gadgets that release moisturising steam into the environment to create a much kinder atmosphere for your dry skin. Combining a humidifier with one or any other of the tips and you will be doing great things for your skin both from the inside and the outside.
