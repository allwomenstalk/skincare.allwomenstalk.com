# 8 Helpful Ways to Prevent and Fade Acne Scars ...

If you’re looking for ways to prevent acne scars or any fade any existing ones, you’ve come to right place! Acne is probably one of the biggest skin concerns of women everywhere and it’s not that uncommon to get some scarring as a result. And while we might be prone to having acneic skin, we don’t have to settle for having unsightly acne scars! Check out these 8 useful ways to prevent and fade acne scars now!

## 1. Lemon Juice

Using lemon juice is one of the easiest and most economical ways to prevent acne scars! All you have to do is slice up a lemon and squeeze the juice into a bowl. Next, add water to the bowl and mix thoroughly. Grab a washcloth, soak part of it in the liquid and wring it out. Apply the washcloth to your skin and repeat daily.

## 2. Prevent Picking

A sure way to prevent acne scars is to prevent any picking at pimples! While there are different types of acne that cause scarring, you can bet that picking at your skin is a surefire way to inflame your skin and possibly cause scarring. Resist the temptation and keep your fingers away no matter how much you want to pop!

## 3. Olive Oil

Olive oil isn’t just for salads and sautéing; it also provides a way to prevent acne scars! Grab some olive oil from the kitchen and simply rub it into skin about 4-5 times each day. Frequently applying olive oil can help repair damaged skin and prevent scarring.

## 4. Avoid Sun

Avoid spending prolonged periods of time in the sun as a way to prevent acne scars. The sun can certainly make acne and existing acne scars much more noticeable, although some people mistakenly believe the opposite is true. So prevent increasing pigmentation by applying sunblock regularly before you head out the door!

## 5. Be Proactive

A great way to prevent acne scars and discoloration is to treat it as soon as possible. Don’t wait until your pimples get bigger before you use any acne medication, apply it ASAP! By treating your acne from the get-go, you’re helping your pimple from getting more severe and preventing any potential scarring!

## 6. Apple Cider Vinegar

A natural way to fade acne scars is to use some apple cider vinegar. It’s smelly, but it contains antiseptic and antibiotic properties that can help reduce acne scarring and draw out toxins from your skin. Apply a small amount of ACV on scarred skin or use it as a toner by mixing one part water to one part ACV. Keep in mind that using ACV straight can be a bit strong for some skin types so you might want to dilute it even more to prevent drying.

## 7. Eat Clean

Eat a clean diet as a way to nourish your skin and as a way to prevent acne scars. It might not make sense but eating an unbalanced diet with processed foods doesn’t do you or your skin any favors! Help your skin stay clear and heal faster by eating a healthy diet of fruits, veggies and whole grains.

## 8. Dermabrasion

If you want to know how to fade acne scars, a pretty effective but more invasive way to do it is to try dermabrasion or dermaplaning. Dermabrasion is a method of controlled surgical scraping –sounds crazy, I know! But, removing layers of your skin can improve the look of skin and make it smoother.

As you can see, ways to prevent acne scars don’t have to be difficult or expensive (except for the dermabrasion)! The best way to prevent acne scars is to treat acne as soon as possible but if your acne is getting worse or you’re suddenly getting acne, talk to a dermatologist. Do you know of any good ways to prevent or fade acne scars?
