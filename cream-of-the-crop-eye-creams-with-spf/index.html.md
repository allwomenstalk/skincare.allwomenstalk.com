# 7 Cream of the Crop Eye Creams with SPF ...

If you’re serious about anti-aging products, you’ll definitely want to look over some of these top-of-the-line eye creams with SPF. Even if you’re not currently wearing any eye cream during the day, once you find out all of the awesome beauty benefits that these eye creams can offer, you’ll be hooked. Not only will you be hydrating and nourishing your sensitive eye area, you’ll get the added benefit of sunscreen so you’ll be protected from the sun’s damaging rays for those times you’re not wearing your sunnies. If you want to learn more about the excellent benefits of eye creams with SPF, keep reading!

## 1. Supergoop! Advanced anti-Aging Eye Cream Broad Spectrum Sunscreen SPF 37

![Supergoop! Advanced anti-Aging Eye Cream Broad Spectrum Sunscreen SPF 37](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v7/se/m97wpjcy_450x444.jpg)

I love Supergoop!’s sunscreen serum so I knew they would make one of the best eye creams with SPF. Their anti-aging eye cream contains SPF 37, and moisturizes and reduces the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles and puffiness, in addition to cooling the skin around your eyes with its applicator. It’s also gluten-free and safe for sensitive skin! Available at Sephora for $45.

## 2. Dior Hydra Life BB Eye Crème

![Dior Hydra Life BB Eye Crème](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/jg/dp6u8zgm_450x448.jpg)

Now this is an eye cream with many benefits! Think of it as a BB crème just for your eye area. Using Dior’s patented Dior Eye Revival technology, it helps get rid of dark circles and puffiness while hydrating, correcting and brightening your eyes in one product. It also contains SPF 20 to protect your eyes from environmental stress and some ladies have been able to replace their concealer with this eye cream so it’s pretty powerful stuff. Available at Sephora for $45.

## 3. Smashbox Camera Ready BB Cream Eyes SPF 15

![Smashbox Camera Ready BB Cream Eyes SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b8/oj/enivooa5_460x385.jpg)

Smashbox makes their own multitasking eye product - Camera Ready BB eye cream. This one product replaces eye cream, concealer, foundation and sunscreen so your eyes will be illuminated and youthful-looking, no matter how little you slept the night before. With the handy pen applicator, you just swipe it on, blend and go! Available in four shades at Ulta for $25.

## 4. Clinique Superdefense SPF 20 Age Defense Eye Cream

![Clinique Superdefense SPF 20 Age Defense Eye Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/if/qo/h3vhxpmr_450x436.jpg)

Clinique is just as famous for their skin care as they are their makeup, so you can count on them to bring you an effective eye cream. Use this eye cream underneath makeup or alone to keep eyes hydrated, healthy-looking and protected from the sun and environmental pollutants. Ophthalmologist tested and oil-free. Available at Sephora for $39.50.

## 5. Shiseido Sun Protection Eye Cream Broad Spectrum SPF 34 Sunscreen

![Shiseido Sun Protection Eye Cream Broad Spectrum SPF 34 Sunscreen](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nt/vd/z17ezyrl_450x448.jpg)

Although the packaging makes it look more like a sunscreen than an eye cream, it’s able to do the job of both without leaving any telltale white residue or stickiness on the skin. This eye cream is perfect for those days you’ll be out and about and need a luxurious eye cream and sunscreen that can stand up to the sun, sweat and water. Available at Sephora for $35.50.

## 6. BareMinerals 5 in 1 BB Advanced Performance Cream Eyeshadow

![BareMinerals 5 in 1 BB Advanced Performance Cream Eyeshadow](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rm/ua/inb6fn3b_450x442.jpg)

As you can probably tell by the name, this isn’t one of your average eye creams with SPF. This is actually an eye shadow with all the benefits of a BB cream including SPF 15. Your under eye area needs sun protection, but this product has your eyelids covered. This multitasker will prime, protect, smooth fine lines, brighten and provide 12 hours of wear. Available at BareEscentuals.com for $18.

## 7. Estee Lauder Double Wear Stay-in-Place High Cover Concealer Broad Spectrum SPF 35

![Estee Lauder Double Wear Stay-in-Place High Cover Concealer Broad Spectrum SPF 35](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h9/g3/a8fqx4f7_250x245.jpg)

The last product I wanted to show you is a concealer with SPF. Estee Lauder’s Double Wear Stay-in-Place High Cover Concealer lasts for 12 long hours, controls oil for 4 hours, minimizes the look of fine lines and wrinkles and is the most lightweight and concentrated concealer you’ll use. So, if you forget to use an eye cream with SPF underneath, this concealer will have you covered. Available at Nordstrom for $24.

Try one of these seven products to keep your eye area looking young and healthy. You don’t want to wait until you see fine lines and wrinkles before you start using an eye cream. Do you currently wear an eye cream during the day?
