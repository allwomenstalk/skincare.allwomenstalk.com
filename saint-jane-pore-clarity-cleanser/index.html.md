Let's dissect the magic behind this cleanser. **Hyaluronic Acid**, a staple in skincare, does wonders here. It's the hydration hero, pulling moisture into your skin, preventing that tight, dry feeling post-cleanse. Imagine your skin as a sponge; hyaluronic acid ensures it's always well-saturated.

Next up, **Glycolic Acid AHA**, the gentle exfoliator derived from sugar cane. It sloughs off dead skin cells, revealing a brighter, more even complexion. Think of it as your skin’s reset button - fresh and ready.
