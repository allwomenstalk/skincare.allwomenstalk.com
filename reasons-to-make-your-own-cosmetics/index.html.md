# 7 Reasons to Make Your Own Cosmetics ...

What are a few of the many the reasons to make your own cosmetics? We're used to buying ready-made cosmetics, which is certainly convenient. But making your own creams and soaps is kinder for your skin, and may work out to be a lot more budget-friendly. There are many other reasons why making your own products is a good idea. Here are some reasons to make your own cosmetics …

## 1. Purity

One of the reasons to make your own cosmetics is that the products will be pure. Commercial products are full of chemicals that are bad for the environment and possibly for your skin as well. Making your own products means that you know exactly what has gone into them. It will also simplify your beauty routine; we don't really need dozens of products.

## 2. Cheaper

You can also save a lot of money by making your own cosmetics. With a few basic ingredients you can make a lot of different products, and you can buy many of the ingredients cheaply. For the same price as one moisturizer you can buy ingredients that will make enough products to last for years. You can also make products as gifts that people will love.

## 3. No Animal Testing

Another major advantage is that any products you make won't have been tested on animals. It's just not right that animals should suffer so that we can wear cosmetics and slap creams on our faces. Making your own products means less support for companies that use animal testing on their products.

## 4. Allergies

If you have skin allergies or sensitivities, it can be difficult to find products that you don't react to. It's really annoying having to check the ingredients to see if the product contains something that'll break you out in hives. Plus you can never be sure that you won't react to something else. Make your own products, and you can avoid any nasty skin problems.

## 5. Kinder to Your Skin

Even if you don't have skin problems, homemade cosmetics are much kinder to your skin. They're also better for the environment; lots of commercial products contain nasty chemicals and the production process causes environmental damage. Your skin will be healthier if you don't put chemicals on it. In the past people had to make their own cosmetics, and they often had better skin.

## 6. Customize

Another advantage of making your own cosmetics is that you can customize them to your liking. You can add your favorite essential oils so that the product smells just how you want, rather than having to check commercial products until you find one you like. You can also use ingredients with properties that you need, or that you know you won't react to.

## 7. Hype

Most commercial products don't actually live up to their advertising. It's all hype. So why spend money on something that's no better than anything you could make? Invest the money into buying the basic ingredients; they'll last for ages and you're not encouraging companies to produce more 'miracle' creams.

Once you start making your own cosmetics you'll love the results, and so will your skin. You'll probably make a few mistakes, but you'll soon learn how to get it right. Your skin doesn't need expensive products that don't deliver what they promise. How much do you spend on cosmetics and toiletries every year?
