# 17 Life Saving Skincare Products for Girls Suffering from Dry Skin ...

The dry skin struggle is real. I'm one of those lucky girls with skin that runs the gamut between dry and oily, but it's the dry skin that really drives me insane. I typically get it around my mouth, chin, and eyebrows. I'll slather on moisturizer – which invariably clogs up my pores – and then apply makeup, only to discover that my dreaded dry skin shows through it and looks horrible. Awful! My latest quest has thus involved finding products for dry skin that are not greasy or oily so that I don't have to trade ugly dry patches for big, juicy zits. So far, these are the products that please my bitchy, stubborn skin the most.
