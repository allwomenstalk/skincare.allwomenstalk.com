# Resolutions to Make if You Want to Have Beautiful Skin in 2024 ...

It might already be half way through January, but it is never too late to start making resolutions! In fact, making a few slightly later can be better, because it means that you are really determined to get them completed! For lots of people, something that they can always stand to improve on is the way that they treat their skin. Here are some great resolutions to have beautiful skin in 2024.

## 1. Protection

Make the resolution to use sunscreen all year round rather than only in the two hottest summer months. Temperature doesn’t have an impact on the power of UV rays, so even if you are enjoying a cold sunny day in February, you still need something protecting your skin!

## 2. Hydration

Join the many millions of people all over the world who are taking up hitting their daily water drinking goals as a main resolution. Getting those eight recommended glasses per day is essential not only for your overall health but specifically for the state of your skin. It’s the best way to moisturise from the inside out!

## 3. Purge

It’s time to go through your cabinets and throw out any skincare products that have moved past their use by dates. They might look and smell fine, but some of the important active ingredients may have broken down, therefore leaving them useless and no beneficial at all to your skincare routine.

## 4. Dermatologist

If you want to start the year off on the right foot, you could always make an appointment to see a dermatologist for a thorough skin check. A medical professional will be able to give you lots of insight regarding the state of your skin, and will be able to highlight areas for you that you should be working hardest on for the year ahead. There is so much cool technology at their disposal these days!

## 5. Routine

You should be trying to commit to a regular routine as early in the year as you can, because your skin is something that likes a pattern. Find your core products and stick to them, because changing brands and ingredients and types too often is only going to confuse your skin and not allow it to settle and flourish like a solid schedule would.
