# 9 Best Acne Skincare Products for Back to School Beauty ...

Adolescence is a phase of great physical, psychological, and emotional turmoil. Adding further complication to this bumpy ride, are little bumpy spots that emerge on your skin – acne. And if not treated well, acne can leave behind scars, not only on your skin, but your confidence and self-esteem as well!

**What is Acne?**
During the [phase of adolescence](https://www.webmd.com/skin-problems-and-treatments/features/bad-habits), the body is undergoing great hormonal changes. Due to the increased production of testosterone during this period, there is an excess production of sebum by the sebaceous glands.

This excess sebum can often lead to clogging of your skin pores with sebum and keratin, resulting in the formation of acne, which can present as whiteheads, blackheads, or even cysts or pustules.

**What Causes Acne?**

Increased [sebum production](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sebaceous_gland) due to hormonal changes. These hormonal changes may be due to:

- Puberty
- Birth control pills
- Menstrual periods
- Pregnancy
- Oil-based cosmetics can also promote acne by clogging down your skin pores
- Heredity also has a role to play in acne
