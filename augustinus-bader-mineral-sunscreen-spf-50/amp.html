Frequently Asked Questions

What's so special about mineral sunscreen?
Oh, mineral sunscreen is fabulous because it uses natural ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide to protect your skin. It's like wearing a shield against the sun without all those chemicals that can irritate your skin.
Why should I choose a sunscreen with SPF 50?
SPF 50 is great because it offers high protection, blocking about 98% of UVB rays. It's perfect if you're planning to spend a lot of time outdoors or have sensitive skin that easily burns. Better safe than sorry, right?
Is there a best time to apply sunscreen?
Applying sunscreen should become a daily habit, just like brushing your teeth. Put it on about 15 minutes before you head outside, so it has time to sink in and get working. And don't forget to reapply every two hours or after swimming or sweating!
Can mineral sunscreen be used on all skin types?
Yes, indeed! Mineral sunscreen is generally gentle and suitable for all skin types, even sensitive or easily irritated skin. If you struggle with breakouts or allergies, mineral formulas might be your new best friend.
How does Augustinus Bader's sunscreen stand out from others?
Besides the effective SPF 50 protection, Augustinus Bader's sunscreen is packed with skin-loving ingredients that nourish and protect your skin. It's like a multi-tasker that leaves your skin happy and healthy. If you’ve tried it, you probably know what I mean!
