Get Flawless Skin with These Homemade Exfoliators ...

For Your Lips • Raspberry Lemon Sugar Scrub • Lemon Honey Sugar Scrub • Mint Sugar Scrub • Apple Cinnamon Sugar Scrub • More ...

By Eliza

Exfoliating is an important step in your skincare routine. It works to remove dead skin cells, built up debris, leftover products and toxins from the environment from your pores, helping prevent blemishes and breakouts. There are lots of exfoliators on store shelves, but you can also make them yourself. Here are some great recipes that will help you get the most glorious skin ever.

1. For Your Lips

You should definitely exfoliate your lips regularly to keep them smooth and soft.
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon petroleum jelly
1 small chunk of your favorite lip balm
1 small handful white sugar
Directions:
Combine the ingredients in a small cup.
Source: queenofallyousee.com

2. Raspberry Lemon Sugar Scrub

The scent of this is fabulous and you'll love how smooth your skin is after using it.
Ingredients:
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup melted coconut oil
1/8 teaspoon raspberry extract
1/8 teaspoon lemon extract
Red food coloring, optional
Directions:
Mix together all of the ingredients in a small bowl.
Source: bespangledjewelry.blogspot.com

3. Lemon Honey Sugar Scrub

Honey is great for your skin so adding it to an exfoliator is a great ideas.
Ingredients:
1 cup cane sugar
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tablespoons raw honey
2 teaspoons dried rosemary
15 drops lemon essential oil
15 drops lavender essential oil
Directions:
Mix together the sugar, oil, honey and rosemary. Drizzle in the essential oils and mix again.
Source: homemademommy.net

4. Mint Sugar Scrub

The scent and feel of the mint will give you a tingle that makes this one perfect.
Ingredients:
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 tablespoon peppermint extract
1 drop green food coloring
Directions:
Soften the coconut oil and combine it with the sugar, peppermint extract and food coloring. Mix well to combine.
Source: lovegrowswild.com

5. Apple Cinnamon Sugar Scrub

The scent of this scrub is enough to make it your favorite beauty product.
Ingredients:
1 cup coconut oil
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 medium apple
Cinnamon extract
Sprinkle of cinnamon powder
Directions:
Puree the apple in the food processor. Combine the apple with the rest of the ingredients and store in jars.
Source: simplyshellie.com

6. Snow Cone Sugar Scrub

You are going to love how this scrub makes your skin feel.
Ingredients:
9 tablespoons coconut oil
6 teaspoons almond oil
1 cup sugar
Directions:
Divide each ingredient in thirds and combine one-third of each in three small bowls. Add a drop of food coloring to each bowl and then layer the three colors in a small jar.
Source: thegunnysack.com

7. Strawberry Sugar Scrub

There's nothing better than strawberries and they have natural exfoliation properties. You are going to love this scrub!
Ingredients:
3/4 cup coconut oil
1/4 cup sweet almond oil
1/2 cup powdered strawberries
2 cups sugar
4 teaspoons strawberry extract
Directions:
Whip the coconut oil for 5 minutes. Beat in the almond oil. Add the strawberries in sections until you have a 1/2 cup of powder. In another bowl, combine the rest of the powdered strawberries with the sugar. Combine the two mixtures, one half at a time, then add the strawberry extract.
Source: thegunnysack.com

8. Chocolate Sugar Scrub

Any chocolate lover will adore using this delicious scrub on their skin.
Ingredients:
1/2 cup raw cane sugar
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 tablespoon cocoa baking powder
1/4 cup almond oil
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
A few drops vitamin E oil
Directions:
Combine the sugars and cocoa powder in a bowl and then add the oil until you get a good consistency. Add the vanilla and vitamin E oil.
Source: theidearoom.net

9. Vanilla Pumpkin Sugar Scrub

This is the perfect treat to baby autumn skin, but you can use it year round too.
Ingredients:
2 cups sugar
1/2 cup coconut oil
1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
Directions:
Combine the oil with the sugar and spices and store in a jar.
Source: smartschoolhouse.com

10. Almond and Floral Body Scrub

The heavenly scent and lovely look of this scrub makes it perfect for yourself and to give as gifts.
Ingredients:
1 cup sea salt
1/2 cup sweet almond oil
6 drops floral oil
1/4 cup lavender buds
Directions:
Combine the ingredients to create the scrub.
Source: liagriffith.com

11. Basil Lime Sugar Scrub

You are not going to be able to get enough of this marvelous body scrub.
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
6 chopped basil leaves
Zest and juice of 1/2 a lime
1 teaspoon coconut oil
Directions:
Whisk together the ingredients in a bowl and store them in a jar.
Source: busycreatingmemories.com

12. Oatmeal Cookie Hand Scrub

Use this to keep your hands looking young, fresh and smooth.
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups oatmeal
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon
1/2 cup coconut oil
Directions:
Pulse the oatmeal in a blender until it's powder. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir until well combined.
Source: amomstake.com

13. Pink Lemonade Sugar Scrub

This exfoliator is probably my favorite! It smells yummy and will totally revolutionize the look and feel of your skin.
Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1/2 cup coconut oil
1/2 teaspoon lemon essential oil
Red food coloring
Directions:
Melt the coconut oil in the microwave. Add the sugar and mix well. Add the essential oil and food coloring and combine.
Source: sparklesofsunshine.com

14. Orange Creamsicle Sugar Scrub

The wonderful smell of this will bring back memories of your childhood and those tasty orange popsicles you loved so much.
Ingredients:
1/4 cup coconut oil
3/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
10 drops orange essential oil
Directions:
Melt the coconut oil and combine it with the rest of the ingredients.
Source: thegunnysack.com

15. Rose Petal Sugar Scrub

Roses are a wonderful ingredient for adding color and scent to your homemade exfoliator.
Ingredients:
1 cup dried rose petals
2 cups sugar
2/3 cup coconut oil
6 to 8 drops essential oils
Directions:
Crush the rose petals in a food processor and then combine with the rest of the ingredients.
Source: happinessishomemade.net

16. Grapefruit Sugar Scrub

I love the refreshing scent of grapefruit and it works wonderfully for a homemade scrub.
Ingredients:
1 grapefruit
1 cup sugar
1 to 3 tablespoons apricot oil
Directions:
Remove the skin from 1/2 the grapefruit and combine in the food processor with the sugar and oil to reach the desired consistency.
Source: dearcrissy.com

17. Snickerdoodle Sugar Scrub

Any cookie lover will adore having this sugar scrub in her medicine cabinet.
Ingredients:
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup coconut oil
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
Directions:
Combine all of the ingredients in a small bowl, then store in small jars.
Source: krystalskitsch.com
