# How Does Buriti Oil Help Your Skin?

Using oil on your skin is trending big time and it has been for quite some time. It appears the practice is here to stay. For good reason too. Applying oil to your skin infuses it with compounds that boost its health and also pump it with hydration. Really, there’s no reason not to be using oils on your skin. Buriti oil is one of the newest and can really do wonders for your skin. Check out all the perks and I think you’ll be rushing out to get some in no time.

## 1. Buriti Oil Comes from an Amazonian Palm Tree

The best oils come from plants, which are loaded with antioxidants and compounds that can really benefit your skin. Buriti oil is no exception. It comes from the fruit that grows on a palm tree that’s native to the Amazon. That’s how you know it contains a wealth of good for you skin compounds!

## 2. Buriti Oil is Full of Antioxidants and Fatty Acids

Your skin relies on both antioxidants and fatty acids to stay hydrated and moisturized. By smoothing on buriti oil, you infuse your entire body with both of these outstanding items. In fact, research shows that people who use buriti oil on their skin experience a dramatic softening of dry, rough patches while also increasing the moisture in your skin.

## 3. You Don’t Want Pure Buriti Oil

Sounds weird, since products that are touted as pure are often better. In the case of buriti oil however, you want to use it mixed with other natural oils. When it’s collected, the buriti oil can be orange to yellow in color and that tint can transfer to your skin. When you use it combined with other skin oils, the effects are erased so you won’t look like a walking Cheeto.

## 4. Buriti Oil Meets a Lot of Needs at One Time

Because of the high concentration of antioxidants and fatty acids in buriti oil, it plays a number of roles in the health of your skin. It helps increase the production of collagen and elastin, protects your skin from free radical damage, reduces your risk of developing skin cancer due to sun damage, and generally makes your skin look and feel younger and more radiant. What more could a girl ask for?

## 5. You Can Add Buriti Oil with Your Typical Skincare Products

While you can certainly slather on buriti oil separate from your regime of other lotions and potions, you can also simply add a few drops to the night cream, day cream and other products that you generally use to save yourself a step and help ensure that the oil is evenly distributed over your skin.

## 6. Buriti Oil Can Treat Specific Skin Issues

Do you suffer from exceptionally dry skin or do you have really sensitive skin? Maybe you experience redness, irritation or rashes on a regular basis. Turns out buriti oil can help combat all of these skin woes. That’s because, in addition to the skin health benefits of the compounds in buriti oil, it also has anti-inflammatory properties that can counteract issues you deal with on your skin. Talk to your dermatologist to find out if buriti oil is right for your skin condition.

## 7. Buriti Oil is Also Highly Effective on Your Hair

Now that you’ve got your skin in tip top shape, start using buriti oil in your hair too. It can fight off dry, brittle strands and help leave your mane gloriously smooth, shiny and healthy. Talk about a double duty product, huh?

Have you ever heard of buriti oil? Tried it? Do you want to give it a try now that you’ve found out how great it is?
