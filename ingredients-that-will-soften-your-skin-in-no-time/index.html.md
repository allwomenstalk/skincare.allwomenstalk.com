# 7 Ingredients That Will Soften Your Skin in No Time ...

There are many ingredients that will soften your skin, but figuring out which ones they are can be hard. After all, most skin creams and lotions contain tons of ingredients. When you buy a skin cream, you obviously want one that is going to do its best work to moisturize the skin all over your body while also softening it at the same time. Look for the following ingredients that will soften your skin and you’ll be set in no time.

## 1. Hyaluronic Acid

I know the name makes it sound like this will be harsh on your skin, but hyaluronic acid is actually one of the best ingredients that will soften your skin. It has a powerful effect on your body’s cell renewal. It also keeps connective tissue strong and moisturizes your skin. With all that going on, hyaluronic acid is one ingredient you should definitely be looking for when you buy skin cream.

## 2. CoEnzyme Q-10

It sounds pretty scientific, but this is actually an ingredient that your body makes naturally. However, by using skin lotions and creams that contain it, you can really boost what’s available for softening the skin all over your body. This magical ingredient is an antioxidant, which means it fights free radical damage and can leave your skin looking smooth, young and beautiful no matter your age.

## 3. Retinol

It’s super easy to find skin care products that contain retinol. That includes lotion and cream, so you’re in luck. Retinol is actually a miracle ingredient that fights aging, reduces the appearance of lines and wrinkles, as well as combating age spots. However, for softening the skin, it’s also top notch. Use products with retinol to turn over old skin cells and reveal the smooth, healthy ones underneath.

## 4. Babassu

I hadn’t heard of this one often, but it turns out to be a great ingredient to be on the lookout for. Babassu is a wax that occurs naturally and when it’s mixed into lotions and creams, it offers a lot in the way of moisture, which can really smooth your skin. The great thing about babassu is that it won’t leave you looking like an oil slick. It’s also safe for use on eczema and other itchy skin conditions.

## 5. Ceramide

This little gem works by helping your skin retain moisture, which helps give it a smoother, younger appearance. Ceramide is common in facial skin products and can really do a lot to smooth fine lines and wrinkles while also plumping your skin and making you look great. Ceramide can also effectively treat and reduce the symptoms of eczema.

## 6. Acai Oil

You’ve probably heard that acai berries are a superfood due to their extremely high content of antioxidants. As a result, many skin lotion and cream manufacturers are incorporating acai oil into their products. When applied to the skin, the oil works to smooth wrinkles. There is still research being done on just how effective acai is for skin, but you can certainly find it in a plethora of beauty products while you wait.

## 7. Polyphenols

There are many types of polyphenols and most fall into the antioxidant category. They come from plants for the most part and can be highly beneficial for smoothing your skin. Polyphenols are linked with a reduction in the appearance of lines and wrinkles, which can smooth your skin and give you a youthful look. Green tea extract is one of the more popular polyphenols you’ll find in skincare products.

I’d recommend seeing a dermatologist before using a new cream or lotion, especially if you have skin issues. What do you look for when you buy skincare products? Do you think you’ll try any of these ingredients?
