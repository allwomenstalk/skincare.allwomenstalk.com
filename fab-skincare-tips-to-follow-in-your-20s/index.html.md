# 7 Fab Skincare Tips to Follow in Your 20s ...

I'm still in my 20's and finding the ideal skin care tips for 20-somethings is hard work! My skin is still super youthful and I don't want to overdo it on anything, but I still want to take care of my skin. So girls, what skin care tips for 20-somethings are out there? Want to check out my skin care tips for 20-somethings? I've got all of the best tips below! They work wonders and will make your skin look absolutely beautiful!

## 1. Protect Yourself from the Sun

First and foremost, you've got to protect yourself from the sun. That means that wearing anything that has UVA protection and also sunscreen is going to help! This skin care tip for 20-somethings is so important but is most often skipped. The reason why you want to protect yourself from the sun is because you don't want to age faster than you have to – UVA protection can keep your skin looking beautiful!

## 2. Gentle Cleansers

When it comes to cleansers, you don't want something that is going to be super rough and going to damage your skin, instead, you want something gentle. Foaming cleansers is actually my favorite kind, only because they are light and gentle. What cleanser do you ladies use? Does it fall in line with my skin care tips for 20-somethings?

## 3. Daytime Moisturizers

The moisturizer that you choose for the daytime is something that should be light, but also protect you and keep your skin hydrated. For me, I use a Lush all natural moisturizer that has nothing but natural things inside of it. It's light, airy and keeps my skin super soft. Aveeno is another brand that I absolutely love and they make some awesome moisturizers for young skin! This skin care tip for 20-somethings works ladies, don't overlook it!

## 4. Spot Treatments for Acne

When you are in your twenties, you probably are still dealing with acne right? It might not be the same as when you were a teenager, but you might be dealing with just a little acne. If so, make sure that you are using spot treatments! This skin care tip for 20-somethings is one that saved me! I didn't want to use a complete acne cleanser, but the spot treatments worked wonders!

## 5. Don't Pick at Acne

Another skin care tip for 20-somethings is don't pick at your acne! It can actually lead to acne scars and truth be told, it can make the acne worse. You've got to make sure that your acne is taken care of via the spot treatments, not by you picking at it.

## 6. Anti-Aging Serum

If you party hard in your twenties and still want to make sure that your skin is taken care of, make sure that you are using some really good anti-aging serum! For me, I use SkinCeuticals C E Ferulic Serum. It works wonders and make sure that it fights all of the free radical damage that can be caused by partying too much!

## 7. Nighttime Moisturizer

Finally, the last skin care tip for 20-somethings that we're going to talk about is keeping your skin protected at night. A nighttime moisturizer is a must girls! After you strip off your makeup, you're going to want something to protect your skin. Cetaphil moisturizing lotion is great for a nighttime moisturizer!

So girls, there you have it, all of my top skin care tips for 20-somethings that really work! So do you have young skin that you want to keep young? Follow these skin care tips for 20-somethings! What other skin care tips do you have? Share!
