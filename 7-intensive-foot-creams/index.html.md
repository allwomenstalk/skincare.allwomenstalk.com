# 7 Intensive Foot Creams ...

Our feet bear the brunt of a lot of daily activities so it’s important that we take the time to care for them. Foot creams are an ideal way to address surface issues such as cracked and dry skin. For an intense repairing treatment, look at creams that are rich in texture and packed with minerals and essential oils. Take a look at a few intensive foot creams below.

## 1. L’Occitane Shea Butter Foot Cream

![L’Occitane Shea Butter Foot Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/of/gdhk8ji954c8fa9d9a0c7283680644_250x245.jpg)

**Price:** $26.00 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This foot cream contains shea butter, lavender essential oil, and arnica extract. The shea butter and lavender softens the skin, while the arnica extract has anti-inflammatory properties. This is a rich cream that has both soothing and moisturising properties. Apply it daily after showering.

## 2. Burt’s Bees ‘Thoroughly Therapeutic’ Honey and Bilberry Foot Creme

![Burt’s Bees ‘Thoroughly Therapeutic’ Honey and Bilberry Foot Creme](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v6/sl/v7e2ge3z54c8fa9e5ac89218500148_250x246.jpg)

**Price:** $9.99 at [walgreens.com](https://goo.gl/gA2i9o)
This intensive foot cream combines vitamin-enriched bilberry extract with honey, jojoba oil, and vitamin E. The bilberry extract exfoliates while the remainder of the ingredients provide moisturising and therapeutic properties. This cream is 99% natural and does not contain parabens or phthalates. Apply daily or as needed.

## 3. AHAVA Dermud Enriched Intensive Foot Cream

![AHAVA Dermud Enriched Intensive Foot Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qk/nm/nv2et52654c8fa9f1892b660921971_163x238.jpg)

**Price:** $26.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Most people tend to turn to intensive foot creams during the winter months, as skin is more susceptible to drying out in such climates. This particular foot cream is formulated to be used all year round. It contains a blend of Dead Sea mud, minerals, and plant derivatives. Apply daily to alleviate symptoms such as scaling, itchiness, and redness associated with dry skin.

## 4. Clarins Foot Beauty Treatment Cream

**Price:** $25.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This cream is a rich, non-greasy treatment designed to restore and revitalise your feet. It is formulated with shea butter, arnica, myrrh, thyme essential oil, and cashew-nut oil. This combination of ingredients acts to moisturise, nourish, and purify the skin. The cream absorbs effortlessly onto the skin and has a pleasant, subtle scent.

## 5. Bliss Foot Patrol

![Bliss Foot Patrol](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z4/sr/moz97s8j54c8faa15b118042952607_250x240.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Most intensive foot creams are formulated with several reparative properties in mind. This foot cream both exfoliates and moisturises. It contains aloe vera, glycolic acid, salycilic acid, and peppermint oil. The addition of peppermint oil is designed to leave feet feeling refreshed.

## 6. Cake ‘Cake Walk’ Foote Crème

**Price:** $16.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This crème contains pure menthol, five essential oils, and mango butter. The mango butter moisturises and nourishes, while spearmint and peppermint extracts help reduce swelling and tension in the muscles. This cream also doubles as massage lotion for the neck and shoulders.

## 7. Caudalie Foot Beauty Cream

**Price:** $15.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This moisturising foot cream has an oil free finish. It is formulated to nourish and repair dry feet. The texture is rich and creamy, and easy to apply. It contains grape seed oil, shea butter, and vinolevure. As with most foot creams, apply daily or as needed to make the most out of this formula.
