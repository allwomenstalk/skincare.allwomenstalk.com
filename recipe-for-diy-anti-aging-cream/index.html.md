# Perfect Recipe for DIY anti\-Aging Cream ...

Do you want a **recipe for DIY anti-aging cream**? We all want flawless, shiny, glowing skin and have tried expensive salon procedures and DIY masks. There are tons of anti-aging creams and serums on the market. But luckily, women like you have tried loads of these anti-aging skin care products and have separated which is the best and which is the worse. Here is famous Korean **recipe for DIY anti-aging cream**.

You will need:

White Rice 2 tbsp
Water 2-3 glasses
Vitamin E ½ tsp
Glycerin 1 tsp
Aloe vera gel ¼ tsp

The main component for this cream is white rice. Take 2 tbsp of white rice and wash it properly. Add 2-3 cups of water and washed rice into a pan. Put it on heat to cook till it becomes thick and mashy. Then strain it. After straining you will get a very creamy and slightly thick paste.

Add ½ tsp vitamin E oil and 1 tsp glycerin and ¼ aloe vera gel. Mix all ingredients well.

Your homemade anti-aging skin cream is ready. Transfer it to a container and you can keep it in the refrigerator up to 15 days.

You can use it as a day cream, night cream or before makeup or sunscreen. Apply twice a day for best results.

Rice is rich in vitamin B, which is a powerful antioxidant that boosts collagen production and removes wrinkles and fine lines. Rice has been used as a natural beauty treatment for thousands of years in Japan and Korea. Rice also helps to hydrate and moisturize your skin.
