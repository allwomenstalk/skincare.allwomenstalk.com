# 8 Great Products to Prep Your Skin for Bikini Season ...

Is your bathroom stocked with products for bikini season? I know, it might seem a little early to be talking bikinis but before you know it, you’ll be getting ready to hit the beach! Getting your skin ready for swimsuit season can seem like a huge chore but luckily there are some amazing skin care products for bikini season that’ll help make the task so much easier!

## 1. St. Tropez Self Tan Bronzing Lotion

![St. Tropez Self Tan Bronzing Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/1_st-tropez-self-tan-bronzing-lotion_250x250.jpg)

Self-tanners have to be on the top of this list of products for bikini season! Bronzing our skin not only makes our skin glow but it can also make us appear leaner and more toned! St. Tropez makes a fabulous line of self-tanning products and this lotion has tons of rave reviews. If you’re not into lotions, they also have mousse and spray tans if that’s more of your thing!

## 2. Supergoop! Everyday Face & Body Lotion with Parsol 1789 plus Broad Spectrum SPF 30 (Endless Summer Pump)

![Supergoop! Everyday Face & Body Lotion with Parsol 1789 plus Broad Spectrum SPF 30 (Endless Summer Pump)](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/2_supergoop-everyday-face-body-lotion-with-parsol-1789-plus-broad-spectrum-spf-30-endless-summer-pump_250x250.jpg)

One of the most important skin care products you can have, regardless of the season, is a good sunscreen. Supergoop is an incredible sunscreen that moisturizes your face and body and provides a broad spectrum protection for 80 minutes. The formula is lightweight, water-resistant and paraben-free. I love Supergoop because it’s not thick and pasty like most sunscreens!

## 3. Sally Hansen Simple Smooth Hair Remover Creme for All Skin Types

![Sally Hansen Simple Smooth Hair Remover Creme for All Skin Types](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/3_sally-hansen-simple-smooth-hair-remover-creme-for-all-skin-types_105x224.jpg)

If you’re not fond of shaving, a hair removal is another great option! This hair remover is water-resistant and works in the shower so it can be just as easy as shaving. Another great feature of this product is that it’s odorless, so you don’t have to worry about that foul depilatory smell, and it works in three short minutes! Veet and Nair also offer some great hair remover options!

## 4. Nip + Fab Dry Leg Fix

![Nip + Fab Dry Leg Fix](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/4_nip-fab-dry-leg-fix_300x300.jpg)

Don’t even think about stepping out of your house with dry, scaly legs! We can hide dry skin in the winter but there’s no hiding it once the weather gets warmer. In the spring and summertime your legs usually take center stage, so treat them to an intense moisturizer that promises to instantly smooth and fix dry legs. Use this moisturizer daily to keep parched skin soft, smooth and hydrated!

## 5. Neutrogena Body Clear Body Scrub

![Neutrogena Body Clear Body Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/5_neutrogena-body-clear-body-scrub_410x410.jpg)

Body scrubs are another important skin care product we can’t forget. Bathing suits obviously show a lot of skin, so let’s make sure our skin is on point and give our skin a good scrubbing! Neutrogena’s Body Clear Body Scrub contains salicylic acid, so if you’re concerned about any body acne, this scrub will help keep skin clear and smooth.

## 6. Burt's Bees Sugar Scrub in Cranberry & Pomegranate

![Burt's Bees Sugar Scrub in Cranberry & Pomegranate](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/6_burts-bees-sugar-scrub-in-cranberry-pomegranate_401x354.jpg)

So what if you don’t need a medicated body scrub and just want a regular, straight-up scrub? No prob, try this fragrant, skin smoothing body exfoliator from Burt’s Bees. This scrub contains hydrating Shea Butter, Pomegranate Oil and sugar crystals to polish and reveal bright, fresh skin that’s just begging to be shown off!

## 7. True Blue Spa Smoothing Foot Scrub

![True Blue Spa Smoothing Foot Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/7_true-blue-spa-smoothing-foot-scrub_280x360.jpg)

Have you neglected your feet all winter long and it’s really starting to show? Treat your feet to this smoothing foot scrub that’s made to target the thicker skin on your feet and leave them feeling fresh, soft and pampered. Follow up this scrub with a thick cream to keep your feet ready for sandals and flip flops!

## 8. Tend Skin

![Tend Skin](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/skincare/2013/03/8_tend-skin_250x250.jpg)

If you’re new to this skin care product, allow me to introduce you to your new skin BFF! Tend Skin is a must-have for women who shave, wax or use electrolysis and get unsightly bumps on their skin as a result. All you have to do is apply this liquid to the affected area and you’ll see an improvement in 1-2 days! This product is great for men and women so feel free to share with the man in your life.

It’s not too early to start thinking about prepping your skin for bikini season! There are lots of products out there that can help us achieve the toned, bronzed look that we all love, so why not take advantage of them? What are your go-to products to get your skin ready for bikini season?
