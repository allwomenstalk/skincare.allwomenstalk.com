# 7 Best Skin Care Products for Men ...

It’s sort of a hush-hush secret: men need skin care, too. They might try to pretend they don’t, and they may tease us about the amount of time we spend on skin care, but inside, they’re dying to try our moisturizers and exfoliants. But rather than quietly loaning them your own skin care products, why not buy them their own, specially designed for men’s needs? Here are the 7 best skin care products for men.

## 1. Burt’s Bees Natural Skin Care for Men Body Wash

![Burt’s Bees Natural Skin Care for Men Body Wash](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zi/fy/zcal6jub54c8f8cddb816028269637_216x250.jpg)

**Price:** $8.00 at [burtsbees.com](http://www.burtsbees.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is far and away my boyfriend’s favorite men’s skin care product. It cleanses his skin, leaving it soft and refreshed, but it doesn’t have a girlie scent. It contains coconut, sunflower and citrus oil for cleansing and softness, as well as woodsy-smelling cypress and fir oils to invigorate and scent.

## 2. Clinique Post-Shave Healer

![Clinique Post-Shave Healer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yi/g2/j096ptvk54c8f8ce8ac47925247951_250x215.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Irritated skin from shaving can become a thing of the past with this post-shave lotion. It soothes irritated skin and even helps heal the minor nicks and cuts from shaving. Your guy will love this! It even smells good, too.

## 3. Jack Black Intense Therapy Lip Balm SPF 25

![Jack Black Intense Therapy Lip Balm SPF 25](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/q9/e0/frhcml3554c8f8cf421b4741002552_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $7.50 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Once, and only once, did my boyfriend accidentally swipe my Burt’s Bees Shimmer. He thought it was regular lip balm and got some lush pink glitter by mistake. No more! Now he uses this girlie-free lip balm, which conditions as well as mine, but will never be confused for my yellow tube, again.

## 4. Aveda Outer Peace Foaming Cleanser

![Aveda Outer Peace Foaming Cleanser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dm/fu/bodz68sp54c8f8cfeae48151163438_156x248.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [aveda.com](http://www.aveda.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
We all strive for inner peace and calm, so why not add a little outer peace to our lives, too? This face wash is ideal for anyone (including men) who suffers from acne and overall skin irritation. It calms the skin, to clear away current break-outs and prevent new ones, with much cleaner, clearer skin, often in as little as 4 weeks.

## 5. The Body Shop for Men Maca Root Eye Rescue Serum

![The Body Shop for Men Maca Root Eye Rescue Serum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/id/uo/vejrdclk54c8f8d0a0767934640478_183x242.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [thebodyshop-usa.com](https://www.thebodyshop.com/en-us/)
I only had to catch my boyfriend using my eye cream once before I bought him this one for his own. It’s formulated for men, with loads of antioxidants and a much more masculine scent. It does the same thing as mine - reduces puffiness and fine lines - but it’s not as expensive… and he loves it!

## 6. Crabtree & Evelyn Nomad Calming Shaving Cream

![Crabtree & Evelyn Nomad Calming Shaving Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/fu/co8dyw3d54c8f8d16d064973718440_208x245.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [crabtree-evelyn.com](http://www.crabtree-evelyn.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Ask any guy what his number one skin complaint is, and he’ll undoubtedly say something related to shaving. That’s why this shave cream is such a good idea - it prevents the dry skin and irritation that traditional shave foams can cause (it’s the air bubbles in the foam, actually). This cream is made with soothing Vitamin E to prevent razor burn, and it smells divine… *ahem* I mean, manly.

## 7. Lab Series for Men Daily Moisture Defense Lotion SPF 15

![Lab Series for Men Daily Moisture Defense Lotion SPF 15](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/d4/dwq5zjr554c8f8d244e64387867996_250x233.jpg)

**Price:** $37.50 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Everyone, men included, wants younger-looking skin, and one way to achieve that is by protecting it from the sun’s damaging rays. This moisturizer smoothes away fine lines, and also prevents new ones by providing SPF 15 built-in. It’s good for all skin types.
