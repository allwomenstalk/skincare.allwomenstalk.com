# 7 Tips for Using Konjac on Your Face ...

Before I share tips for using konjac, I better tell you what it is. Konjac is a vegetable fiber and is a popular facial exfoliator in Asian cultures. The item is full of vitamins and minerals and some areas of the world have used konjac for more than 1,000 years. I guess that means it probably works, right? Even if you’ve never heard of it, it might be worth a try. After all, we’re all just on the lookout for healthy, young looking skin, aren’t we? Check out these tips for using konjac and let me know how it works out for you.

## 1. Go Easy on the Water

According to experts on konjac sponges, you don’t need a lot water to make the product work for you. In fact, you likely don’t even need any cleanser either, unless you have a lot of make-up products on your face. So one of the best tips for using konjac is to just add a bit of the wet stuff and go ahead and wash your face with the sponge.

## 2. Use a Circular Motion

Many users of konjac say that it’s similar to using a washcloth. For that reason, they recommend using a gentle circular motion when you use it to cleanse and exfoliate your face. Start at your forehead and work your way down to your jaw and chin. That way you know you covered your entire face and haven’t left out anything. It’s as easy as that!

## 3. Don’t Worry about Skin Type

In general, it’s a good idea to consider your skin type when choosing your skincare products. That’s because many are designed for a specific issue, such as dryness or oily skin. However, experts on konjac say that it’s ideal for any skin type and won’t dry your skin out at all. That’s good news for all of us!

## 4. Avoid Acne

If you have any breakouts on your face, it’s a good idea to avoid scrubbing them with your konjac. It won’t make your acne itself worse, but it can irritate the issue or make it worse. Gently work your way around the pimples. Experts also say you should avoid your eye area with the konjac.

## 5. Combine with Other Skincare Products

Despite the mega benefits of konjac, one of the best tips for using it is to mix and match it with your other skincare products. If you have specific skin issues, don’t stop using any of the products your dermatologist has recommended. Also, make sure you cleanse your face regularly and moisturize after washing. That way you get the most of out each of your skincare products, including konjac.

## 6. Rinse after Use

It’s gross, but your face isn’t all that clean by the end of the day. You’ve been exposed to stuff in the air, you probably have make-up on and you’ve likely touched something covered with germs and then touched your face. All of the grime, bacteria and germs must come off, but you don’t want them festering on your konjac sponge. That’s why it’s important to always rinse your sponge and hang it to air dry after each use.

## 7. Replace It Every Few Months

Just like with a loofah or other body sponge, you’ll need a new one every three months or so. When your konjac starts looking worn out, toss it and get a new one. The good news is that because it’s a vegetable fiber, it’s biodegradable.
