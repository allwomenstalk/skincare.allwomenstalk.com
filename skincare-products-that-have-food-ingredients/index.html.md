# 7 Skincare Products That Have Food Ingredients ...

It’s no secret that your kitchen is a great place to look for babying your skin, but I’m going to share with you some great skincare products that have food ingredients. They’re often approved by dermatologists for all your skincare needs and they certainly aren’t as messy as trying to whip something up at home. Look for these great items at drugstores and see how awesome they really are. These are my favorite skincare products that have food ingredients, but please share yours with me so I can try something new too.

## 1. Derma E Evenly Radiant Dark Circle Eye Crème

![Derma E Evenly Radiant Dark Circle Eye Crème](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ur/to/f4nlthow_450x450.jpg)

You’ve probably heard that green tea is good for your health, but did you know that’s it’s also great for your skin? That’s what makes this one of the best skincare products that have food ingredients. The addition of green tea to this skin crème helps protect your skin from sun related damage. Combined with the other great ingredients in this product, you have anti-aging and moisturizing benefits that can’t be beat.

## 2. Olivella All Natural Virgin Olive Oil Moisturizer

![Olivella All Natural Virgin Olive Oil Moisturizer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nq/jt/jpxnja3n_372x372.jpg)

Olive oil often pops up on lists of ingredients to use in homemade facial masks. To save yourself some trouble, pick up this great moisturizer. It’s loaded with 100% virgin olive oil straight from the Mediterranean. That means you can count on it for anti-aging and smoothing benefits. The antioxidants that olive oil contains are also good for fighting free radical damage and warding off skin cancer.

## 3. Andalou Naturals Skin Care Essentials Kit

![Andalou Naturals Skin Care Essentials Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wn/sw/xk29szja_450x448.jpg)

This great kit features several products chock full of a range of food products that are great for your skin. That includes fruit cells, apricot milk and fruit juice. The makers of this set promise that using the kit will give you softer, smoother, younger and radiant looking skin in no time. Plus the packaging is so pretty.

## 4. Now Foods, Solutions, Tea Tree and Cranberry Bath and Shower Gel

![Now Foods, Solutions, Tea Tree and Cranberry Bath and Shower Gel](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/f4/h6/k833tdzn_224x224.jpg)

I love the smell of cranberries, but they also offer a lot in the way of skincare. This shower gel will leave you fresh and yummy smelling, but it also promises to improve and restore the texture and look of the skin all over your body.

## 5. Shea Moisture Three Butters Body

![Shea Moisture Three Butters Body](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p8/tq/bphmj8j1_137x365.jpg)

You probably don’t eat a lot of butter because you’ve heard that it’s bad for your health. However, the body butters used in this product are nothing but good for you. This wonder product contains mango and avocado butters, which load your skin up with lots of antioxidants that help prevent skin cancer and other health conditions. Not only that, but it smells good and feels wonderful as you apply it.

## 6. Earth Science Fragrance Free Shampoo

![Earth Science Fragrance Free Shampoo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hc/t2/pbyzxy19_450x450.jpg)

Wheat germ and chamomile combine in this shampoo to give you a product that takes great care of the skin on your scalp. You’ll get manageable hair and a nourished scalp that helps you show off the healthiest and most beautiful head of hair imaginable.

## 7. Organic Fiji Coconut Oil Soap

![Organic Fiji Coconut Oil Soap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yk/pt/0422sugo_450x321.jpg)

You can use this great soap on your face and body, which means less time spent on washing without sacrificing everything you crave in a skincare product. Coconut oil is getting a ton of hype these days and for good reason. You can cook with it, but it has some super great skincare benefits too. This soap promises to clean and treat your skin while also smelling super great!
