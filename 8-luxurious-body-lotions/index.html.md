# 8 Luxurious Body Lotions ...

Every now and again, it’s nice to treat your skin. A bit of TLC will help keep it in tip top condition. A good body lotion is a great way to nourish your skin. I like ones with a silky smooth formula and gorgeous scent. Treat your skin with these following luxurious body lotions.

## 1. Philosophy Apricots and Cream Body Lotion

![Philosophy Apricots and Cream Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x6/c1/chutkc3r54c8f5d28d6eb665323609_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $24.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is one luxe lotion. The formula is rich and creamy, and is intended to help aid moisture retention. It’s made with natural olive and macadamia nut oils, shea butter, and antioxidants such as vitamins C and E. The scent is quite strong on application but fades to a subtle, delicate fragrance on the skin.

## 2. L’Occitane Vineyard Rose Ultra Rich Body Cream

![L’Occitane Vineyard Rose Ultra Rich Body Cream](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eh/g7/wd5ext8x54c8f5d3595b9356969073_250x177.jpg)

**Price:** $21.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This rich body cream works a treat on dry skin. It’s enriched with vitamin E and rose water, and will have skin feeling moisturised and nourished in no time at all. The rose scent is subtle and romantic. This cream would be great for treating dry winter skin.

## 3. Kiehl’s Gardenia Hand and Body Lotion

![Kiehl’s Gardenia Hand and Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dn/zs/nydxidv054c8f5d410a01591020241_163x239.jpg)

**Price:** $20.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Give dry skin a boost with this Kiehl’s lotion. The formulation is lightweight and glides effortlessly onto the skin. I love a good gardenia scent, and this is no exception. It’s fresh and delicate. This body lotion is part of a limited edition, so you better get in quick!

## 4. First Aid Beauty Ultra Repair Cream to Go

![First Aid Beauty Ultra Repair Cream to Go](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/av/sd/a6tgfjrt54c8f5d4b5694305673230_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $15.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The name of this body cream says it all, really. If your skin in need of some first aid hydration, then check this cream out. It’s formulated with ingredients such as colloidal oatmeal, shea butter, and eucalyptus oil. It’s also helpful in providing relief to minor skin irritations. This product is allergy tested and dermatologist-recommended.

## 5. Pangea Organics French Chamomile with Sweet Orange and Lavender Lotion

![Pangea Organics French Chamomile with Sweet Orange and Lavender Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yg/z5/fv3nch0954c8f5d56b7b7254189652_250x248.jpg)

**Price:** $18.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This organic lotion is a great option for the environmentally and ethically minded. It features all organic ingredients as well as Certified Fair Trade cocoa butter. This high performance moisturiser is designed to help skin stay hydrated. The blend of fragrances is also quite wonderful.

## 6. Clinique Deep Comfort Body Butter

![Clinique Deep Comfort Body Butter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ao/zr/i8g1rug454c8f5d634675469827836_255x248.jpg)

**Price:** $25.00 at [shop.nordstrom.com](http://shop.nordstrom.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This body butter is designed for intensive treatment. The rich formula will help moisturise dry spots and soften hardened skin. It’s deeply hydrating and is easily absorbed by the skin. The scent is almost unnoticeable, which is great for those wanting to layer other scents on top.

## 7. Carol’s Daughter Ecstasy Shea Souffle

![Carol’s Daughter Ecstasy Shea Souffle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pm/l1/q2jzs05g54c8f5d6f3010326817022_250x232.jpg)

**Price:** $13.00 at [sephora.com](http://sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
The soufflé texture of this lotion makes it light the touch. It glides effortlessly onto the skin. It’s made from shea butter, cocoa butter, sweet almond oil, and jojoba oil. It’s paraben-free and is formulated without synthetic dyes or petro-chemicals. The sweet scent includes notes of pineapple, peach, mango, and vanilla.

## 8. Jo Malone Orange Blossom Body Lotion

![Jo Malone Orange Blossom Body Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/tg/n3/lzl7n3py54c8f5d7b6bba037245716_163x237.jpg)
