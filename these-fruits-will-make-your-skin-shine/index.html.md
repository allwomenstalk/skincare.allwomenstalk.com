# 10 Fruits to Eat if You Want Glowing and Gorgeous Skin ...

Who among us hasn’t looked in the mirror and wished for better skin. I did it just this morning, in fact. The truth is that what you eat can have a major impact on how you look on the outside, including the state of your skin. By eating plenty of fruits, you can get that healthy glow you want while also boosting your health in other ways. So, next time you make your shopping list, be sure you include the following fruits.

## 1. Strawberries Are a Delicious Choice Anytime of the Day

![Strawberries Are a Delicious Choice Anytime of the Day](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ea/s2/gnvcsycm573281c287894170772151_500x500.jpg)

What makes a strawberry so great for your skin? For starters, this berry is loaded with vitamin C, which is a nutrient that plays a big role in keeping your skin firm and blemish free. Berries are also a great source of antioxidants, which work to repair damage to your skin and combat the free radicals that can lead to aging and skin cancer. Eat strawberries on their own or add them to a bowl of oatmeal or a smoothie.

## 2. Have a Wedge of Cantaloupe

![Have a Wedge of Cantaloupe](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z5/dt/zpfng1df573282abdbab4493341407_500x500.jpg)

You know that cantaloupe is a healthy choice because it’s orange in color. That indicates the presence of beta-carotene, which is converted to vitamin A in your body. This is a nutrient that helps keep your skin looking young and glorious. In addition, this yummy melon is also rich in vitamin C and antioxidants. Have a wedge with your lunchtime sandwich.

## 3. Apricots Might Be Small, but They Pack a Big Punch

![Apricots Might Be Small, but They Pack a Big Punch](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bq/dh/gpyxtogb5732837949c0f392499265_466x466.jpg)

When you can find fresh apricots on store shelves, stock up in a big way. Like cantaloupe, apricots are chock full of vitamin A with a side of vitamin C. This powerful combination of nutrients helps you protect and nourish your skin on a daily basis. As an added bonus, apricots also contain a small amount of vitamin E, which is great for smooth, healthy skin as well. An apricot is great eaten plain and makes for one delicious snack.

## 4. Cut Open a Fresh Kiwi for Breakfast

![Cut Open a Fresh Kiwi for Breakfast](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pz/dr/opmupvuh57328415edc87147334338_500x502.jpg)

Kiwis might be small, but one of them contains your entire quota of vitamin C for the day. That’s great news for your skin because as you know, vitamin C is needed to keep it healthy and young. Some research also indicates that eating kiwis can help fight wrinkles and acne. If you have a hard time with the tartness of a kiwi, try adding it to a smoothie to help balance out the tang.

## 5. Nibble on a Peach when You Get Hungry

![Nibble on a Peach when You Get Hungry](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m3/rf/rr9y3nbg57328605780d3210009504_500x500.jpg)

Peaches are full of water, which is vital for healthy, smooth and fresh skin. Skimping on your fluid intake leaves your skin parched and dry, which is unhealthy and uncomfortable. The good news is that you don’t have to rely on water alone to get the hydration you need. Peaches are a great choice, and they also contain vitamins A and C.

## 6. Today is a Great Time to Try Papaya

![Today is a Great Time to Try Papaya](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/po/bi/hjvt8woc5732872f07324370389872_500x493.jpg)

If you’ve never tried a papaya before, today is a good time to toss one in your grocery cart. Because they are orange, you can count on them to help you fulfill your vitamin A needs, which translates to great skin all over your body. The antioxidants they contain only add to the benefits. Papaya is wonderful in a smoothie or in a fruit salad. Yum!

## 7. An Apple a Day…

![An Apple a Day…](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pd/u5/s67rxws857328b1da708d623599642_600x600.jpg)

An apple a day may keep the doctor away, but it can also keep acne away. Because of the high pectin content in apples, many experts agree that eating them boosts digestion, which can help slough away toxins that lead to acne on your skin. While that is still a debatable statement, apples do contain a wealth of antioxidants, which help battle all kinds of skin issues. There’s nothing like a crisp, crunchy apple at lunchtime, right?

## 8. Avocados, Avocados, Avocados

![food, produce, green, plant, fruit,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cl/l0/x9a8nqxj58e4275cde439695608325_600x421.jpg)

We don't think you'll need much convincing when we tell you to eat avocados. Let's face it, we all are obsessed with the fruit, but what's great is that they are super beneficial for your skin. They contain biotin, which helps cell regeneration and growth, and they have fatty acids that promote that glowy skin we all crave. Extra guac, please??

## 9. B-a-N-a-N-a-S!

![banana family, food, banana, produce, plant,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uy/w2/bhcdpv2a58e4277942922666350761_580x386.jpg)

Bananas are great to eat in so many ways. You can have them in a smoothie or dipped in peanut butter, or go classic and eat them straight from the peel. Whatever you choose, know you're getting great benefits, as the potassium found in them moisturizes and hydrates your skin! They also help to fade dark spots and blemishes. Score.

## 10. When Life Hands You Lemons...

![citrus, food, fruit, produce, plant,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qv/al/nn7qvct158e4279ad7946241388655_520x347.jpg)

Lemons are a great fruit to add as a part of your beauty routine. They naturally even out your skin tone and help reduce acne marks and spots. They're also great at deep cleaning, as they break down the crud that clogs your pores. A popular way to get your daily dose of lemon is to have a glass of honey and lemon water in the morning.

Which of these fruits is your favorite?
