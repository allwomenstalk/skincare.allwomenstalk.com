# How to Get Skin like These 11 Victoria's Secret Models ...

The Victoria's Secret Fashion Show proved how beautiful those models are. If you'd like to have skin like they do, then you should follow the tips that [RedBook Mag](https://goo.gl/2tiH47) learned from the models themselves.

## 1. Adriana Lima

![hair, human hair color, face, eyebrow, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ls/nk/njm0y4wq5875cefe92f69564210182_600x600.jpg)

"I'll go to the health food store and buy argan, coconut and almond oils to use on my face, body, and hair. They're the best hydrators."

## 2. Devon Windsor

![hair, clothing, lady, hairstyle, long hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/qm/ba20hrp95875cf8bd2147838280236_600x600.jpg)

"I wash morning and night with a gentle cleanser, then follow up with moisturizer – my favorite is Chanel Hydra Beauty Creme. I'll use a facial cleansing brush a couple of times a week to keep my skin clear and soft, but the most important step is drinking lots of water!"

## 3. Taylor Hill

![hair, person, photography, beauty, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/of/k5/uru0sjd15875ce1111694513256179_450x450.jpg)

She uses coconut oil in her skin and hair, because "It's the best moisturizer."

## 4. Behati Prinsloo

![hair, face, eyebrow, lip, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eb/kd/jn94o5mj5875cfe3cec46564238805_595x595.jpg)

"My #1 tip is to wear sunblock! I also love anything by Ling Skincare – I used one of their masks right before this."​

## 5. Rachel Hilbert

![face, hair, eyebrow, person, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mi/pf/w2j7we8g5875d08cd597a276042612_600x601.jpg)

Her advice is to drink tons of water and never go without washing your face and moisturizing. La Mer creams are her favorite.

## 6. Gigi Hadid

This gorgeous girl swears by St. Ives Apricot Scrub found at most retailers and pharmacies.

## 7. Leomie Anderson

She reveals her skincare secret is washing her face daily with an all natural shea butter body wash, drinking lots of water, and her Clarisonic facial brush.

## 8. Joan Smalls

Water with lemon is this supermodel's go-to trick. And she makes sure to use a serum. Her fave? Estée Lauder Advanced Night Repair used multiple times a day.

## 9. Kendall Jenner

This chick is all about simplicity-"washing your face before bed, eating right, and staying hydrated" are her fab skin tricks.

## 10. Josephine Skriver

Who knew models get acne?! This gorgeous gal loves her some Proactiv 3 step solution combined with hydration, lemon juice in her water, and giving her face a rest on off days.

## 11. Ming Xi

This swanky supermodel mixes up her products. She likes "high-level Clé du Peau products-the black line-in the winter" and Eve Lom in the summer when her face isn't too dry.

Who's your favorite Victoria's Secret Model?
