# DUA by Dua Lipa x Augustinus Bader Is The Hottest Skincare Line of Fall 2025 …

Every once in a while, a celebrity skincare line drops that makes the entire beauty industry pause mid-serum. And when I heard that Dua Lipa — global pop phenomenon, Grammy winner, and woman whose cheekbones might actually refract light — was teaming up with Augustinus Bader, I did just that. I put down my jade roller, sipped my matcha, and whispered, “Okay, this could actually be good.”

Meet [DUA, a high-performance skincare line](http://duabyab.com/) created by Dua Lipa in collaboration with Professor Augustinus Bader — the biomedical scientist behind some of the most efficacious creams of our time. Available exclusively at DUABYAB.COM, the three-piece launch feels less like another celebrity vanity project and more like a carefully choreographed skincare symphony. And yes, it performs as beautifully as Dua’s stage sets.

This is science meets spotlight. It’s TFC5™ meets “Training Season.” It’s the radiance of a sold-out tour — without the tour exhaustion.

**The Collaboration That Makes Too Much Sense**

Let’s start with the obvious: Dua Lipa didn’t just slap her name on a jar of moisturizer. She’s been a long-time devotee of Augustinus Bader, whose research-driven formulas have graced every A-list vanity from Berlin to Beverly Hills. Bader’s proprietary TFC technology (short for Trigger Factor Complex) is the stuff of legend — the result of over 35 years of stem cell and tissue repair science that quite literally helps skin help itself.

For DUA, Professor Bader and his team introduced TFC5™, the fifth evolution of that groundbreaking complex — optimized for everyday use, for skin with minimal to moderate damage. Think of it as Bader’s genius turned down from a symphony to a sonata — still powerful, but with a softer touch.

**Dua explains it best:**

_“I created this skincare line to be my everyday essential – a skincare routine that’s clean, effective, and effortless. It was incredibly important to me to work with a team who values clean formulas and high-quality ingredients, and I knew I could trust Augustinus Bader to bring this to life.”_

The result? A trio of daily essentials — Balancing Cream Cleanser ($40), Supercharged Glow Complex ($80), and Renewal Cream ($75) — that deliver visible results without the complexity. It’s skin science simplified, and impossibly chic while doing it!

**The Science Behind the Shine**

To appreciate DUA is to understand the quiet brilliance of TFC5™. Developed from Augustinus Bader’s pioneering research into cellular repair, this advanced complex uses biomimetic peptides, moisture-enhancing proteins, and antioxidants to guide skin renewal in harmony with its natural rhythms.

Unlike traditional actives that force change, TFC5™ supports your skin’s own regenerative processes. It’s the difference between barking orders and gently leading by example. The result? Balanced, resilient, genuinely healthy skin — not just temporarily polished.

TFC5™ also carries a slightly gentler signaling intensity than its older sibling, TFC8®, making it ideal for daily use across all skin types — whether your complexion leans dry, oily, or delightfully unpredictable.
