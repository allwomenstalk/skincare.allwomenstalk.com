# Best Essential Oils to Treat Acne ...

Wondering about the **best essential oils for acne**? If you are somebody who likes to be knowledgeable about all of the different options in the world of health and beauty, then we are sure that you will have come across a large number of essential oils in your time. Not only can they be used in a therapeutic way, but they can also have a great positive impact on a number of different physical complaints of the body, and one such complaint that can benefit greatly from essential oil use is acne. Acne is definitely something that affects teenagers more than it affects adults, but it’s certainly a skin issue that can be experienced by people of all ages. Here are the **best essential oils for acne**.

## 1. Tea Tree

Tea tree oil is one of the **best essential oils for acne**. You will definitely have seen tea tree oils being used and promoted in skincare products before. The oil has strong antifungal and antimicrobial properties, which makes it a really effective and efficient fighter against the spreading nature of acne. Tea tree oil is particularly good at reducing the number of lesions on your face, helping to positively change the everyday appearance of the ailment.

## 2. Lavender

You might associate lavender more with perfume, but it can be really good for treating acne symptoms too. It boasts both antioxidant and antimicrobial properties, which work to nourish and soothe the affected skin, healing dry patches and reducing the appearance of pesky dark spots and light scarring. Lavender has the added benefit of being able to ease symptoms of stress, something that can be common amongst bad acne sufferers.

## 3. Clary Sage

The important element of clary sage in terms of treating acne is something called linalyl acetate. This is something that helps to reduce inflammation of the skin, which will do great things for the red look of your acne. It can also regulate the production of oil in your skin, something that is mostly associated with the occurrence of horrible breakouts. Its third and final beneficial quality is the fact that it can curb the spread and growth of bacteria, which is how acne thrives on your face and neck.

## 4. Juniper Berry

Juniper berry oil is one of the most used essential oils, thanks to its antimicrobial and antibacterial properties. When used in the right ways, it can be a powerful fighter against skin irritations and infections, which makes it the perfect home remedy for acne! It also has stress reducing and detoxifying properties, which can help to reduce the presence of certain toxins in your body that, if left within, can lead to outbreaks of acne further down the line.
